<h1>Clatter Carbine — the chapter 6 ARK precursor rifle</h1>
<p>Clatter Carbine is RE9's mid-tier rifle, found in chapter 6 RPD archive. Reliable but unexceptional. Most players use it as bridge weapon until Requiem Sniper Rifle (chapter 7) unlocks.</p>

<blockquote>Clatter Carbine is the franchise's most workmanlike rifle. Not exciting. Solid utility for the chapters it covers. Replaceable when better tools arrive.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Base damage: 95. Headshot multiplier: 2.6x. Magazine: 20 rounds. Reload speed: 2.8s. Fire mode: semi-auto.</p>

<h2>Best use cases</h2>
<p>Chapter 6 RPD medium-range engagements. Chapter 7 ARK initial corridors (before Requiem Sniper unlock).</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>Insanity Clatter Carbine becomes underpowered by chapter 7. Replace with Requiem Sniper Rifle as soon as available.</p>

<h2>Korean community tier</h2>
<p>Korean community ranks Clatter Carbine as B-tier. Functional but not memorable.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Use Clatter Carbine for the chapter span where it's the available rifle. Don't grow attached. Replace at first opportunity.</p>
