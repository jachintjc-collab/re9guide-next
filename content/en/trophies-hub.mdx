<!-- MISSABLE TROPHIES -->
<div>
 <div>⚠ Missable trophies — pay attention</div>
 <div>
 <div>
 <div>Eye Spy</div>
 <div>Defeat <strong>Chunk #1</strong> as Grace before her chapter ends. Drops Eye Spy Charm. Skip Chunk = lose forever.</div>
 </div>
 <div>
 <div>Order Up!</div>
 <div>Defeat <strong>The Chef</strong> during Grace's chapter. Hemolytic Injector instant-kills him.</div>
 </div>
 <div>
 <div>You Little Rascal!</div>
 <div>All 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam. <strong>Several are area-locked</strong>; see All 25 Mr. Raccoon Locations.</div>
 </div>
 <div>
 <div>Case Closed!</div>
 <div>All 75 Files. <strong>Many are story-locked</strong>, manual save before transitions.</div>
 </div>
 <div>
 <div>No Safe Is Safe</div>
 <div>All 5 safes. Only <strong>Grace</strong> can open them. Cannot be done as Leon.</div>
 </div>
 <div>
 <div>Coin Collector</div>
 <div>Spend any Antique Coin at the Parlor. <strong>Only 1 coin needed</strong> — easy to overlook.</div>

<!-- FAQ -->
<div>
 <div>Trophy FAQ</div>
 <div>

 <div>
 <div>How long does it take to Platinum Resident Evil Requiem?</div>
 <div>Total estimated time is <strong>30-40 hours</strong> across two playthroughs. First run on Standard for collectibles (12-15 hours), then Insanity speedrun with Infinite Ammo (3:40-4 hours), plus NG+ Final Puzzle and cleanup.</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Does Infinite Ammo void trophies in Resident Evil Requiem?</div>
 <div><strong>No.</strong> Infinite Requiem and Infinite RPG-7 do NOT void any trophies, including the Insanity Remarkable Agent and Speedrunner trophies. This is confirmed for both PS5 and Steam versions.</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>What is the hardest trophy in Resident Evil Requiem?</div>
 <div><strong>Remarkable Agent</strong> (complete on Insanity) is the hardest skill-based trophy. For collectible trophies, <strong>Case Closed</strong> (75 Files) is the most missable, many files are locked behind point-of-no-return transitions.</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Can I get Platinum without playing on Insanity?</div>
 <div><strong>No.</strong> Remarkable Agent (Gold trophy) requires completing the main story on Insanity difficulty. There is no workaround. However, Infinite Requiem makes it manageable for any skill level.</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Do I need NG+ for Platinum?</div>
 <div><strong>Yes</strong>; to access Insanity difficulty (which requires one prior completion). Most players use the first playthrough for collectibles, then NG+ on Insanity for Remarkable Agent.</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Are there any DLC trophies?</div>
 <div>As of v1.02 (May 2026), there are <strong>49 base game trophies</strong> with no DLC trophies announced. Capcom may add a "Leon Must Die Forever" mode that includes additional achievements.</div>
