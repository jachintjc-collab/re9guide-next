<h1>Flame Rounds — the incendiary ammo that specifically counters Plant variants</h1>
<p>Flame Rounds are specialty ammunition for the GM-79 Grenade Launcher and certain shotgun configurations. They burn over time. Specifically effective against Plant 43 variants. Generally less impactful than standard ammo.</p>

<blockquote>Flame Rounds are situational tools. Save them for Plant 43 encounters. Don't use them as general combat ammo.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Initial damage: 80. Burn DOT: 30/sec for 5 seconds = 150 total burn damage. Total per round: 230 damage.</p>

<h2>Why Plant 43 specifically</h2>
<p>Plant 43 variants take +50% damage from fire-source attacks per RE9 codex. A flame round on Plant 43 deals 345 effective damage. Standard grenades deal 200. Significant advantage.</p>

<h2>Best Flame Round scenarios</h2>
<p>Chapter 3 Plant 43 mini-boss. Chapter 7 Plant 43 hybrid encounters. Chapter 8 final boss tendrils (briefly fire-vulnerable in phase 2).</p>

<h2>Ammo scarcity</h2>
<p>Flame Rounds: 6-8 per playthrough. Save specifically for Plant 43 encounters.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>Insanity Plant 43 encounters become much more manageable with Flame Rounds. Plan inventory specifically.</p>
