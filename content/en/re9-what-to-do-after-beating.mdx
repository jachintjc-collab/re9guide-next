<p><strong>Short answer:</strong> <strong>1) Start NG+ for Infinite Requiem Revolver + Master Key.</strong> <strong>2) Hunt remaining 7 endings.</strong> <strong>3) Insanity NG+ for Chunk family audio + Eveline Reference Photo.</strong> <strong>4) Speedrun for S-Rank trophy.</strong> Full post-game checklist below.</p>
<h2>The 4-step post-game roadmap</h2>

<h3>Step 1: Immediate NG+ (after first ending)</h3>
<p><strong>What you unlock:</strong></p>
<ul>
 <li>Infinite Requiem Revolver charm (big for Insanity)</li>
 <li>Master Key (opens 7 previously locked rooms)</li>
 <li>Phoenix Down charm (free, auto-equipped)</li>
 <li>Cutscene Gallery in main menu</li>
</ul>
<p>See our <a href="re9-ng-plus-what-carries-over.html">NG+ carryover guide</a>.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Hunt the remaining 7 endings</h3>
<p>RE9 has 8 endings total. First playthrough = 1 ending. NG+ gives free chapter selection.</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Grace endings (4):</strong> True, Bad, Secret, Insanity</li>
 <li><strong>Leon endings (4):</strong> True, Bad, Secret, Insanity</li>
 <li><strong>Trophy:</strong> "Ending Master" (8/8)</li>
</ul>
<p>See our <a href="re9-best-endings-ranked-tier-list.html">endings tier list</a>.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Insanity NG+ (the full experience)</h3>
<p><strong>What this unlocks:</strong></p>
<ul>
 <li>"True Survivor" trophy</li>
 <li>Chunk family audio (most emotional moment in RE9)</li>
 <li>Eveline Reference Photo (Vivien lab, Insanity-only)</li>
 <li>Ada Wong beacon cutscene (post-completion)</li>
</ul>
<p>See our <a href="re9-insanity-survival-guide.html">Insanity survival guide</a>.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Speedrun for S-Rank</h3>
<p><strong>What this unlocks:</strong></p>
<ul>
 <li>"Speed Demon" trophy (Sub-4-hour)</li>
 <li>"S-Rank" trophy (final completionist trophy)</li>
 <li>Last piece of Platinum trophy</li>
</ul>
<p>See our <a href="speedrun-guide-sub-4-hours.html">sub-4-hour speedrun guide</a>.</p>

<div>
 <table>
 <tr><th>Goal</th><th>Estimated time</th><th>Difficulty required</th></tr>

 <tr><td><strong>Platinum trophy</strong></td><td><strong>35-45 hours total</strong></td><td>3 playthroughs minimum</td></tr>

<h2>Don't miss these post-game moments</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Marcus Halverson family audio (Insanity-only)</strong> — most emotionally devastating moment in RE9. Don't skip the cutscene.</li>
 <li><strong>Ada Wong beacon</strong>; sets up future RE10 / DLC content.</li>
 <li><strong>Vivien Reference Photo</strong>. Insanity NG+ Vivien lab. Final piece of the Eveline-Vivien puzzle.</li>
 <li><strong>Eveline Bulgaria photo detail</strong> — hidden in Mia helicopter cameo. Foreshadows RE10.</li>
</ol>

<h2>What to skip on replay</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Tutorial chapter</strong> (Wrenwood Hotel), pace yourself, don't re-engage Chunk</li>
 <li><strong>Courtyard</strong> (Ch. 4); speedrun east wall skips drone segment</li>
 <li><strong>Care Center 3rd floor</strong> — only matters first playthrough</li>
</ul>
