<p><strong>The E-virus is the family of mold-virus chimeras at the core of RE7-RE9.</strong> Eveline strain. Plant 43 variant. Marcus Halverson exposure. This is the complete biology decode.</p>
<h2>Complete chronological breakdown</h2>
<div><table><tr><th>When</th><th>Event</th></tr><tr><td><strong>Core biology</strong></td><td>E-virus is a chimera: fungal mycelium + viral encapsulation. Spreads through spores (airborne) and direct mold contact. Mutation rate: ~12% per generation.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Eveline strain (RE7)</strong></td><td>Original E-virus carrier. Bio-weapon designation: subject E-001. Designed to spread mold influence + behavioral conditioning. Defeated 2017.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>E-007 Vivien research strain</strong></td><td>Vivien Ashcroft 2009 modified strain. Reduced spread rate. Increased behavioral attachment. Foundation for therapeutic research.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>E-014 ARK research strain</strong></td><td>ARK Facility 2018 strain modification. Higher mutation, faster growth. Foundation for Plant 43 variant.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Plant 43 variant</strong></td><td>ARK Facility 2018-2020 synthesized variant. Replaces viral encapsulation with thicker mycelium. Slower spread but harder to kill. Marcus exposure protocol used this strain.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Transmission vectors</strong></td><td>1) Airborne spores (8-second proximity DoT), 2) Direct mold contact (instant infection), 3) Mind influence (via established mold network), 4) Inherited (Rose Winters).</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Treatment failures</strong></td><td>All conventional antiviral treatments fail. Vivien&rsquo;s 2009 research showed behavioral conditioning could counteract mind influence; but couldn&rsquo;t eliminate physical infection. Marcus&rsquo;s therapeutic hypothesis was based on this.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Marcus tragedy connection</strong></td><td>Marcus believed Plant 43&rsquo;s mycelium structure could be reverse-engineered into leukemia treatment. He self-administered after Lily died. Resulted in Chunk transformation.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Mia Winters partial immunity</strong></td><td>Mia&rsquo;s 2017 exposure left her partially immune. Cannot reinfect via spores. Can pass partial immunity to Rose.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Eveline reappearance theory</strong></td><td>E-virus consciousness persists in mold network. Each subsequent exposure can &ldquo;reactivate&rdquo; Eveline patterns. Vivien&rsquo;s 2009 notes predicted this.</td></tr></table></div>
