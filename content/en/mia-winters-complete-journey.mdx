<p><strong>Mia Winters survives where Ethan didn&rsquo;t.</strong> Her arc from RE7&rsquo;s infected tanker survivor to RE9&rsquo;s 3-minute helicopter cameo spans 9 years of canonical RE history. The EW-2017 helicopter is one of the franchise&rsquo;s most loaded easter eggs.</p>

<h2>Complete chronological timeline</h2>
<div>
 <table>
 <tr><th>When</th><th>Event</th></tr>
 <tr><td><strong>Background</strong></td><td>Mia Winters, born 1989. Connections operative recruited for Eveline transport. Originally on tanker route from Africa to USA.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2017 (RE7 events)</strong></td><td>Tanker disaster off Louisiana coast. Mia survives but infected by Eveline mold. Captured by Bakers in Dulvey. Saved by Ethan.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2017-2018</strong></td><td>Mia and Ethan retire to Romania. Mia attempts normalcy. Therapy. Avoiding past.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2018</strong></td><td>Daughter Rose born. Mia hides her child&rsquo;s mold-based powers from authorities.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2021 (RE8 Village events)</strong></td><td>Village invasion. Mia injured trying to protect Rose. Ethan dies in heroic sacrifice. Mia survives. Carries Rose to safety.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2021-2023</strong></td><td>Mia raises Rose in secret. Connections to surviving Eveline mold consciousness continues. Mia knows but doesn&rsquo;t tell Rose.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2023 (Shadows of Rose events)</strong></td><td>Rose enters Eveline&rsquo;s mindscape. Defeats Eveline manifestation. Returns to Mia. Their relationship is strained but intact.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2024-2025</strong></td><td>Mia returns to active operative status. Works with intelligence agencies tracking Eveline reappearances. Keeps Rose hidden.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>February 2026 (RE9 events)</strong></td><td>Mia receives intelligence about Grace Ashcroft and Vivien research. Boards EW-2017 helicopter (registered to Ethan Winters memorial). Arrives at ARK Facility for cameo.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>RE9 cameo (Grace True Ending)</strong></td><td>3-minute scene. Mia speaks to Grace about Vivien. Three lines about Rose: &ldquo;She&rsquo;s safe. She&rsquo;s not coming. That&rsquo;s the deal we made.&rdquo; Cissy Jones returns to the role. Career-best voice work.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>EW-2017 helicopter detail</strong></td><td>&ldquo;EW&rdquo; = Ethan Winters initials. &ldquo;2017&rdquo; = RE7 release year. Capcom didn&rsquo;t need to do this. Subtle tribute.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Future (RE10?)</strong></td><td>Bulgaria photograph in helicopter. Director Nakanishi confirms Mia&rsquo;s arc continues. Speculation: RE10 features Rose as protagonist with Mia returning.</td></tr>
