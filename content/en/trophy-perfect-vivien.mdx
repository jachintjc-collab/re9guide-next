<h1>Perfect Vivien — defeat Vivien Plant 43 boss without taking damage</h1>
<p>Defeat the chapter 3 Vivien Plant 43 boss without losing any HP. Other damage during chapter 3 doesn't disqualify; only damage during the boss encounter counts.</p>

<blockquote>Perfect Vivien is one of RE9's hardest perfect-fight trophies. Plant 43 boss has minimal predictable patterns. Frame-perfect parry mastery required.</blockquote>

<h2>What counts as boss damage</h2>
<p>HP loss during the active boss encounter. The encounter starts when the boss room transitions and ends at boss kill confirmation.</p>
<p>Damage taken from non-boss enemies in adjacent rooms: doesn't count against Perfect Vivien.</p>

<h2>The Plant 43 boss attack patterns</h2>
<p>Vine sweep (predictable, parry-able). Spore cloud (avoid by positioning, not parry-able). Tendril whip (frame-perfect parry only). Boss-room environmental hazards (water puddles + electrical discharge).</p>
<p>The tendril whip is the main run-killer. Parry requires 2-frame window on Standard, 1-frame on Insanity.</p>

<h2>Required loadout</h2>
<p>Mortal Edge knife (NG+ only). Endurance Charm (insurance against accidental damage). Marksman's Eye Charm (crit damage = faster boss death = shorter exposure window). W870 Police fully upgraded.</p>
<p>Pursue this trophy on NG+ only. First-playthrough completion without Mortal Edge knife borderline impossible.</p>

<h2>Save management</h2>
<p>Save before boss room entry. Reload on any HP drop. The boss room has no respawn checkpoint, so save scumming requires manual saves.</p>

<h2>Insanity Perfect Vivien</h2>
<p>Insanity tendril whip frame window shrinks to 1 frame. Standard Perfect Vivien is achievable for patient players; Insanity is for masters only.</p>

<h2>Korean Perfect Vivien strategy</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 boss strategy community on Naver has documented Plant 43 boss frame data including all attack windows. The Korean coverage is essential for pursuing Perfect Vivien on any difficulty.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Perfect Vivien is the trophy that confirms parry mastery. NG+ Mortal Edge + Standard difficulty + Korean Naver strategy = achievable in 10-20 dedicated attempts. Don't pursue without Mortal Edge.</p>
