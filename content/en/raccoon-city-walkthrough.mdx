<h2 id="overview">Chapter overview: open-world hub</h2>
<p>Raccoon City Square is the campaign's most open chapter. The central plaza contains the mandatory Titan Spinner boss, but the four surrounding districts (north / east / south / west) can be tackled in any order. Each district gates one key collectible or item required for the ARK Facility. you can skip districts on a first run if you want, but you'll regret it during the Victor Gideon fight.</p>
<p>This is also the chapter where you pick up the <strong>Marksman 1A Rifle</strong>, the single most important weapon for the Plant 43 + Victor Gideon fights. Do <em>not</em> skip the north district.</p>

<h2 id="approach">Approach from the Orphanage</h2>
<p>The Orphanage exit drops Leon onto the Square's northeast corner. The first thing you see is the rotating shadow of the Titan Spinner across the plaza. that's your boss.</p>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Hug the perimeter</strong> — the Titan Spinner has aggro range only inside the plaza fountain ring.</li>
 <li><strong>Tag the save typewriter</strong> in the boarded-up coffee shop at the northeast corner.</li>
 <li><strong>Decide your district order</strong>. Recommended for a first run: north (Marksman) → east (Raccoons) → south (puzzle) → west (keycard) → plaza (boss).</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="plaza">Central plaza: Titan Spinner boss</h2>
<p>The Titan Spinner is a giant rotating bioweapon that occupies the central fountain plaza. Full tactics in our <a href="/how-to-beat-titan-spinner/">Titan Spinner boss guide</a>. Key points:</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Cover-based fight</strong>; use the abandoned cars and bus wreckage as moving cover.</li>
 <li><strong>Save Marksman 1A rounds</strong> for the head pustule (≈8-shot kill).</li>
 <li><strong>Insanity tip</strong>; the rotation speed doubles in the second phase. Pre-place a grenade trap on the predicted lunge line.</li>
</ul>
<p>The Titan Spinner drops the <strong>ARK Approach Permit</strong> on death, required to enter the ARK Facility chapter.</p>

<h2 id="north">North district: Marksman 1A pickup</h2>
<p>The north district is the pawn shop alley. The <strong>Marksman 1A Rifle</strong> is in the locked back room of "Hank's Pawn", accessible by shooting the padlock through the front window.</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Marksman 1A Rifle</strong> + 12 starter rounds.</li>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #15</strong> on the rooftop air vent (jump from the fire escape).</li>
 <li><strong>Antique coin</strong> in the cash register drawer.</li>
 <li><strong>File: Hank's Pawn Receipt</strong> — references RE2 cast.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="east">East district: Mr. Raccoon dolls + safe</h2>
<p>The east district is the broken footbridge area. Two of the missable <a href="/all-25-mr-raccoon-locations/">Mr. Raccoon dolls</a> live here:</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #16</strong> on top of the destroyed pillar (need explosive damage from grenade or RPG).</li>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #17</strong> in the boarded-up fountain stairwell.</li>
 <li><strong>Safe #5 alternate location</strong>; bonus reward if all 4 prior safes opened (see <a href="/all-5-safe-codes-guide/">safe codes guide</a>).</li>
 <li><strong>2× antique coins</strong> + <strong>3× Mr. Raccoon doll fragments</strong>.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="south">South district: Quartz puzzle</h2>
<p>The south district is the half-collapsed shopping mall. The escalator pit has a Quartz puzzle similar to the basement's; three rotating discs to align so the cracked window opens. Solution: <strong>outer disc 2 clockwise, middle 1 counter, inner 3 clockwise</strong>.</p>
<p>Solving it drops a <strong>Quartz Piece</strong> (the last one needed for the ARK door) plus an Eye Spy Charm refill. Full step-by-step in <a href="/all-quartz-puzzle-solutions/">quartz puzzles guide</a>.</p>

<h2 id="west">West district: ARK Keycard</h2>
<p>The west district is the train station ruins. The <strong>ARK Keycard</strong> is on the dead engineer in the locomotive cabin — climb the side ladder, drop through the broken roof. Required to enter the ARK Facility (you cannot proceed without it).</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>ARK Keycard</strong> (mandatory).</li>
 <li><strong>File: Engineer's Final Transmission</strong>, last narrative piece before ARK.</li>
 <li><strong>Mortal Edge Hatchet refill</strong> on the workbench (free durability restore).</li>
</ul>
<div>💡 <strong>Tip:</strong> Use the train station save room before exiting. Once you trigger the ARK Facility cutscene, you cannot return to Raccoon City Square.</div>

<h2 id="next">What comes next: ARK Facility</h2>
<p>With all 4 districts cleared and the Titan Spinner down, the ARK Approach Permit + ARK Keycard unlock the final chapter. The ARK Facility is the longest and hardest chapter in the campaign. Plant 43, The Commander, and Victor Gideon, all in one sprint.</p>
