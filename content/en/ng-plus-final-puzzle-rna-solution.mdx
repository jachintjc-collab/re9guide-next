<h2>Why this puzzle exists</h2>

<p>"The Girl" is a recurring antagonist throughout Grace's chapters, a child-like figure with bandaged hands who appears in dreams, hallucinations, and key story moments. After the main credits roll on a first playthrough, players who pay attention to her dialogue notice a strange chant: <em>"Let's play. GGC-AAG-AUA-ACG-UGU-CAU."</em></p>

<p>This is RNA notation — the four letters G/A/U/C represent the four ribonucleotides (Guanine, Adenine, Uracil, Cytosine). Combined with the Severed Hand item Grace can find in NG+, these letters decode to a Sun-Moon-Star symbol sequence that opens a hidden puzzle in the Lead Researcher's Office.</p>

<h2>Complete walkthrough</h2>

<h3>// PRE-NG+ PREPARATION (first playthrough)</h3>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 01</div>
 <div>Find <strong>Marie's Doll</strong> in the Collection &amp; Testing room (Care Center Basement). It looks like an old porcelain doll with bandaged hands — visually similar to The Girl.</div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 02</div>
 <div>Store Marie's Doll in <strong>Grace's item box</strong> immediately. This is critical, items in the box carry over to NG+.</div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 03</div>
 <div>Find the <strong>Severed Hand</strong> in the Care Center West Wing 2F (sealed locker, Level 2 Wristband required). Use the <strong>Laser Microscope</strong> in the Blood Lab to analyze it. Records the RNA sequence for NG+.</div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 04</div>
 <div>Read all 3 RNA decoding Files: "<strong>Anomalous Cellular Replication</strong>" (Lab Notes A-12), "<strong>The G-C Bond</strong>" (Researcher's Diary 4), and "<strong>Bandaged Anomaly</strong>" (East Wing Patient File 11). These unlock the letter mapping.</div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 05</div>
 <div>Complete the main story to credits. Save your clear data when prompted. NG+ unlocks.</div>
</div>

<h3>// NG+ EXECUTION</h3>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 06</div>
 <div>Start NG+. Marie's Doll appears in your starting inventory automatically.</div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 07</div>
 <div>Progress through the story until you <strong>rescue Emily</strong> from the Care Center Basement.</div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 08</div>
 <div>Return to the <strong>Blood Lab</strong> with Emily. Re-analyze the Severed Hand on the Laser Microscope. The Sun Puzzle in the Lead Researcher's Office becomes interactable again.</div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 09</div>
 <div>Bring Emily AND Marie's Doll to the Lead Researcher's Office. Approach the Sun Puzzle Box. The puzzle now accepts <strong>18 symbol inputs</strong> instead of 4.</div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 10</div>
 <div>Input the decoded RNA sequence: <strong>Sun-Sun-Sun → Moon-Moon-Sun → Moon-Star-Moon → Moon-Sun-Sun → Star-Sun-Star → Sun-Moon-Star</strong></div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>// STEP 11</div>
 <div>A hidden compartment opens. Inside is a <strong>special letter</strong> from Marie addressed to "The Girl." Picking it up triggers a unique cutscene.</div>
</div>

<h2>What the puzzle reveals</h2>

<p>The cutscene reveals Marie's connection to "The Girl", they are sisters separated by Capcom's bioweapons program. Marie's Doll was originally The Girl's, lost when she was taken by Umbrella researchers years before the events of RE9.</p>

<p>The puzzle's solution implies that Marie chose to remain in the Care Center as a "guardian" of The Girl's spirit, refusing to leave even when offered escape. This thread closes a major emotional arc that's only hinted at in the main story.</p>

<h2>Reward summary</h2>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Hidden trophy "Reunion of the Bandaged"</strong> — Bronze (very rare achievement)</li>
 <li><strong>Special ending cutscene</strong>, extends the post-credits scene by ~3 minutes</li>
 <li><strong>"Marie's Letter" item</strong>, viewable in the Special Content menu post-completion</li>
 <li><strong>Concept art unlock</strong> — early designs of The Girl character</li>
</ul>

<h2>Common mistakes</h2>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Mistake 1:</strong> Selling Marie's Doll. Some players think it's just a junk item, DO NOT sell or discard.</li>
 <li><strong>Mistake 2:</strong> Trying the puzzle in first playthrough. The 18-symbol input only activates in NG+.</li>
 <li><strong>Mistake 3:</strong> Not reading all 3 RNA Files. The mapping is unlocked through these, without them, you can't decode the sequence.</li>
 <li><strong>Mistake 4:</strong> Bringing Emily but forgetting Marie's Doll (or vice versa). Both must be in your inventory simultaneously.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Why this is the "best" RE9 secret</h2>

<p>Capcom designed this puzzle as a true Easter egg for completionists. It requires:</p>

<ul>
 <li>Reading all related Files (lore-deep players)</li>
 <li>Storing rather than selling unusual items (cautious players)</li>
 <li>Two playthroughs minimum (dedicated players)</li>
 <li>Understanding RNA biology basics (puzzle solvers)</li>
</ul>

<p>It's the kind of puzzle that took the speedrun and 100% communities about <strong>two weeks</strong> after launch to fully decode and document. Now that the mapping is public, you can solve it on your second NG+ run.</p>
