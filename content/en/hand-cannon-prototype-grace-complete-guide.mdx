<h1>Hand Cannon Prototype — Grace's Insanity reward, the franchise's best handgun</h1>
<p>The Hand Cannon Prototype unlocks at Insanity completion. It's RE9's most powerful handgun and carries forward to NG+. Pair it with NG+ Mortal Edge knife for the franchise's best dual loadout.</p>

<blockquote>Hand Cannon is RE9's best weapon, full stop. Korean community discussion often defaults to other weapons because Hand Cannon is so dominant it ends discussion.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Base damage: 220. Headshot multiplier: 3.2x. Magazine: 6 rounds. Reload speed: 2.8s. Stagger: maximum (one-shots most enemies including bosses).</p>

<h2>Unlock requirements</h2>
<p>Complete RE9 on Insanity difficulty. Unlocked weapon appears in NG+ inventory automatically. The unlock persists across multiple NG+ playthroughs.</p>

<h2>Why it's broken</h2>
<p>Hand Cannon damage scales past the threshold where enemies can effectively respond. Boss encounters become 3-4 shot kills. Common enemies are one-shot kills regardless of position.</p>

<h2>The ammo limitation</h2>
<p>Hand Cannon ammo is scarce even on NG+. Standard chapter ammo drops total roughly 25-30 rounds per playthrough. Use selectively — boss encounters and emergencies.</p>

<h2>Best Hand Cannon scenarios</h2>
<p>Boss encounters (3-4 shot kills make tight time windows trivial). Mr. X emergency (single shot staggers him significantly). Eveline final boss phase 3 (Hand Cannon shots are particularly effective).</p>

<h2>Insanity NG+ considerations</h2>
<p>Hand Cannon is essentially required for Insanity NG+ pursuit of True Survivor or similar elite trophies. Don't attempt those goals without it unlocked.</p>

<h2>Korean community tier</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 tier lists place Hand Cannon at S+ (the only weapon at that tier). Naver discussion treats Hand Cannon as automatic best handgun.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Hand Cannon transforms RE9's difficulty curve. Unlock it through Insanity. Use it sparingly. Save Hand Cannon ammo for moments that matter.</p>
