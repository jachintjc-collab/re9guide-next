<p><strong>Short answer:</strong> The <strong>Requiem Revolver</strong> is the best weapon in RE9. Highest single-shot damage in the game, headshot multiplier, and the only weapon that reliably staggers <em>all</em> bosses on Insanity difficulty. Best #2: W870 Police shotgun for close-range bosses. Best #3: Clatter Carbine for crowd control.</p>
<h2>Why Requiem Revolver is #1</h2>
<div>
 <table>
 <tr><th>Stat</th><th>Value</th><th>Why it matters</th></tr>
 <tr><td>Base damage</td><td>250 per shot</td><td>2x AR damage, 3x Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol damage</td></tr>
 <tr><td>Headshot multiplier</td><td>×3.5</td><td>875 damage per headshot kills Mr. X in 3 shots</td></tr>
 <tr><td>Stagger reliability</td><td>100% on all bosses</td><td>Only weapon with universal stagger</td></tr>
 <tr><td>Unlocks at Chapter 2</td><td>Yes</td><td>Available early</td></tr>
 <tr><td>NG+ Infinite version</td><td>Yes</td><td>Free unlock after first run</td></tr>
 </table>
</div>

<h2>The S-tier weapons (best 2)</h2>
<h3>#1: Requiem Revolver</h3>
<p>The undisputed best. Pair with <strong>Crafter's Touch</strong> charm to make ammo sustainable. On Insanity, this is what separates survivors from quitters.</p>

<h3>#2: W870 Police shotgun</h3>
<p>Best close-range weapon. Devastates Mr. X transformation, Chunk's spine weak spot, and crowd control. Spread upgrade post-v1.02 is +8%.</p>

<h2>The A-tier weapons</h2>
<h3>#3: Clatter Carbine</h3>
<p>Best for Plant 43 swarm phases and clearing multiple enemies. 60 round capacity. Full-auto on weak points.</p>

<h3>#4: Knife</h3>
<p>Parry tool — not a primary. With Knife Edge charm + Iron Skin, can sustain knife-only Insanity runs.</p>

<h2>Which weapon to upgrade first?</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Requiem Revolver (immediately)</strong>; 12,000 currency for tier-1 upgrade. Tier-3 by Chapter 5.</li>
 <li><strong>W870 Police shotgun (Chapter 4-5)</strong>, second-priority. Tier-2 minimum before Water Treatment.</li>
 <li><strong>Clatter Carbine (Chapter 6)</strong> — third-priority. Tier-2 for RPD encounters.</li>
 <li><strong>Skip</strong>, Don't waste currency on Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol/S&S M232 S&S M232 handgun after Chapter 3.</li>
</ol>
