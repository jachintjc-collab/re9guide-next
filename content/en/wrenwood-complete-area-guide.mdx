<p><strong>Wrenwood is Area 1 of RE9</strong> — the opening hotel where you control Grace and meet Chunk for the first time. This is a 45-minute introduction designed to teach core mechanics: stealth, item pickup, basic combat with the Silencer 9 pistol, and the parry system using Mortal Edge.</p>

<div>
<strong>📍 Area 1 of 4 · Duration: ~45 min · Sub-sections: 1</strong>
</div>

<h2 id="overview">Overview &amp; Structure</h2>
<p>Wrenwood is one of 4 main areas in RE9. The game has no Chapter Select after completion. area progression is strictly linear. You cannot return to this area on a regular save once exited.</p>

<h2 id="bosses">Bosses in this area</h2>
<p>No major bosses. First Chunk encounter is stealth-only (unkillable).</p>

<h2 id="collectibles">Collectibles in Wrenwood</h2>
<ul><li>Worn Notebook (D-tier item)</li><li>Rusty Spoon (RE7 reference, D-tier)</li><li>Eye Spy charm (guest room 217)</li><li>Survivor&rsquo;s Will charm (boiler room)</li></ul>

<h2 id="weapons">Best weapons for Wrenwood</h2>
<p>You have only the starter <strong>Silencer 9 pistol</strong> + Mortal Edge here. Use them carefully, Chunk cannot be killed in this area.</p>

<h2 id="insanity-tactics">Insanity-specific tactics</h2>
<p>On Insanity, even Wrenwood Hotel becomes challenging. Equip Iron Skin + Phoenix Down before entering. Stealth Chunk completely — do not waste resources.</p>

<h2 id="trophies">Trophies earned in Wrenwood</h2>
<ul>
 <li>"Welcome to Wrenwood" (area completion)</li><li>"First Chunk Encounter" (story)</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="faq">FAQ, Wrenwood</h2>

<p><strong>Q: Can I return to Wrenwood after leaving?</strong><br>
A: Not on Standard play. You can revisit on NG+ with free area selection. Plan collectible runs accordingly.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What is the minimum playthrough time for Wrenwood?</strong><br>
A: ~45 min on first playthrough. NG+ speedrun cuts to 60% of that time with Infinite Magnum (Requiem Revolver infinite ammo).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Which charm should I equip for this area?</strong><br>
A: Crafter&rsquo;s Touch + Iron Skin + Survivor&rsquo;s Will (early game stability).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Are there missable collectibles in Wrenwood?</strong><br>
A: Eye Spy charm in room 217 is the only missable. Easy to skip if you don&rsquo;t explore.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What is the Wrenwood hardest moment?</strong><br>
A: First Chunk encounter; but it&rsquo;s scripted unkillable. Just escape.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Does the Wrenwood area have RE callbacks?</strong><br>
A: Subtle — the Rusty Spoon item references RE7&rsquo;s Mia opening. Not visually obvious.</p>

<p><strong>Q: How much currency is available in Wrenwood?</strong><br>
A: Approximately 5,000 from enemy drops + 500 from items.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Is Wrenwood different on Insanity NG+?</strong><br>
A: Same layout, but enemies scaled +40% HP, player takes 2x damage, pickup spawn -30%. Infinite Magnum unlock makes Requiem Revolver mid-fight crafting unnecessary.</p>
