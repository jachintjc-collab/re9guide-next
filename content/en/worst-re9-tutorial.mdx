<h1>Worst RE9 tutorial — Mortal Edge parry teaching specifically</h1>
<p>RE9's tutorial is generally informative. The Mortal Edge parry teaching is the franchise's worst tutorial moment in years.</p>

<blockquote>Mortal Edge parry tutorial happens once, in 4 seconds, in chapter 1. Players who blink miss it. Should have been repeated in chapter 2.</blockquote>

<h2>The 4-second window</h2><p>The Mortal Edge parry mechanic is taught via a brief NPC demonstration during chapter 1's escape sequence. If you're focused on threat instead of NPC, you miss the teaching.</p><h2>What should have happened</h2><p>Repeat the teaching in chapter 2 with explicit prompt. Add tutorial mode practice. Make the parry frame window adjustable for learning.</p><h2>Korean community response</h2><p>Korean Naver community published parry tutorials specifically to compensate for the in-game teaching's failure.
