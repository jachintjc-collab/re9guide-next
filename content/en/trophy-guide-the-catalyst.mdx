<h1>The Catalyst — trigger 5 specific environmental kill sequences</h1>
<p>Trigger 5 specific environmental kills throughout the game. Each is a one-time scripted opportunity to use environment to kill multiple enemies. Trophy ticks at the fifth successful trigger.</p>

<blockquote>The Catalyst is one of RE9's most fun trophies. The environmental kills are well-designed showpieces. Worth pursuing for the gameplay, not just the trophy.</blockquote>

<h2>The 5 environmental kill sequences</h2>
<p>Wrenwood hotel basement chain explosion: shoot the gas tank near the boiler with three approaching zombies in range.</p>
<p>Care Center pharmacy chemical chain: shoot the labeled chemical containers in sequence with enemies clustered. 5-8 kills typical.</p>
<p>Rhodes Hill conservatory chandelier: shoot the chandelier cable with enemies directly below. 3-4 kills.</p>
<p>RPD library bookshelf collapse: shoot the support beam with enemies in the aisle. 2-3 kills.</p>
<p>ARK biolab electrical chain: shoot the main breaker with enemies on the wet floor. 4-6 kills.</p>

<h2>Timing windows</h2>
<p>Each scenario has a specific window when enough enemies are in range. Trigger too early = waste setup. Trigger too late = enemies have moved out of range.</p>
<p>The Care Center pharmacy is the largest window (about 4 seconds). The RPD library is the smallest (about 1 second).</p>

<h2>Reset mechanics</h2>
<p>Each environmental kill is a one-time scripted setup. Failing to trigger doesn't allow reset on the same save. NG+ resets all 5.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>Enemy density on Insanity makes triggering each scenario easier. All 5 are reliably achievable in a single Insanity playthrough with attention to triggering windows.</p>

<h2>Korean community documentation</h2>
<p>Korean Naver community has annotated all 5 environmental kill scenarios with video timing references. The Korean coverage is the best available for understanding the precise trigger windows.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>The Catalyst is one of the most enjoyable RE9 trophies. The environmental kills are RE9's homage to RE4's classic environmental kill design. Pursue with attention rather than treating as checkbox content.</p>
