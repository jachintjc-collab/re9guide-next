<h1>Worst RE9 cutscenes — when the writing fails the performance</h1>
<p>RE9's cutscenes are generally strong. Three specific cutscenes are notably weak: chapter 2 town meeting, chapter 4 Mia phone call (an unused alternate scene), and chapter 6 corridor exposition dump.</p>

<blockquote>Three weak cutscenes can't undermine RE9's overall strong cinematic writing. They're worth noting but don't define the game.</blockquote>

Cuts 3 minutes that don't earn their length.</p><h2>Chapter 4 unused Mia phone</h2><p>Available in NG+ data mining. Capcom cut it for good reason but the data remains.
