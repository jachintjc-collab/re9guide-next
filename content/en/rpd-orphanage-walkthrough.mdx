<h2 id="overview">Chapter overview: RPD + Orphanage</h2>
<p>This is the campaign's longest single chapter and its narrative pivot. Leon's history with the RPD becomes plot-relevant for the first time, the Mr. X chase introduces the campaign's scariest pursuit mechanic, and the Orphanage delivers the emotional revelation that recontextualizes Grace's arc.</p>
<p>Budget at least 90 minutes for a first run. Save before triggering the RPD reception cutscene, there are limited save points until you reach the Orphanage.</p>

<h2 id="approach">Approach: Raccoon City waterfront</h2>
<p>Leon emerges from the Water Treatment Plant rooftop ladder onto the waterfront docks. The path to the RPD is linear (no exploration branches) but has three pickup points:</p>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Container yard</strong> — antique coin behind the leftmost container (counts toward <a href="all-22-antique-coins-locations.html">22 coins</a>).</li>
 <li><strong>Overturned police cruiser</strong>; Requiem Revolver rounds in the open trunk.</li>
 <li><strong>Burning checkpoint</strong>. file: "RPD Evacuation Notice" on the dead officer's clipboard.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="rpd-entry">RPD entry hall + reception</h2>
<p>The RPD's main entrance is sealed; you enter through the parking garage side door. The reception hall is exactly as RE2 veterans remember it — the goddess statue, the directory board, and the spiral staircase up to East Wing 2F.</p>
<p>Two zombies in the entry, four in the reception hall. Conserve ammo where you can, the Kendo detour next will refill you anyway, but you want to leave the reception with the S&S M232 handgun reloaded.</p>

<h2 id="kendo">Kendo Gun Shop detour (optional but recommended)</h2>
<p>Kendo's Gun Shop is a two-block detour east of the RPD entry. Robert Kendo is gone but the shop is unlocked. first time in the series the player can directly loot the gun shop.</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Kendo Custom S&S M232 S&S M232 handgun</strong> — significantly stronger than the default sidearm. Bring it to the final boss.</li>
 <li><strong>50 S&S M232 handgun rounds + 20 W870 Police shotgun shells</strong> behind the back-room counter.</li>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #11</strong> on the gun-shop sign (shoot it from outside before entering).</li>
 <li><strong>File: "Kendo's Last Letter"</strong> on the back-room desk; narrative.</li>
</ul>
<div>💡 <strong>Tip:</strong> The Kendo detour is the last safe area before Mr. X spawns. Stock everything here, then return to the RPD.</div>

<h2 id="evidence">RPD Evidence Room safe</h2>
<p>The Evidence Room is on RPD East Wing 1F. Behind the cage door is Safe #3 from our <a href="all-5-safe-codes-guide.html">safe codes guide</a>. The room is patrolled by one heavy zombie; knife stealth-kill from behind, do not waste ammo.</p>
<p>The safe contains: <strong>Requiem Revolver ammo (8 rounds)</strong> + <strong>First Aid Spray</strong> + <strong>antique coin</strong>. Open it now — you cannot return after Mr. X spawns.</p>

<h2 id="mrx">Mr. X first encounter (chase sequence)</h2>
<p>Triggering the East Wing 2F cutscene drops the ceiling and Mr. X drops down. This first encounter is a <strong>chase, not a fight</strong>. he cannot be killed yet and his HP regenerates instantly.</p>
<p>Full route map + parry strategy in our <a href="how-to-beat-mr-x-super-tyrant.html">Mr. X / Super Tyrant boss guide</a>. Quick summary:</p>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Do not fight</strong>; sprint to the East Wing 2F window.</li>
 <li><strong>Drop to the courtyard awning</strong> below the window — auto-cutscene.</li>
 <li><strong>Run east through the broken fence</strong> into the alley.</li>
 <li><strong>The Orphanage gate</strong> is at the end of the alley. Mr. X cannot pass it.</li>
</ol>
<div>⚠ <strong>If Mr. X grabs you</strong>, the damage is non-lethal in this sequence but it costs 20 seconds. Stay ahead of him by ~5 meters at all times.</div>

<h2 id="orphanage">Orphanage second half</h2>
<p>The Orphanage is the chapter's emotional core. The gameplay slows down: zero combat, three story rooms to explore, and a single major collectible. the <strong>Orphanage Keepsake</strong>, which becomes a key item for the Victor Gideon final boss's alternate ending.</p>
<p>Read every file in the headmistress' office. Three of them tie back to plot beats from the Wrenwood Hotel intro chapter. The save typewriter is on the second floor — use it before exiting.</p>
<p>The Orphanage transitions automatically to Raccoon City Square once you collect the Keepsake.</p>

<h2 id="next">What comes next: Raccoon City Square</h2>
<p>Raccoon City Square is the next chapter, the open-world hub between Orphanage and ARK Facility. The Titan Spinner boss waits in the central plaza, and the side districts contain three of the campaign's missable Mr. Raccoon dolls.</p>
