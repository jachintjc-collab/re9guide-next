<h1>Stuck: Eveline phase 3 can't dodge — the multi-voice attack pattern</h1>
<p>Eveline final boss phase 3 has the multi-voice chorus attack that confuses many players. Specific dodging strategy required.</p>

<blockquote>Phase 3 isn't dodge-based. It's parry-based. The multi-voice indicates parry windows, not dodge windows. Change your approach.</blockquote>

<h2>The actual mechanic</h2><p>Phase 3 multi-voice attacks are parry-triggered, not dodge-triggered. Each voice harmonic indicates a frame-perfect parry opportunity.</p><h2>What dodge fails</h2><p>Dodging works for phase 1 and 2. Phase 3 enemy projectiles are too dense for dodge timing. Parry is required.</p><h2>What works</h2><p>Mortal Edge knife equipped. L2 parry timing matching audio harmonics. The phase 3 audio is the teaching tool.</p><h2>If Mortal Edge not unlocked</h2><p>Standard knife works but each parry deals less damage. Phase 3 takes longer; survival window tighter.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Phase 3 is parry-based combat. If you can't parry reliably, drop difficulty for first completion, then practice on NG+. The audio cues are very generous if you trust them.</p>
