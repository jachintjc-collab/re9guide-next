<h2 id="overview">Boss overview: A modernized Plant 42</h2>

<p>Long-time Resident Evil fans will recognize Plant 43's lineage; it's a clear callback to Plant 42 from the original Resident Evil. This iteration has been bioweaponized into something far more dangerous: a three-headed plant with snapping mandibles, a pulsing flower core, and explosive seed pods.</p>

<p>You'll encounter Plant 43 immediately upon entering the ARK Facility courtyard. There's no warm-up — the cutscene rolls and the fight starts in seconds.</p>

<h2 id="prep">Pre-fight preparation</h2>

<p>Before stepping into the ARK courtyard, prepare the following:</p>

<ol>
 <li><strong>Sweep the supply boxes</strong>; there are two destructible supply boxes on the right side of the arena. Get them at fight start.</li>
 <li><strong>Equip the SMG</strong> as your primary. its high fire rate is perfect for shredding the side buds quickly.</li>
 <li><strong>Have the Marksman 1A ready</strong> — single-shot precision rifle for the central flower.</li>
 <li><strong>Stock at least 2 First Aid Sprays</strong>; Plant 43's middle-head rush deals significant damage if it connects.</li>
 <li><strong>Reload everything</strong> before the cutscene triggers.</li>
</ol>

<div>
 💡 <strong>Tip:</strong> If you have Hand Grenades, use them on the side buds. Two grenades can destroy both side buds simultaneously, dramatically shortening the fight.
</div>

<h2 id="strategy">Step-by-step strategy</h2>

<h3>// PHASE 1: DESTROY SIDE BUDS</h3>

<p>The fight begins with all three heads active. The two side buds will lunge at Leon with snapping bites. The central head spits seed projectiles.</p>

<ol>
 <li><strong>Sprint to the right side of the arena</strong>. destructible supply boxes are here.</li>
 <li><strong>Stay mobile</strong> — circle the courtyard counter-clockwise to avoid the side buds' lunges.</li>
 <li><strong>Focus fire on the LEFT side bud first</strong>; it has slightly less HP than the right.</li>
 <li><strong>SMG bursts work best</strong>. empty 1-2 magazines per bud.</li>
 <li><strong>Watch for the middle-head rush</strong> — when the central flower charges forward, sidestep immediately.</li>
</ol>

<h3>// PHASE 2: TARGET THE CENTRAL FLOWER</h3>

<p>Once both side buds are destroyed, Plant 43 enters its vulnerable state. The central head's maw opens fully, exposing the glowing red flower.</p>

<ol>
 <li><strong>Switch to a precision weapon</strong>; Marksman 1A or fully-upgraded W870 Police shotgun.</li>
 <li><strong>Wait for the maw to open</strong>; the flower is only vulnerable when the mouth is fully extended.</li>
 <li><strong>Land 3-4 precise shots</strong> on the flower core.</li>
 <li><strong>Dodge the seed projectiles</strong> — Plant 43 spits clusters of explosive seeds during this phase.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="finisher">The cinematic finisher</h2>

<p>This is the highlight of the fight. After enough damage to the central flower, Plant 43 thrashes violently and vomits; dislodging a <strong>truck full of explosive canisters</strong> directly in front of Leon.</p>

<div>
 ⚠ <strong>Don't miss the cue:</strong> When the truck appears, you have a <strong>3-4 second window</strong> to shoot the canisters. If you miss it, Plant 43 recovers and you have to whittle it down conventionally; much slower.
</div>

<h3>// HOW TO TRIGGER THE FINISHER</h3>

<ol>
 <li><strong>Land enough flower shots</strong> — typically 3-4 hits with Requiem or 6-8 with S&S M232 handgun.</li>
 <li><strong>Watch the cutscene briefly</strong>, Plant 43 will rear back and spit out the truck.</li>
 <li><strong>Quickly aim at the canisters</strong>, they're labeled with hazard symbols, easy to spot.</li>
 <li><strong>Fire one shot</strong> — any weapon works; the canisters are weak to all damage.</li>
 <li><strong>Massive explosion = instant kill</strong>. Cinematic plays out automatically.</li>
</ol>

<div>
 <div><h4>// CANISTER SHOT</h4><p>Aim center-mass on the truck cluster. Even S&S M232 handgun rounds detonate them.</p></div>
 <div><h4>// IF YOU MISS</h4><p>Plant 43 recovers but doesn't gain HP. Just keep shooting the flower normally.</p></div>
</div>

<h2 id="insanity">Insanity difficulty tips</h2>

<div>
 ⚠ On Insanity, Plant 43's seed projectiles can deal 40-60% HP damage per hit. Movement is non-negotiable.
</div>

<p>Insanity-specific changes:</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Side bud HP scales ~2x</strong>; bring more SMG ammo or use Hand Grenades</li>
 <li><strong>Seed projectile splash damage increases</strong>. keep moving even when reloading</li>
 <li><strong>Middle-head rush one-shots if armor is broken</strong> — repair armor before this fight</li>
 <li><strong>The canister finisher window is shorter</strong>; about 2.5 seconds. Don't hesitate.</li>
 <li><strong>Infinite Ammo trivializes this fight</strong>. Infinite Requiem one-shots the flower core in 2 hits</li>
</ul>

<h2>What comes next</h2>

<p>After Plant 43 falls, Leon enters the ARK Facility's interior corridors. Your next major encounter is <strong>The Commander</strong> — the second-hardest fight in the game and the source of the <em>Mortal Edge Hatchet</em> (mandatory for the Victor Gideon final boss).</p>

<p>Don't skip Plant 43's drop pickups. Hand Grenades and Requiem Bullets sometimes drop from the corpse. Sweep the area before exiting.</p>
