<h1>Grace True Ending — unlock the true ending route</h1>
<p>The true ending requires specific dialogue choices and complete chapter 6 archive reading. Trophy unlocks at the post-credits scene (the Korean BSAA field commander scene). Most players miss this without a guide.</p>

<blockquote>Grace True Ending is the franchise's most narratively important trophy. Required watching for anyone planning to play RE10. The unlock conditions are obscure but the payoff is significant.</blockquote>

<h2>The unlock conditions</h2>
<p>Read all chapter 6 RPD archive documents (specifically the Sherry Birkin involvement files and the Marvin Branagh case file). Choose the third dialogue option ("I don't accept this") during the chapter 8 Eveline final boss conversation. Complete the boss fight.</p>
<p>Both conditions are necessary. Missing either fails the true ending and gives the standard ending instead.</p>

<h2>The chapter 6 archive details</h2>
<p>Five specific documents must be read: "Sherry Birkin — BSAA personnel file" (chapter 6 main archive), "Reconstruction memo 2010" (same archive), "Marvin Branagh case file 1998" (RPD chief's office), "Investigator deaths summary 2025-2026" (chapter 6 secondary archive), "Brother case file 1998-0127" (chapter 6 deep archive — requires manor study key from chapter 5).</p>
<p>The brother case file is the hardest to access. It requires the manor study key from chapter 5 (acquired by solving the manor study puzzle). Most players skip the puzzle and lock themselves out of true ending without realizing.</p>

<h2>The dialogue choice details</h2>
<p>During the Eveline boss fight, Eveline initiates three dialogue exchanges. The third exchange (mid-fight, after phase 2 begins) offers three response options: "I had to" (default, standard ending), "I'm sorry too" (alternative standard), "I don't accept this" (true ending requirement).</p>
<p>The third option only appears if you've read all five chapter 6 archive documents. Missing the documents removes the option from the dialogue menu — most players see only two options and don't realize a third was supposed to be available.</p>

<h2>Verifying you're on track</h2>
<p>The pause menu's archive section shows read documents. If all five are marked read, you're eligible for the true ending dialogue option. Check before the chapter 8 boss fight.</p>
<p>If you reach the Eveline fight without all five read, you cannot acquire the third dialogue option on that save. NG+ replay required.</p>

<h2>The true ending content</h2>
<p>Three additional minutes beyond the standard ending. Sherry Birkin phone call. BSAA conference room scene with hostile faction. "TO BE CONTINUED" title card. Confirms RE10 development direction.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>True ending trophy doesn't require Insanity. Standard difficulty completion with correct conditions produces the trophy. Pursue on Standard if you only want the trophy without the Insanity challenge.</p>

<h2>Korean community on true ending</h2>
<p>Korean Naver community documented the true ending unlock conditions within 36 hours of launch. The conditions are more thoroughly documented in Korean RE9 guides than in English-language equivalents. Search "RE9 진엔딩 조건" for current Naver coverage.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Grace True Ending is the trophy that distinguishes engaged players from speedrunners. Plan for it. The unlock conditions reward careful chapter 5-6 exploration. The post-credits content is essential for RE10 anticipation.</p>
