<h1>Knife Novice — 10 knife kills</h1>
<p>Kill 10 enemies with the knife. Trophy fires at the 10th knife kill. Easy to achieve in chapter 1-2 with deliberate knife-only encounters.</p>

<blockquote>Knife Novice is a starter knife trophy. Pursue Predator instead for meaningful knife engagement.</blockquote>

<h2>What counts</h2>
<p>Direct knife kills only. Same rules as Predator (covered in trophy-guide-predator). 10 instead of 100.</p>

<h2>Easiest farming</h2>
<p>Chapter 1 hotel basement on Standard. Multiple zombies, knife-killable. Achievable within 5 minutes of chapter start.</p>

<h2>Why it's an easy trophy</h2>
<p>The 10-kill threshold is calibrated for early-game completion. Most players hit it organically by chapter 2 without specifically pursuing it.</p>

<h2>Insanity Knife Novice</h2>
<p>Slightly harder due to enemy HP scaling. Still achievable within chapter 1-2 timeframe.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Knife Novice happens. If you've played through chapter 2 and don't have it, you haven't used the knife at all — adjust your play style.</p>
