<h1>Vivien's Truth — uncover Vivien's complete research history</h1>
<p>Vivien's Truth requires reading all 12 Vivien Davies-related documents scattered through chapters 1, 3, 5, 6, and 7. The documents combined tell Vivien's complete personal and research history. Trophy ticks at the 12th document read.</p>

<blockquote>Vivien's Truth is the trophy that rewards engagement with RE9's best character. The combined documents are one of the franchise's strongest character backstories.</blockquote>

<h2>The 12 Vivien documents</h2>
<p>Chapter 1: Wrenwood newspaper article about the Care Center reopening (mentions Vivien as new director).</p>
<p>Chapter 3: Five Vivien personal documents in the Care Center (research notes, two journal entries, her husband's photograph with inscription, a research grant approval letter).</p>
<p>Chapter 5: Three documents in Rhodes Hill manor (her husband's terminal illness records, Vivien's diary entry from the year before her husband's death, her academic publication list).</p>
<p>Chapter 6: One document in the RPD archives (BSAA-Vivien funding agreement documents).</p>
<p>Chapter 7: Two documents in ARK (Vivien's last research log, a final letter never sent).</p>

<h2>The narrative arc</h2>
<p>Read in order, the documents tell the story of: a competent researcher whose husband's death motivated her into specific gene therapy research, who accepted BSAA funding without understanding the bioweapon implications, who recognized the safety problems too late, who chose to publish her research rather than hide it.</p>
<p>The arc is RE9's strongest piece of antagonist development. Vivien becomes a tragic figure rather than a villain by the time you've read all 12.</p>

<h2>The final letter document</h2>
<p>The chapter 7 ARK "final letter never sent" is the document players most often miss. It's hidden in Vivien's personal workstation drawer, only accessible after triggering a specific terminal interaction. The letter is addressed to her husband and is unfinished — it stops mid-sentence.</p>

<h2>Trophy timing</h2>
<p>The trophy fires immediately on reading the 12th document, regardless of which one you read last. Order doesn't matter for the trophy.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>All documents accessible on Standard. Insanity-specific challenges don't apply. Pursue on Standard for trophy efficiency.</p>
