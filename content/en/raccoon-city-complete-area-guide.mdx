<h2 id="overview">Overview &amp; Structure</h2>
<p>Raccoon City is one of 4 main areas in RE9. The game has no Chapter Select after completion — area progression is strictly linear. You cannot return to this area on a regular save once exited.</p>

<h2 id="weapons">Best weapons for Raccoon City</h2>
<p>Stock the <strong>W870 Police shotgun</strong> upgraded to T2-T3 for Mr. X transformation. The <strong>Clatter Carbine</strong> is required for Titan Spinner Phase 2.</p>

<h2 id="insanity-tactics">Insanity-specific tactics</h2>
<p>Insanity Raccoon City: Mr. X one-shots without Iron Skin charm. RPD pillars are mandatory cover. Don&rsquo;t leave RPD with less than 4 Requiem Revolver rounds for Titan Spinner.</p>

<h2 id="faq">FAQ, Raccoon City</h2>

<p><strong>Q: Can I return to Raccoon City after leaving?</strong><br>
A: Not on Standard play. You can revisit on NG+ with free area selection. Plan collectible runs accordingly.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What is the minimum playthrough time for Raccoon City?</strong><br>
A: ~2.5 hours on first playthrough. NG+ speedrun cuts to 60% of that time with Infinite Magnum (Requiem Revolver infinite ammo).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Which charm should I equip for this area?</strong><br>
A: Crafter&rsquo;s Touch + Iron Skin + Phoenix Down + Bloodhound (4-slot NG+).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Are there missable collectibles in Raccoon City?</strong><br>
A: Silver Pendant (Orphanage attic), Knife Edge (alley shop), Quartz Fragment 5 (clock tower top) all require deliberate exploration.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What is the Raccoon City hardest moment?</strong><br>
A: Mr. X transformation cutscene catches first-time players off-guard. Pre-stock health before triggering it.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Does the Raccoon City area have RE callbacks?</strong><br>
A: MASSIVE, RPD Main Hall is a pixel-for-pixel RE2 Remake recreation aged 28 years. Officer Leon Kennedy 1998 documents. S.T.A.R.S. office reference. Goddess statue.</p>

<p><strong>Q: How much currency is available in Raccoon City?</strong><br>
A: Approximately 20,000 total (mainly enemy drops). No Antique Coins in this area.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Is Raccoon City different on Insanity NG+?</strong><br>
A: Same layout, but enemies scaled +40% HP, player takes 2x damage, pickup spawn -30%. Infinite Magnum unlock makes Requiem Revolver mid-fight crafting unnecessary.</p>
