<h1>Knife Master — 100 frame-perfect parries</h1>
<p>Land 100 frame-perfect parries (the precise parry window, not the wider basic parry). Trophy fires at the 100th frame-perfect registration. Difficult to achieve but possible with knife-combat focus.</p>

<blockquote>Knife Master is the franchise's most precision-demanding trophy. Mortal Edge knife makes it manageable on Standard. Insanity Knife Master is for true masters.</blockquote>

<h2>What frame-perfect parry means</h2>
<p>RE9 has two parry windows: basic parry (10 frames Standard, 7 frames Insanity) and frame-perfect parry (2 frames Standard, 1 frame Insanity).</p>
<p>Basic parry triggers from any input within the window. Frame-perfect parry requires hitting the exact frame the attack lands. The visual feedback differs — frame-perfect produces a distinct golden flash.</p>

<h2>Why frame-perfect specifically</h2>
<p>The trophy specifically counts frame-perfect registrations, not basic parries. This is the trophy's main difficulty. Most parry attempts produce basic parries, which feel correct but don't count toward Knife Master.</p>

<h2>Best learning environment</h2>
<p>Chapter 1 hotel basement on Standard. Multiple zombies, predictable attack patterns, no time pressure. Practice the timing here before pursuing the trophy elsewhere.</p>
<p>Chapter 3 Care Center hallways on Insanity for the most challenging practice — fewer mistakes allowed.</p>

<h2>Insanity Knife Master</h2>
<p>The frame-perfect window shrinks to 1 frame on Insanity. Trophy becomes substantially harder. Most players who pursue Knife Master do so on Standard first, then attempt Insanity separately.</p>
<p>Mortal Edge knife is essential for Insanity attempts — the damage multiplier makes mistakes more recoverable.</p>

<h2>Korean knife combat community</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 knife-combat threads on Naver have documented frame-perfect parry timing for every enemy attack type. The Korean coverage includes specific visual cues to identify the frame window.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Knife Master rewards combat precision. Build the muscle memory on Standard before attempting Insanity. The trophy comes from accumulated practice rather than dedicated grind sessions.</p>
