<h1>Worst RE9 camera choices — when the framing fails</h1>
<p>RE9's third-person camera generally works. Specific scenarios cause framing problems: tight corridor combat, certain boss encounters, vertical movement.</p>

<blockquote>Camera issues in RE9 are real but minor. Most players don't notice. Specific corridor encounters in chapter 3-4 are the worst offenders.</blockquote>

<h2>Care Center tight corridors</h2><p>Camera collides with walls in 60-90cm corridors, causing visibility blackouts during combat. Chapter 3 has several.</p><h2>Vertical movement framing</h2><p>Climbing animations have brief camera resets that can disorient. Most noticeable in chapter 5 manor stairs.</p><h2>Vivien Plant 43 boss camera</h2><p>The boss arena camera occasionally fails to track player position during phase 2 mobility. Player loses visual contact with boss.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Camera issues are tolerable for Standard play but become problematic on Insanity where visibility loss causes deaths. Capcom should patch corridor collision.</p>
