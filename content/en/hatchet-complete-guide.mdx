<h1>Hatchet — chapter 1 backup melee, retire by chapter 3</h1>
<p>The Hatchet is RE9's secondary melee weapon, found in the chapter 1 Wrenwood toolshed. Slower than knife, more damage per hit. Useful early game. Outclassed by knife with Mortal Edge unlock.</p>

<blockquote>Hatchet is a chapter 1-2 specialty. Retire it once knife mastery improves. Don't carry it into NG+ inventory.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Damage: 85 per hit. Attack speed: 50% slower than knife. Stamina cost: 30% higher than knife.</p>

<h2>Best use cases</h2>
<p>Chapter 1 zombie clearing when ammo is scarce. Chapter 2 Cerberus follow-up after firearm stagger.</p>

<h2>Limitations</h2>
<p>Slower attack window than knife. Higher stamina cost. No parry capability. Doesn't benefit from Mortal Edge upgrade.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>Hatchet on Insanity is borderline non-viable past chapter 2. The slower attack window costs you HP. Replace with knife earlier.</p>

<h2>Korean community tier</h2>
<p>Korean community ranks Hatchet as C-tier. Limited utility. Most Korean players skip it.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Pick up Hatchet for the chapter 1 toolshed event. Use it in chapter 1-2. Don't carry beyond that. Knife is better for the rest of the game.</p>
