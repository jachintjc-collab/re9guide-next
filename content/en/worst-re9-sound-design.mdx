<h1>Worst RE9 sound design — environmental audio gaps</h1>
<p>RE9's sound design is generally strong. Specific environments have audio gaps where ambient sound fails to support gameplay.</p>

<blockquote>RE9 sound design strength is the Mr. X footstep audio. Its weakness is silence in environments that should have ambient threat audio.</blockquote>

<h2>ARK sub-level 2 silence</h2><p>Same environment that fails visually also fails aurally. Long stretches of dead silence between encounters.</p><h2>Chapter 2 outdoor sequences</h2><p>Wind / rain ambient is too quiet. Real forests at night are loud; RE9 forests are silent.</p><h2>What works</h2><p>Mr. X footstep audio (franchise legacy). Eveline phase 3 multi-voice. Vivien Plant 43 boss audio.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Specific gaps. Overall strong. Capcom audio team should pass on quietest sections of ARK.</p>
