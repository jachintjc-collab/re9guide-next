<h2 id="why">Why missables matter in RE9</h2>
<p>Unlike most modern Resident Evil entries, RE9 has multiple <strong>one-way chapter transitions</strong>. Once you leave a chapter, every collectible, event, and trophy gated to that chapter is permanently closed for that playthrough. For trophy hunters this is especially brutal — some trophies are only obtainable within a single chapter window.</p>
<p>This guide covers 4 categories of missables:</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Collectible locks</strong>: Mr. Raccoon dolls, antique coins, files, weapons inaccessible after chapter end</li>
 <li><strong>One-time events</strong>: NPC dialogues, environmental interactions, cutscene variants</li>
 <li><strong>Trophy locks</strong>: Trophies that can only be earned within specific chapters</li>
 <li><strong>Weapon/tool locks</strong>: Limited pickups only available during one chapter</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="critical">Critical 7; never miss these</h2>
<div>⚠ <strong>Never miss these 7 items</strong>, missing any of them blocks platinum trophy completion until NG+:</div>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Safes #1-#4 (Care Center)</strong> — Open all four before entering the Courtyard. <a href="/all-5-safe-codes-guide/">Safe codes guide</a></li>
 <li><strong>Eye Spy Charm</strong>. Reward from Chunk #1 (Care Center East Wing 2F). Lifesaver on Insanity.</li>
 <li><strong>Mortal Edge Hatchet</strong>; Reward from The Commander (ARK interior). Essentially required for Victor Gideon.</li>
 <li><strong>Marksman 1A Rifle</strong> — Back room of Hank's Pawn (Raccoon City North district). Critical for Plant 43 + Victor Gideon.</li>
 <li><strong>ARK Keycard</strong>. Raccoon City West district train station. Required to enter ARK Facility.</li>
 <li><strong>Orphanage Keepsake</strong>. Chapter 6 only. Triggers NG+ alternate ending.</li>
 <li><strong>Hemorrhetic Injectors</strong> — One-shot kill key for Chef/Chunk. Stockpile in early chapters.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="ch1">Chapter 1: Wrenwood Hotel</h2>
<p>Relatively safe chapter, but several missables:</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>S&S M232 S&S M232 handgun</strong>; 1F front desk (unmissable)</li>
 <li><strong>Herb pouches</strong>. 2F Manager's Office — missed if you skip the office</li>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #1</strong>; 3F suite room ceiling vent (requires explosive damage from grenade or RPG)</li>
 <li><strong>Rooftop cutscene variant</strong>; Wait 5 seconds on the roof for an extra NPC line</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="ch2">Chapter 2: Care Center (most missable-rich chapter)</h2>
<p>The Care Center is the campaign's first major sandbox and contains the most missables of any chapter. See <a href="/care-center-walkthrough/">Care Center walkthrough</a> for full step-by-step routing.</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Safe #1 (Bar &amp; Lounge / Café)</strong> — Reward: 3× antique coins</li>
 <li><strong>Safe #2 (Examination Room)</strong>, Reward: Requiem Revolver ammo</li>
 <li><strong>The Chef boss (optional)</strong>; Killing him drops Pantry Key + "First Blood" trophy</li>
 <li><strong>Chunk #1 Thomas (East Wing 2F)</strong> — Killing him drops Eye Spy Charm (Insanity-critical)</li>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #2-#5</strong>, Scattered across the Care Center</li>
 <li><strong>Antique coins #1-#4</strong></li>
 <li><strong>Files #1-#8</strong></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="ch3">Chapter 3: Care Center Basement</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Safe #3 (Furnace Room)</strong> + <strong>Safe #4 (Sterilization Chamber / Lab Safe)</strong></li>
 <li><strong>Chunk #2 Timothy (mandatory)</strong>; Reward: MSBG 500 W870 Police shotgun upgrade</li>
 <li><strong>Quartz Pieces ×2</strong> — Required for Courtyard fountain puzzle</li>
 <li><strong>Artificial Heart + Lungs</strong>. Organless corpse puzzle</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="ch4">Chapter 4: Courtyard</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Blister-borne boss (mandatory)</strong>; Reward: Mortal Edge Hatchet upgrade</li>
 <li><strong>Courtyard fountain Quartz puzzle</strong> — Eye Spy Charm refill + antique coin</li>
 <li><strong>Chapel save room</strong>, Only save point</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="ch5">Chapter 5: Water Treatment Plant</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>The Girl boss (mandatory)</strong>; Reward: Sunlight Lens</li>
 <li><strong>3 sunlight units restart order</strong> — Fixed sequence (A → C → B)</li>
 <li><strong>Valve codes</strong>, Normal (A=3 B=7 C=5), Insanity (A=4 B=2 C=8)</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="ch6">Chapter 6: RPD &amp; Orphanage</h2>
<div>💡 <strong>Most missable-dense chapter</strong>; once Mr. X spawns, no backtracking is possible.</div>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Kendo Custom S&S M232 S&S M232 handgun</strong> — Kendo Gun Shop (2 blocks east of RPD). Significantly stronger sidearm.</li>
 <li><strong>Safe #5 (Monitor Control Room / Observation Room)</strong>. Open before Mr. X spawns</li>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #11</strong>; Kendo Gun Shop sign (shoot from outside)</li>
 <li><strong>Orphanage Keepsake</strong> — Headmistress' office. Triggers NG+ alternate ending.</li>
 <li><strong>Orphanage files ×3</strong>; Narrative connectors back to Wrenwood Hotel intro</li>
 <li><strong>Memorial trophy</strong>. Orphanage only</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="ch7">Chapter 7: Raccoon City Square</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Marksman 1A Rifle</strong> — Hank's Pawn back room (North district)</li>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #15-#17</strong>; Scattered across all 4 districts</li>
 <li><strong>ARK Keycard</strong>, Train station locomotive (West district) — mandatory</li>
 <li><strong>Final Quartz Piece</strong>. Mall escalator pit (South district)</li>
 <li><strong>Safe #5 alternate location</strong>; Bonus content if all 4 prior safes were opened</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="ch8">Chapter 8: ARK Facility</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #22-#24</strong> — Lab levels (#23 is the most missable; on the ARK Director's desk)</li>
 <li><strong>Final antique coins ×4</strong>, Hidden inside lab equipment</li>
 <li><strong>NG+ RNA puzzle</strong> — NG+ only (Director's office). see <a href="/ng-plus-final-puzzle-rna-solution/">RNA puzzle guide</a></li>
 <li><strong>Alternate ending</strong>, Requires both Orphanage Keepsake + correct RNA puzzle answer</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="trophy">Missable trophies</h2>
<p>These trophies can only be earned within specific chapters. Missing them requires NG+ or (when unlocked) chapter select:</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>First Blood (Bronze)</strong> — Defeat The Chef in Chapter 2</li>
 <li><strong>Locked &amp; Loaded (Bronze)</strong>; Defeat Chunk #1 in Chapter 2</li>
 <li><strong>Memorial (Silver)</strong>; Collect Orphanage Keepsake in Chapter 6</li>
 <li><strong>Open-Air Surgeon (Bronze)</strong> — 4-cyst Blister-borne kill in Chapter 4</li>
 <li><strong>Anatomy 101 (Bronze)</strong>. Complete organless corpse puzzle in Chapter 3</li>
 <li><strong>Light of Day (Silver)</strong>. Defeat The Girl in Chapter 5</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="tips">Tips to never miss anything</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Save in a new slot at the start of every chapter</strong> — gives you a backtrack point if you realize you missed something</li>
 <li><strong>Check the chapter checklist on this page</strong> before triggering any chapter-transition cutscene (keep this page open on mobile)</li>
 <li><strong>Insanity-run players: follow this guide religiously</strong>; Insanity's chapter-select unlocks are inconvenient</li>
 <li><strong>NG+ can recover most misses</strong>; but trophies count per-playthrough, so plan accordingly</li>
 <li><strong>Microsoft Clarity data from our user base shows</strong> the highest 100%-completion failure rate is in Chapters 2 and 6 — be extra thorough in those two</li>
</ol>
