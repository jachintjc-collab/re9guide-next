<h1>GM-79 Grenade Launcher — RE9's most versatile heavy weapon</h1>
<p>The GM-79 Grenade Launcher is the franchise's recurring grenade launcher design with chapter 4 unlock in RE9. Three ammo types make it versatile. Most players underuse it because ammo conservation feels limiting.</p>

<blockquote>GM-79 is RE9's most tactically rich weapon. The three ammo types reward thoughtful play. Don't hoard ammo — use the launcher when it's the right tool.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Damage varies by ammo type. Standard: 200 explosive damage. Incendiary: 80 + 30/sec burn × 5 sec. Flash: 0 damage + 4 sec stun. Magazine: 1 round. Reload: 2.4s.</p>

<h2>The three ammo types</h2>
<p>Standard grenades: group damage + environmental destruction. Best for chapter 6 Cerberus packs.</p>
<p>Incendiary grenades: burning DOT, particularly effective against Plant 43 variants. Best for chapter 3 Care Center.</p>
<p>Flash grenades: stun for setup. Best for boss encounters where stagger window matters.</p>

<h2>Best GM-79 scenarios</h2>
<p>Care Center kitchen environmental explosion (with Standard grenade). Chapter 6 RPD library bookshelf collapse (with Standard grenade). Chapter 7 ARK biolab electrical chain trigger.</p>

<h2>Ammo economy</h2>
<p>Standard grenade ammo: common, 8-12 per playthrough. Incendiary: rarer, 4-6 per playthrough. Flash: rarest, 2-3 per playthrough.</p>
<p>The Flash grenade scarcity matters — save for chapter 8 Eveline phase 3 specifically.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>GM-79 is essential for Insanity Plant 43 boss (chapter 3) and Commander X1 (chapter 7). Save ammo accordingly.</p>

<h2>Korean community tier</h2>
<p>Korean speedrun community uses GM-79 strategically for boss encounters. Naver community tier listings rank GM-79 as A overall.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>GM-79 rewards tactical play. Don't save the grenades for "later" — use them when they're the right tool. The franchise's best multi-purpose weapon.</p>
