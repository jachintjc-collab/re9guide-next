<h1>Secret Ending — unlock the hidden ninth ending</h1>
<p>Beyond the 8 standard endings, RE9 has a secret ninth ending unlocked through extremely specific conditions. Less than 1% of players discover it without a guide. The ending is brief but lore-significant.</p>

<blockquote>The Secret Ending is RE9's deepest hidden content. Pursue if you've already done the 8 endings and want more. The unlock conditions are obscure even by RE standards.</blockquote>

<h2>The unlock conditions</h2>
<p>Complete the game on Insanity NG+. Read all 89 documents AND all 47 audio logs AND find all 18 treasures on the same save. Complete the True Ending (Grace variant or Leon variant). After credits, return to chapter 1 in NG+++ mode (the second NG+) and visit the Wrenwood church confessional.</p>
<p>The confessional dialogue option "I've seen everything" only appears if all prior conditions are met. Selecting it triggers the secret ending sequence.</p>

<h2>The Secret Ending content</h2>
<p>Approximately five minutes of unique content. Father Riley appears in the confessional but is revealed to be an Eveline-mold projection (his physical death occurred between chapters 5 and 8). The conversation reframes Father Riley's entire role in the game.</p>
<p>The ending suggests Eveline's mold network has been infiltrating broader regions than RE9's plot established. This is significant RE10 setup.</p>

<h2>Why this is RE9's hidden content peak</h2>
<p>The Father Riley reveal recontextualizes the chapter 5 confrontation. His confession about confessional secrets carries different weight if his physical body was already mold-controlled at that point.</p>
<p>The ending suggests RE10 will feature extended Eveline mold network rather than fresh pathogen plot. Korean fan theory aligns with this read.</p>

<h2>Discovery rate</h2>
<p>Capcom telemetry at GDC 2026: 0.4% of total players discovered the Secret Ending without a guide. After guide publication on Naver (March 2026), discovery rate climbed to about 8%.</p>

<h2>Korean community on the Secret Ending</h2>
<p>The Secret Ending was first documented by Korean Naver RE9 community in March 2026. The Korean coverage remains the most detailed analysis of the implications. The Korean RE10 fan theory community has built extensive speculation around the secret ending's Eveline network implications.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>The Secret Ending is for true completionists who've done everything else. Don't pursue without completing all other major trophies first. The unlock conditions require essentially full game mastery. The narrative reward is real but earned through extensive engagement.</p>
