<h1>Worst RE9 puzzles — the quartz puzzle specifically</h1>
<p>RE9 has 17 puzzles. Sixteen are well-designed. The Rhodes Hill quartz puzzle is the franchise's worst puzzle in recent memory.</p>

<blockquote>Rhodes Hill quartz puzzle is genuinely bad design. Pixel-precise interaction that controller can't reliably achieve. Universal player hatred is earned.</blockquote>

<h2>Why the quartz puzzle fails</h2><p>Requires clicking specific pixels on dim paintings. Click hitbox is small. Controller can't reliably hit the target. PC mouse precision insufficient at default settings.</p><h2>What good puzzle design looks like</h2><p>Compare to ARK core mosaic puzzle. Spatial logic, reasonable hitbox, satisfying solve. Same designer, opposite outcome.</p><h2>The patch v1.02 attempt</h2><p>v1.02 slightly expanded the click hitbox. Insufficient improvement. Players still struggle.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>The quartz puzzle should be patched in v1.03 with major hitbox expansion. Or the puzzle should be redesigned entirely. RE10 should hire a usability tester.</p>
