<p><strong>The Raccoon Police Department is one of gaming&rsquo;s most iconic locations.</strong> First appeared in RE2 (1998). Aged 28 years in RE9 (2026). Every architectural detail preserved. Every bloodstain accounted for. This is the complete RPD timeline.</p>

<h2>Complete chronological timeline</h2>
<div>
 <table>
 <tr><th>When</th><th>Event</th></tr>
 <tr><td><strong>1996</strong></td><td>RPD building constructed. Originally an art museum before conversion to police station. Goddess statue retained as decorative centerpiece.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>September 1998 (RE2 Remake events)</strong></td><td>T-Virus outbreak in Raccoon City. RPD overrun within 48 hours. Officer Leon Kennedy reports for first day on duty. Mr. X arrives via helicopter.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>October 1998</strong></td><td>Raccoon City Destruction Incident. RPD building partially damaged but structure intact. Surviving officers evacuate.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>1999-2005</strong></td><td>RPD officially abandoned. US government cordons off the building. Some classified research extracted from evidence rooms.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2005-2020</strong></td><td>Building deteriorates. Time-weathered architecture. Government property. no civilian access. Goddess statue remains in main hall.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2020-2023</strong></td><td>Documentary crews granted limited access. Several photos and footage leaked publicly. Foundation of RE9&rsquo;s visual recreation.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2023</strong></td><td>Capcom begins RE9 development. Director Nakanishi visits the real abandoned location for reference. Pixel-for-pixel recreation begins.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>February 2026 (RE9 events)</strong></td><td>Leon Kennedy returns to RPD. Same fountain. Same Goddess statue. Same staircase. Mr. X transformation cutscene plays in main hall. Bloodstains from 1998 still visible.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>RPD as character</strong></td><td>In RE9, the RPD itself functions as a character. The main hall is a 10-minute walkable space. Capcom didn&rsquo;t need to do this. They did it anyway.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Officer Leon Kennedy 1998 documents</strong></td><td>Hidden in RE9 RPD basement: paperwork dated September 29, 1998. Time-locked easter egg. Leon&rsquo;s original first-day report visible.</td></tr>
