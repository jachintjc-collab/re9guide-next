<p><strong>ARK Facility is where RE9 ends.</strong> Founded 2014 under Connections sponsorship. Became Plant 43&rsquo;s research home. Site of Marcus Halverson&rsquo;s transformation into Chunk. Final destination for Grace and Leon. This is the 12-year complete history.</p>
<h2>Complete chronological breakdown</h2>
<div><table><tr><th>When</th><th>Event</th></tr><tr><td><strong>2014 founding</strong></td><td>ARK Facility (Advanced Research Knowledge) founded under Connections sponsorship. Location: classified, but later revealed as USA East Coast underground complex.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2014-2017 mandate</strong></td><td>Initial research: Eveline E-virus mold studies. Vivien Ashcroft (still alive) influential in early protocols.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2018 Plant 43 development</strong></td><td>Plant 43 synthesized from modified E-virus strain. Originally intended for therapeutic applications.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2020 The Commander arrives</strong></td><td>Major Richard Vance assigned as facility operations leader. Pivots research toward weaponization.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2023 Marcus Halverson tragedy</strong></td><td>Marcus&rsquo;s daughter Lily diagnosed with leukemia. He proposes Plant 43 therapeutic research. Rejected by Vance.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2024 Marcus transformation begins</strong></td><td>Marcus self-administers Plant 43 culture. Becomes &ldquo;subject C-7.&rdquo; 14-month observation period.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2025 Marcus = Chunk completion</strong></td><td>Marcus&rsquo;s transformation complete. Designated &ldquo;Chunk&rdquo; by facility. Cover-up of original identity begins.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2025 Vivien&rsquo;s research breakthrough</strong></td><td>Vivien&rsquo;s sealed 2009 research is reviewed. Pattern matches Eveline reappearances. Decision made to send Grace into Rhodes Hill.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>February 2026 RE9 events</strong></td><td>Grace + Leon reach ARK Facility. Defeat Plant 43, The Commander, Chunk Final, Victor Gideon. Vivien&rsquo;s Lab reveals everything.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Post-RE9 status</strong></td><td>ARK Facility destroyed during finale. Connections leadership goes dark. Many records survive in Vivien&rsquo;s lab.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>RE10 implications</strong></td><td>Director Nakanishi confirmed ARK Facility may be referenced in RE10. Some surviving researchers may form opposition organization.</td></tr></table></div>
