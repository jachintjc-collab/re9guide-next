<h1>Corporate villains — institutional antagonists across RE</h1>
<p>The corporate institutional antagonists across the RE franchise. Spencer, Tricell, the Family, and the BSAA-internal hostile faction implied by RE9.</p>

<blockquote>RE's corporate villains have evolved from single-villain Umbrella to networks of institutional corruption. The BSAA faction is the next stage of this evolution.</blockquote>

Lord Spencer — original Umbrella founder, deceased post-RE5. Tricell — secondary pharmaceutical corporation, dismantled post-RE5. The Family — covert organization, last referenced RE6. The BSAA hostile faction — internal to BSAA, members specifically named in the chapter 5 Father Riley letter. The hostile faction is the central RE10 antagonist set up by RE9's true ending.
