<h1>Worst RE9 chapter pacing — chapter 4 specifically</h1>
<p>RE9's chapter pacing is mostly strong. Chapter 4's Wrenwood-to-Rhodes-Hill traverse is the franchise's worst paced chapter in years.</p>

<blockquote>Chapter 4 is RE9's pacing disaster. Most players speedrun through. The chapter could be cut by 30% with no narrative loss.</blockquote>

<h2>What chapter 4 does wrong</h2><p>Excessive optional content with no clear gameplay reward. Sparse enemy encounters that don't justify the corridor length. Cutscene density too high for traverse chapter.</p><h2>Why it happened</h2><p>Per Nakanishi at GDC 2026: chapter 4 was reworked late in development as Wrenwood-Rhodes Hill connector. Original chapter was cut entirely. The replacement is padding.</p><h2>What should have happened</h2><p>Cut chapter 4 to 25 minutes of focused traverse. Move Pacifist trophy mechanics to chapter 2.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Chapter 4 is the franchise's worst-paced chapter since RE6 chapter 2. Skip cutscenes on NG+. Hope RE10 handles transitions better.</p>
