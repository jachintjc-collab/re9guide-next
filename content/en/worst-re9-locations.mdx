<h1>Worst RE9 locations — which areas underwhelm</h1>
<p>RE9 has four major areas. Three are excellent. Chapter 7-8 ARK sub-level 2 (the pathogen storage area) is the franchise's most-tedious environment in years.</p>

<blockquote>ARK sub-level 2 is RE9's worst environment. Repetitive corridors, low encounter variety, asset reuse that breaks immersion.</blockquote>

<h2>ARK sub-level 2 problems</h2><p>Six near-identical corridor segments. Same wall texture across 60% of the floor. Three enemy encounters across 30 minutes of gameplay.</p><h2>Why it happened</h2><p>Per development discussion at GDC 2026: ARK sub-level 2 was scope-cut late in development. Original design had distinct sub-areas; final version compressed them.</p><h2>What works in ARK</h2><p>Upper levels (vestibule, reception) are excellent. Core boss area is strong. Sub-level 1 administrative wing works.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>ARK sub-level 2 is the franchise's worst-paced environment in 5 years. Skip exploration; speedrun through.</p>
