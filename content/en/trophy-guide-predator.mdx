<h1>Predator — kill 100 enemies with knife only</h1>
<p>Kill 100 enemies using only the knife (or Mortal Edge knife variant). Firearm kills don't count. The 100-count is per-save. Estimated 8-15 hours of focused knife play to accumulate.</p>

<blockquote>Predator rewards knife-combat investment. Mortal Edge knife makes this approachable. Standard knife on Insanity is the harder version.</blockquote>

<h2>Knife-only kill mechanics</h2>
<p>Knife attacks: light (square), heavy (triangle), parry (L2 or L1 depending on custom mapping). All three contribute to knife kills.</p>
<p>Mortal Edge knife (unlocked after Insanity completion): same controls, more damage. Recommended for Predator pursuit.</p>
<p>Doesn't count: firearm follow-up that kills after knife damage. Only the kill blow counts.</p>

<h2>Easiest enemies for knife kills</h2>
<p>Standard zombies: 2-4 knife hits to kill. The most efficient knife target.</p>
<p>Blister Borne (Care Center hybrids): 4-6 hits but vulnerable to parry-counter. Strong knife-XP target.</p>
<p>Cerberus: 3-5 hits, mobile, requires positioning. Risky but efficient.</p>

<h2>Avoid knife-killing</h2>
<p>Lickers: too dangerous in melee. Skip with firearms.</p>
<p>Plant 43: melee range is the kill zone for the plant. Use firearms.</p>
<p>Bosses: knife damage is reduced significantly. Boss kills with knife are extremely time-consuming.</p>

<h2>Insanity Predator</h2>
<p>Insanity multiplies knife kill challenge. Standard zombies require 4-7 hits on Insanity. Predator on Insanity is the franchise's most demanding knife-skill challenge. Estimated 25+ hours dedicated knife play.</p>
<p>Mortal Edge knife essential on Insanity. Standard knife is borderline unviable for Predator-scale numbers.</p>

<h2>Korean knife-only community</h2>
<p>Korean speedrun community has a knife-only category for RE9 Insanity. The category requires Predator-level engagement plus several other knife-only restrictions. The Korean Naver thread on knife-only Insanity is one of the most-active RE9 challenge threads.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Pursue Predator on Standard NG+ first. Mortal Edge knife + Standard difficulty = manageable grind. Save Insanity knife pursuit for after you've completed the Insanity trophy line separately.</p>
