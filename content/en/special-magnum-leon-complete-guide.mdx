<h1>Special Magnum (Leon's variant) — series callback weapon</h1>
<p>Special Magnum is Leon's signature weapon from RE4. RE9 includes it as unlockable through specific Leon-cooperation dialogue choices. Mechanically distinct from base Magnum class.</p>

<blockquote>Special Magnum is fan service done well. The mechanical distinction from generic Magnum class makes it actually different to use, not just visually different.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Base damage: 145. Headshot multiplier: 2.9x. Magazine: 5 rounds. Reload speed: 2.6s. Recoil: high (the trade-off for damage).</p>

<h2>Unlock conditions</h2>
<p>Choose Leon-cooperation dialogue option at chapter 4 ("Yes, send backup"). Then choose Leon-trust option at chapter 7 ("Trust Leon's judgment"). Both required.</p>

<h2>The recoil consideration</h2>
<p>Special Magnum recoil is significantly higher than other handguns. Single-shot accuracy is good; follow-up shot accuracy is poor. Plan combat around single-shot effectiveness.</p>

<h2>RE4 callback design</h2>
<p>Special Magnum's animation and sound design directly references the RE4 Punisher pistol. Capcom's audio team specifically recreated the firing sound from RE4 (2005 original, not the 2023 remake).</p>

<h2>Best use cases</h2>
<p>Single Boss damage windows. Critical headshot setups. Cerberus single-target stops.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>Special Magnum on Insanity is reliable for single-shot scenarios. Don't try to follow up with second shots — accept the kill and reload.</p>

<h2>Korean community tier</h2>
<p>Korean RE veterans rate Special Magnum highly for nostalgia value. Mechanical tier is A-/B+. The Korean tier listing balances nostalgia and stats.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Special Magnum is the franchise's best Leon-callback weapon. Use it if you went through the dialogue path. The single-shot effectiveness makes it competitive without being broken.</p>
