<h2>How this calculator was built</h2>
<p>Loadout recommendations cross-reference every boss-specific strategy guide on this site, plus the All Bosses Ranked tier list. Each weapon-to-boss recommendation has been verified through actual playthroughs on PS5 and Steam, with Insanity loadouts re-tested after the v1.02 patch verification pass (see <a href="capcom-v1-02-patch-diary.html">v1.02 Patch Diary</a>).</p>
<p>The Insanity loadouts assume you have access to Infinite Ammo from the Special Content Store (post-clear unlock). If you're attempting Insanity without Infinite Ammo as a personal challenge, expect each boss to take 2-3x longer and require 50% more consumables.</p>
