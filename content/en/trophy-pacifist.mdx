<h1>Pacifist — complete chapter 4 with zero enemy kills</h1>
<p>Complete the chapter 4 Wrenwood-to-Rhodes-Hill traverse sequence without killing any enemies. Knockback, stagger, and avoidance allowed. Kills disqualify.</p>

<blockquote>Pacifist is RE9's smartest restriction trophy. Chapter 4 is designed for it — multiple stealth and avoidance options exist. Rewards exploration.</blockquote>

<h2>What counts as kill</h2>
<p>Any enemy death attributed to Grace's actions. Damage that reduces enemy HP to zero. Environmental damage you trigger that kills enemies.</p>
<p>Enemies killed by other enemies (e.g., zombie-vs-Cerberus conflicts you observed but didn't initiate) don't count against Pacifist.</p>

<h2>Chapter 4 encounter avoidance options</h2>
<p>Forest path zombie encounters: detour through woods. Loses some collectibles but bypasses combat.</p>
<p>Stable Cerberus pair: hide in barn loft until they patrol away.</p>
<p>Manor approach Blister Borne: time the approach during their patrol gap.</p>
<p>Manor entry guard: distract with thrown bottle (non-damaging), enter while distracted.</p>

<h2>Required preparation</h2>
<p>Stealth gear loadout. Quickstep Charm essential. Quiet boots equipped.</p>
<p>Save scum aggressively. Pacifist requires near-perfect avoidance execution. Save before each encounter.</p>

<h2>Insanity Pacifist</h2>
<p>Insanity adds enemy density and shorter patrol windows. Pacifist Insanity is significantly harder than Standard. Some encounters become near-impossible to avoid without timed glitch use.</p>

<h2>Korean Pacifist community</h2>
<p>Korean Naver community has documented Pacifist routing through chapter 4 with specific timing windows. The Korean route includes one specific Korean-discovered shortcut that isn't in English-language guides.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Pacifist is one of RE9's most enjoyable restriction trophies. Rewards careful play. Save scum aggressively. The trophy is achievable for patient players who enjoy stealth gameplay over combat.</p>
