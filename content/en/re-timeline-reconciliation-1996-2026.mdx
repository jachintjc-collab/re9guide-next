<h2>Pre-Raccoon era — 1960s through 1996</h2>
<p>1960s: Lord Oswell E. Spencer founds Umbrella Corporation. James Marcus joins Umbrella as research director.</p>
<p>1967-1979: Project W operates. Approximately 15-30 children undergo prenatal genetic modification + Progenitor-derivative exposure. Albert Wesker is the most successful surviving subject.</p>
<p>1978-1988: James Marcus develops T-virus prototype at Arklay facility through systematic human subject testing. Marcus is killed by Spencer and Wesker in 1988.</p>
<p>1988-1996: William Birkin develops G-virus from T-virus base. Annette Birkin co-leads. Sherry Birkin born 1991.</p>
<p>1996: Spencer Mansion incidents (RE0 / RE Zero). Bravo Team encounters Marcus's revenge mechanism. Alpha Team responds. Outbreak contained through facility destruction.</p>

<h2>The Raccoon City era — 1998</h2>
<p>September 1998: Raccoon City T-virus outbreak begins. Initial vector through Birkin laboratory containment failure.</p>
<p>Early September 1998: Leon Kennedy's first day as RPD officer (RE2 events). Claire Redfield's search for Chris coincides. Sherry Birkin escapes with help from Leon and Claire.</p>
<p>Late September 1998: Jill Valentine and Carlos Oliveira evacuate (RE3 events). Marvin Branagh dies at RPD reception desk. Nemesis pursuit continues.</p>
<p>September 30, 1998: US government nuclear destruction of Raccoon City. Death toll approximately 100,000. Marcus Bridges (Grace Ashcroft's brother, age 18) is among confirmed dead per public record. Actual cause of death is later revealed to be Vivien Davies's research, not the Raccoon City incident — BSAA cover-up.</p>

<h2>The Code Veronica era — December 1998 to February 1999</h2>
<p>December 1998: Claire's pursuit of Umbrella leads to Antarctic facility (RE Code Veronica). Wesker reappears post-mansion presumed death. Alfred and Alexia Ashford encountered.</p>
<p>February 1999: Antarctic facility destroyed. T-Veronica virus contained. Wesker disappears with research samples.</p>

<h2>The post-Umbrella collapse — 2003-2004</h2>
<p>2003: Umbrella Corporation formally collapses following financial and legal consequences of Raccoon City liability.</p>
<p>2003-2004: BSAA (Bioterrorism Security Assessment Alliance) founded by international consortium of governments and post-Umbrella corporate interests. Chris Redfield and Jill Valentine among founding agents.</p>
<p>2004-2005: ARK Facility (Pennsylvania, originally 1979 federal continuity-of-government site) transferred to BSAA jurisdiction. Begins serving as central pathogen sample archive.</p>

<h2>RE4 and Las Plagas era — 2004-2005</h2>
<p>2004: Leon Kennedy's Spanish operation (RE4 events). President Graham's daughter rescue. Las Plagas exposed. Saddler defeated. Ada Wong's involvement begins.</p>
<p>2004-2010: Las Plagas variants studied by various organizations. Master Plagas integration research begins (eventually leading to RE9 Commander X1).</p>

<h2>The Revelations era — 2011-2014</h2>
<p>2011: RE Revelations 1 events. Veltro terrorism. T-Abyss virus containment by Jill Valentine and Parker Luciani aboard Queen Zenobia.</p>
<p>2013: RE6 events. C-virus outbreak across multiple regions (Eastern Europe, China, North America). Carla Radames as Family agent. Multiple BSAA operations.</p>
<p>2014: RE Revelations 2 events. Claire Redfield's TerraSave operation in isolated island context. Alex Wesker encountered.</p>

<h2>The Beneviento era — 2014-2017</h2>
<p>2014: Connections Inc Project Beneviento operates. Eveline engineered using Mia Winters's biological material. Containment failure during transport leads to Eveline's contact with the Baker family in Louisiana.</p>
<p>2017: RE7 events. Ethan Winters arrives at the Baker farmhouse. Eveline confronted and destroyed. Mia Winters survives. BSAA arrives and recovers samples.</p>
<p>2017 (immediate post-RE7): BSAA places Mia Winters in cryostorage at ARK Facility for E-pathogen exposure containment. The placement is preventive — Mia is potentially compromised but not actively infected.</p>

<h2>RE Village era — 2021</h2>
<p>2021: RE Village events. Ethan Winters pursues kidnapped daughter Rose to Romanian village. Connections Inc Beneviento research expanded into European operations. Ethan dies (canonical outcome left ambiguous for RE10).</p>
<p>2021-2026: Vivien Davies's research at Care Center continues. BSAA shell corporation funding expands. Master Plagas integration program continues development.</p>

<h2>The Care Center buildup — 2022-2026</h2>
<p>2022: Vivien's husband dies of untreatable genetic condition. Her research focus shifts toward addressing similar conditions.</p>
<p>2023-2025: Vivien's research program produces seventeen successful gene therapy treatments. Safety violations accumulate. BSAA shell funding expands.</p>
<p>2025 (Spring): Three BSAA contract investigators sent to investigate Wrenwood. All three die under suspicious circumstances. Wrenwood local authorities cover up the deaths.</p>
<p>2026 February 27: Resident Evil: Requiem game timeline begins. Grace Ashcroft, contract investigator (47th BSAA contract), arrives at Wrenwood.</p>
