<p><strong>The Connections is the shadow organization behind everything in RE7, RE8, and RE9.</strong> Founded 1995 to fill the post-Umbrella vacuum. Created Eveline. Sponsored ARK Facility. Marcus Halverson&rsquo;s employer. This is the complete decode.</p>
<h2>Complete chronological breakdown</h2>
<div><table><tr><th>When</th><th>Event</th></tr><tr><td><strong>1995 founding</strong></td><td>The Connections founded as Umbrella Corporation reorganizes post-1998 Raccoon City. Former Umbrella scientists join. Shadow biotech focus.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>1998-2005</strong></td><td>Post-RE2 era. Connections acquires Umbrella research. Begins E-virus project at hidden facilities. Recruits military intelligence assets.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2006-2009 Eveline project</strong></td><td>Connections develops Eveline as mold-virus chimera. Vivien Ashcroft lead researcher. Eveline begins humanization arc.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2014 ARK Facility commissioned</strong></td><td>Connections sponsors ARK Facility construction. Hidden US East Coast location. Plant 43 research begins.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2017 RE7 incident</strong></td><td>Eveline tanker mission goes wrong. Mia Winters compromised. Ethan defeats Eveline. Connections covers up loss.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2020 The Commander Vance arrives</strong></td><td>Major Richard Vance assigned to ARK Facility leadership. Marks Connections&rsquo; militarization phase.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2023 Marcus tragedy</strong></td><td>Marcus Halverson&rsquo;s daughter dies. Marcus self-experiments with Plant 43. Connections allows it for &ldquo;research advancement.&rdquo;</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2024 Ada Wong defection</strong></td><td>Ada Wong leaves Connections after discovering Marcus cover-up. Goes underground. Begins covert investigation.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2026 RE9 events</strong></td><td>Grace defeats Connections-aligned bosses (Victor Gideon, Chunk, The Commander). ARK Facility destroyed. Major blow to Connections operations.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Post-RE9 status</strong></td><td>Connections leadership goes dark. Speculation: organization fractured, some splinter cells operating. RE10 will likely show consequences.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Director quote</strong></td><td>Famitsu interview (May 2026): &ldquo;The Connections is the new Umbrella. They&rsquo;ll persist beyond RE9 because the franchise needs a continuing antagonist organization.&rdquo;</td></tr></table></div>
