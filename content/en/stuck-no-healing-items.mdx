<h1>Stuck: no healing items — when supply has dried up</h1>
<p>Healing item supply can dry up if players neglect crafting or overuse. Specific recovery strategies.</p>

<blockquote>No healing items means you used herbs without crafting back stock. The herb system is the bottleneck. Solve by herb hunting.</blockquote>

<h2>Immediate recovery</h2><p>Search current chapter for any missed herbs. Herbs respawn in some locations across NG+ (not within single save).</p><h2>Crafting strategy</h2><p>Combine two Green Herbs for 1 Strong Heal. More efficient HP per resource than using individually.</p><h2>What to ration</h2><p>Save Strong Heals for boss encounters. Use Green singles for between-encounter recovery.</p><h2>If genuinely out</h2><p>Use safe room recovery (slow regenerate). Equip Endurance charm to reduce damage taken.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Herb hunting is RE's classic survival loop. Embrace it. Insanity especially rewards thorough herb collection. Don't skip rooms.</p>
