<h1>Baker family — RE7's central infected family + RE9 codex references</h1>
<p>Jack, Marguerite, Lucas, and Zoe Baker — Eveline's first infected family in RE7. RE9 background terminals provide updated context on what happened post-RE7 to the Baker remains.</p>

<blockquote>The Baker family is RE's most tragic infected unit. RE9's codex closure on their fate is the franchise's quietest grief moment.</blockquote>

The Baker family operated a Louisiana plantation home in 2014 when Eveline arrived. The infection cascade across the family was within 8 weeks. RE7 ended with Jack and Marguerite dead, Lucas at large, Zoe rescued by Ethan or Mia depending on RE7 ending route. RE9 codex updates: Lucas was apprehended by BSAA in 2019 and died in cryostorage 2022. Zoe was placed in BSAA protective custody and remains there.
