<h1>Ending Master — see all 8 endings</h1>
<p>All 8 endings unlocked. Each ending requires specific gameplay decisions, dialogue choices, or chapter completion order. Eight playthroughs minimum unless you save-scum the final chapter for ending variants.</p>

<blockquote>Ending Master is the trophy that rewards understanding RE9's branching system. Some endings are easy to miss without knowing the choice triggers. Plan systematically.</blockquote>

<h2>The 8 endings + trigger conditions</h2>
<p>Standard ending: default completion, no specific conditions.</p>
<p>True ending: read chapter 6 archives + chapter 8 third dialogue option (covered in trophy-grace-true-ending).</p>
<p>Grace-solo ending: complete chapters 1-8 without accepting Leon's chapter 4 backup offer.</p>
<p>Leon-rescue ending: complete with maximum Leon-cooperation dialogue choices.</p>
<p>Vivien-survival ending: rare branch — requires specific chapter 3 dialogue showing sympathy + don't trigger the boss fight (use the bypass route).</p>
<p>Father Riley-confession ending: complete chapter 5 Father Riley confrontation with the second dialogue option ("Don't break the seal").</p>
<p>Mia-departure ending: chapter 7 Mia cameo with specific dialogue that has Mia leave separately from Grace.</p>
<p>BSAA-cover-up ending: chapter 8 final decision to suppress Vivien's research records instead of publishing.</p>

<h2>Save-scumming strategy</h2>
<p>Save before the final chapter 8 decision point. Multiple endings (Standard, True, Cover-up) branch from that single decision plus prior dialogue. Save scum the decision for 3-4 endings without full replay.</p>
<p>Other endings (Grace-solo, Vivien-survival, Mia-departure) require full playthrough due to earlier branch points.</p>

<h2>Time investment estimate</h2>
<p>Four full playthroughs (Standard, plus three early-branch endings) covers all 8 with save scumming. Estimated 60-80 hours total.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>Endings are not difficulty-locked. Pursue all 8 on Standard for efficiency. Insanity completion doesn't unlock additional endings.</p>
