<h1>Match Pistol — Leon's gift, the franchise's most accurate handgun</h1>
<p>The Match Pistol is unlocked in chapter 4 through Leon's storyline. It's modeled on a real-world competition pistol (the Pardini SP1 specifically). The accuracy bonus is significant. Underused because most players don't realize how good it is.</p>

<blockquote>Match Pistol is the most underrated weapon in RE9. Korean players overrate Hand Cannon and underrate the Match Pistol. That's just my read of forum threads, take it or leave it.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Base damage: 48. Headshot multiplier: 2.8x. Magazine: 8 rounds. Reload speed: 2.0s. Recoil: extremely low (the defining stat).</p>

<h2>The accuracy advantage</h2>
<p>Match Pistol's recoil pattern is roughly 60% tighter than standard handguns. At medium range, headshot reliability is closer to rifle territory than pistol territory.</p>

<h2>The ammo problem</h2>
<p>Match Pistol uses a unique 9mm Match ammunition. Drops are rare. Workshop crafting requires specific components from chapter 5 onward. Ammo scarcity is the weapon's main limitation.</p>

<h2>Best use cases</h2>
<p>Long-corridor zombie encounters where Mortal Edge parry isn't viable. Boss encounters where precision matters more than raw damage. Chapter 6 Mr. X pursuit corner shots.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>On Insanity, the Match Pistol becomes a precision specialist. Use for headshots only; conserve ammo. Pair with shotgun for close-range work.</p>

<h2>Korean community tier</h2>
<p>Korean speedrun community lists Match Pistol as B+/A- depending on the route. Naver community discussion is split — some consider it elite, others consider it limited by ammo economy.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Run a Match Pistol primary build for one playthrough. The headshot reliability changes how you approach combat. Don't dismiss it because of the ammo scarcity.</p>
