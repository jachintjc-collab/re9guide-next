<h2 id="faq">FAQ — RE9 NG+ Carryover</h2>

<p><strong>Q: Do I keep my weapon upgrades in NG+?</strong><br>
A: Yes. All weapons carry over at maxed-out tier-3 stats. You don't need to re-upgrade anything.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Does the Requiem Revolver become Infinite automatically?</strong><br>
A: No. You unlock the Infinite Requiem Revolver charm but must equip it. It uses a charm slot.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Do collectibles like Antique Coins respawn in NG+?</strong><br>
A: No; they do NOT respawn. Once collected in NG, they are gone. NG+ adds 3 new collectibles not present in the first run.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What about my First Aid sprays and ammo?</strong><br>
A: All consumables reset to 0. You start NG+ with your weapons but no ammo. Plan accordingly.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Can I switch difficulty in NG+?</strong><br>
A: Yes. NG+ allows any difficulty choice. Many players go straight to Insanity NG+ for the full experience.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Does NG+ work across PS5 and Steam saves?</strong><br>
A: No. NG+ is per-platform per-save. Cloud sync doesn't bridge platforms.</p>

<p><strong>Q: How many NG+ playthroughs for Platinum?</strong><br>
A: Minimum 3; first Standard NG, then Insanity NG+, then a speedrun. See our <a href="/all-49-trophies-platinum-guide/">Platinum guide</a>.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Will RE10 keep this NG+ system?</strong><br>
A: Director Nakanishi confirmed NG+ is a franchise standard. Specific items unconfirmed.</p>
