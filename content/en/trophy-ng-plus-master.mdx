<h1>NG+ Master — complete NG+ playthrough</h1>
<p>Complete one full NG+ playthrough at any difficulty. NG+ inherits all weapons, charms, and currency from the previous save. Trophy fires at NG+ credits.</p>

<blockquote>NG+ Master is the gateway to most other RE9 trophy pursuits. Complete one NG+ to unlock the harder trophy attempts.</blockquote>

<h2>What unlocks it</h2>
<p>Complete RE9 on any difficulty using the NG+ continuation option from a previous save. The trophy fires at credits regardless of how you played the NG+ run.</p>
<p>NG+ on Casual or Standard is the easiest path. Insanity NG+ is significantly harder and doesn't provide additional trophy benefit for this specific trophy.</p>

<h2>What NG+ inherits</h2>
<p>All weapons. All weapon attachments. All Charms. All currency. All trophies from previous save. Specific story-progress items don't carry (chapter 1 starts fresh).</p>
<p>Documents and audio logs reset — you'll need to re-read for Detective and Lorekeeper trophies on the NG+ save if those weren't completed prior.</p>

<h2>Why pursue NG+ Master</h2>
<p>Many other trophies are NG+-only viable. Speed Demon, True Survivor, knife-only categories, ending alternate routes. NG+ Master is the prerequisite for all of these.</p>

<h2>Insanity NG+</h2>
<p>Inheriting Mortal Edge knife + Hand Cannon makes Insanity NG+ substantially more approachable than first-playthrough Insanity. Recommended Insanity attempt for True Survivor pursuit.</p>

<h2>Korean NG+ community</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 NG+ playthrough guides documented optimal NG+ playthroughs for various trophy pursuits. The Korean Naver coverage of NG+ optimization is more comprehensive than English-language equivalents.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>NG+ Master is essentially a participation trophy for engaged players. Complete any NG+ playthrough; trophy unlocks. Use the playthrough to pursue another trophy simultaneously for efficiency.</p>
