<h1>Headshot Master — 100 headshot kills</h1>
<p>100 enemy kills via headshot. Trophy ticks at the 100th headshot-confirmed kill. Counter is per-save and includes all enemy types except certain bosses with non-head weak points.</p>

<blockquote>Headshot Master is one of the easier RE9 grind trophies. Accumulates naturally with consistent firearm play.</blockquote>

<h2>What counts as headshot</h2>
<p>Direct headshot kill: bullet hits head, enemy dies. Counts.</p>
<p>Headshot stagger followed by body kill: doesn't count. Only direct headshot kill registers.</p>
<p>Headshot that triggers a different enemy state (e.g., zombie crawler from headshot to standing zombie): doesn't immediately count, but if a subsequent shot kills the now-prone enemy with head damage, that counts.</p>

<h2>Best weapons for headshot accumulation</h2>
<p>Marksman's M40 rifle: scope makes headshots reliable. Best dedicated headshot weapon.</p>
<p>S&W M1911: solid headshot stopping power with good ammo economy.</p>
<p>Hand Cannon: overkill but reliable headshot kills on tough enemies.</p>

<h2>Enemy headshot weakness</h2>
<p>Standard zombies: 1 well-placed headshot from any weapon = kill.</p>
<p>Crimson Heads: 2 headshots required.</p>
<p>Blister Borne: head is not the optimal weak point (covered in virology-blister-borne); but headshots still kill, just less efficiently than body shots.</p>
<p>Most other enemy classes have varying head weakness.</p>

<h2>Bosses that don't have head weak points</h2>
<p>Chunk (head is not weak point). Plant 43 (no human-style head). Some Plant 43 variants. Cerberus (head works but small target).</p>

<h2>Insanity Headshot Master</h2>
<p>Insanity enemies have more HP, requiring better placement for one-shot headshot kills. Trophy still achievable but takes more ammunition consumption.</p>

<h2>Korean accuracy community</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 community has documented headshot hitbox sizes for each enemy type. The Korean Naver coverage of headshot reliability per weapon is more detailed than English equivalents.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Headshot Master happens during normal play if you favor precision over volume. Don't grind it specifically. Use the Marksman's M40 when you have the rifle and the trophy will accumulate.</p>
