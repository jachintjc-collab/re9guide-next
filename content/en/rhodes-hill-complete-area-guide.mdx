<h2 id="overview">Overview &amp; Structure</h2>
<p>Rhodes Hill is one of 4 main areas in RE9. The game has no Chapter Select after completion — area progression is strictly linear. You cannot return to this area on a regular save once exited.</p>

<h2 id="weapons">Best weapons for Rhodes Hill</h2>
<p>You acquire the <strong>Requiem Revolver</strong> in Care Center (S-tier weapon). Upgrade to T2 (8,000 currency) before Water Treatment for The Girl boss fight.</p>

<h2 id="insanity-tactics">Insanity-specific tactics</h2>
<p>Insanity Rhodes Hill: The Girl fight is the hardest pre-Chapter 8 fight. Stock 3+ First Aids before entering Water Treatment. Bloodhound charm mandatory for The Girl stealth approach.</p>

<h2 id="faq">FAQ; Rhodes Hill</h2>

<p><strong>Q: Can I return to Rhodes Hill after leaving?</strong><br>
A: Not on Standard play. You can revisit on NG+ with free area selection. Plan collectible runs accordingly.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What is the minimum playthrough time for Rhodes Hill?</strong><br>
A: ~3.5 hours on first playthrough. NG+ speedrun cuts to 60% of that time with Infinite Magnum (Requiem Revolver infinite ammo).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Which charm should I equip for this area?</strong><br>
A: Crafter&rsquo;s Touch + Iron Skin + Phoenix Down (Insanity) OR + Marksman&rsquo;s Eye (Standard).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Are there missable collectibles in Rhodes Hill?</strong><br>
A: Family Photograph in Care Center records is trophy-required and easy to miss. Check filing cabinets thoroughly.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What is the Rhodes Hill hardest moment?</strong><br>
A: The Girl boss at Water Treatment. Insanity-tier difficulty even on Standard. Stealth approach is mandatory.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Does the Rhodes Hill area have RE callbacks?</strong><br>
A: Yes — Vivien&rsquo;s research papers reference Eveline (RE7). The Chef boss in Courtyard is an RE2 kitchen sequence homage.</p>

<p><strong>Q: How much currency is available in Rhodes Hill?</strong><br>
A: Approximately 35,000 total (3 Antique Coins = 30,000 + 5,000 drops).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Is Rhodes Hill different on Insanity NG+?</strong><br>
A: Same layout, but enemies scaled +40% HP, player takes 2x damage, pickup spawn -30%. Infinite Magnum unlock makes Requiem Revolver mid-fight crafting unnecessary.</p>
