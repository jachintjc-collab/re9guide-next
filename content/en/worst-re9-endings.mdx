<h1>Worst RE9 endings — which of the 8 don't earn their inclusion</h1>
<p>RE9 has 8 endings. Five are well-designed. Three are notably weaker variations that don't add narrative value.</p>

<blockquote>RE9's eight-ending count is more than the game needed. Five endings of higher quality would have served the narrative better.</blockquote>

<h2>Father Riley confession ending</h2><p>Weakest ending. Adds nothing the chapter 5 confrontation didn't establish. Player effort to unlock doesn't pay off.</p><h2>BSAA cover-up ending</h2><p>Mechanically interesting (Grace suppresses research) but emotionally hollow. The Sherry Birkin angle works better in true ending.</p><h2>Grace-solo ending</h2><p>Functional but redundant with standard ending narratively.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Cut three weakest endings, focus on five that work. Save Pacifist-related ending mechanic for RE10. The over-count of endings dilutes the strong ones.</p>
