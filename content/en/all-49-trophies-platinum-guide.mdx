<h2 id="overview">Trophy overview</h2>

<p>Resident Evil Requiem features a balanced trophy list with mix of story, combat, collectible, and challenge achievements. Most trophies (~30) unlock naturally through normal play. The remaining 19 require specific actions, collectibles, or difficulty completions.</p>

<p>Key facts every trophy hunter should know:</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Infinite Ammo cheats do NOT void trophies.</strong> The Special Content Store unlocks Infinite Requiem and Infinite RPG after first completion — both are safe to use on Insanity.</li>
 <li><strong>You only need to play through twice.</strong> One run on any difficulty (for collectibles + Insanity unlock) and one Insanity run.</li>
 <li><strong>Several trophies are missable.</strong> See the Missable Warning section below before starting.</li>
 <li><strong>Estimated total time:</strong> 30-40 hours including Insanity speedrun and 100% collectibles.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="missable">⚠ Missable trophies warning</h2>

<div>
 <strong>Critical missables — pay attention to these:</strong><br><br>
 <strong>1. Eye Spy</strong>, Chunk disappears after Grace's section ends<br>
 <strong>2. Order Up!</strong>. The Chef must be killed during Grace's chapter<br>
 <strong>3. You Little Rascal!</strong> — Several Mr. Raccoons are area-locked<br>
 <strong>4. Case Closed!</strong>. Many files are story-locked, can't backtrack<br>
 <strong>5. Coin Collector</strong>. Some Antique Coin sources are missable
</div>

<div>
 💡 <strong>Save strategy:</strong> Use ALL 12 manual save slots. Save at the start of every area. If you miss a collectible, you can reload to that exact point. There is no chapter select after credits — your saves are your only safety net.
</div>
