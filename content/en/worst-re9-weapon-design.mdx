<h1>Worst RE9 weapon design — the Clatter Carbine specifically</h1>
<p>RE9 has 17 weapons. Sixteen have distinct identities. The Clatter Carbine is the franchise's most-anonymous weapon in years.</p>

<blockquote>Clatter Carbine should have been redesigned or cut. Generic mid-tier rifle that no one remembers. Replaceable in any direction.</blockquote>

<h2>Why Clatter Carbine fails</h2><p>No distinct identity. Damage/RPM placement is between Match Pistol and Requiem Sniper. Visual design is generic military rifle. Audio is unremarkable.</p><h2>What it should have been</h2><p>Either a higher-damage single-shot precision rifle or a faster-RPM assault carbine. Pick a direction. Don't sit in the middle.</p><h2>Korean community</h2><p>Korean weapon discussion threads on Naver consistently identify Clatter Carbine as the most-skipped weapon. The Korean speedrun community doesn't use it.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Cut Clatter Carbine from RE10. Add either a distinctive precision rifle or distinctive assault weapon in its place. Mid-tier identity doesn't earn a slot.</p>
