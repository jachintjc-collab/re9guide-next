<h1>Requiem Sniper Rifle — chapter 7 unlock, the franchise's best precision rifle</h1>
<p>Requiem Sniper Rifle unlocks at chapter 7 through ARK facility weapon cache. It's the franchise's most accurate dedicated sniper rifle. Specific use cases reward it; general play doesn't.</p>

<blockquote>Requiem Sniper Rifle is a specialist tool. If you're not playing chapter 7 onward, ignore it. If you are, master it for the chapter-specific advantages.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Base damage: 350. Headshot multiplier: 3.5x. Magazine: 5 rounds. Reload speed: 3.2s. Scope: 4x with low-light enhancement.</p>

<h2>Best Requiem Sniper Rifle scenarios</h2>
<p>Chapter 7 ARK long-corridor zombie encounters. Chapter 8 Eveline phase 1 distant attacks. Commander X1 boss precision shots between his attack windows.</p>

<h2>The scope mechanic</h2>
<p>4x scope with low-light enhancement makes the rifle particularly effective in chapter 7 ARK's dim corridors. The enhancement adds about 2 stops of effective light gain.</p>

<h2>Ammo limitations</h2>
<p>Sniper ammo is rare. Chapter 7 yields roughly 10-15 rounds total. Chapter 8 adds 4-6 more. Manage carefully.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>Insanity Requiem Sniper Rifle is essential for safer Commander X1 engagement. Phase 1 of that fight from sniper distance saves significant HP.</p>

<h2>Korean community tier</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 sniper rifle community ranks Requiem as A-/B+. The rating reflects niche utility rather than general power.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Requiem Sniper Rifle is a chapter 7-8 specialist. Don't expect it to revolutionize earlier gameplay. Use it for the specific situations that reward precision at distance.</p>
