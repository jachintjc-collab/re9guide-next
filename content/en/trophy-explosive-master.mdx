<h1>Explosive Master — 50 enemies killed by explosives</h1>
<p>Kill 50 enemies using explosive weapons. Grenade Launcher, Rocket Launcher, environmental explosions, and grenade-class throwables all count. The 50-count is per-save, doesn't reset between chapters.</p>

<blockquote>Explosive Master is one of the most natural-to-grind trophies in RE9. Doesn't require deliberate effort if you use the GM-79 Grenade Launcher regularly.</blockquote>

<h2>What counts as explosive kill</h2>
<p>Direct GM-79 Grenade Launcher kills (multiple ammo types — all count). Direct Rocket Launcher kills (the chapter 8 weapon). Environmental explosions you trigger (gas canisters, electrical panels, vehicle fuel tanks). Hand-thrown grenades (frag, incendiary, flash — flash doesn't count, only damaging types).</p>
<p>Doesn't count: kills from explosions you didn't trigger, kills from enemy-vs-enemy explosion damage, kills that occur from explosion-induced fire damage after the initial explosion.</p>

<h2>Easiest farming locations</h2>
<p>Wrenwood hotel basement (chapter 1) has three explosive gas tanks and infinite respawning enemies on Insanity. Standard difficulty has limited respawns but still works for 5-10 kills per visit.</p>
<p>Care Center pharmacy (chapter 3) has chemical storage that creates a chain-explosion if triggered with one grenade. Single grenade can produce 8-12 kills under right conditions.</p>
<p>ARK biolab (chapter 7) has a specific room with electrical panel chain explosion. Single trigger produces 6 kills typically.</p>

<h2>The Rocket Launcher math</h2>
<p>Rocket Launcher gets 5 ammo in the standard playthrough. That's potentially 5+ kills depending on enemy clustering. Save Rocket Launcher ammo for boss-area trash mob clusters where 2-3 kills per rocket is realistic.</p>

<h2>Insanity bonus</h2>
<p>Insanity respawn rates make Explosive Master trivial. Most players complete this trophy during their first Insanity playthrough without trying. Standard playthrough requires more deliberate farming.</p>

<h2>Korean speedrun strategy</h2>
<p>Korean speedrun community has documented an explosive-only run category. The category requires Explosive Master plus several other restrictions. The Korean Naver thread on this category includes detailed environmental explosion location maps.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Explosive Master is the trophy you'll get without trying if you use the GM-79 regularly. Don't grind it specifically. Use grenades when they're tactically appropriate and the count will accumulate. NG+ will finish it if Standard didn't.</p>
