<p><strong>The Commander is the man who knew Marcus Halverson became Chunk; and ordered it.</strong> Real name: Major Richard Vance, ARK Facility operations leader from 2020-2025. His audio logs in RE9 reveal the full Chunk story.</p>

<tr><td><strong>ARK assignment</strong></td><td>Posted to ARK Facility 2020. Mandate: oversee Plant 43 weaponization research. Reports directly to The Connections leadership.</td></tr>
