<h2 id="s-tier">S-tier — almost certainly happening</h2>

<h3>#1: Ada Wong DLC</h3>
<p><strong>Why #1:</strong> The Ada Beacon cutscene (NG+ Insanity unlock) shows Leon hearing Ada's voice on a satellite frequency. She says three words before signal cuts. The setup is too specific to be decoration; Capcom doesn't voice-act 3 sentences and animate a unique cutscene for a one-shot easter egg. Famitsu director Nakanishi specifically refused to confirm "no DLC" when asked about Ada at the May 2026 interview.</p>
<p><strong>Most likely scope:</strong> 4-6 hour campaign featuring Ada infiltrating a Connections facility, set 2-3 months after RE9's ending. Could feature young Leon flashback.</p>
<p>See our <a href="/ada-wong-re9-reversal-arc/">Ada Wong RE9 reversal arc</a>.</p>

<h3>#2: Sherry Birkin Italy DLC</h3>
<p><strong>Why S-tier:</strong> Leon's personal codex contains: "Last spoke to Sherry 6 months ago. She's in Italy with the BSAA." First named Sherry reference in mainline RE since RE6. Capcom historically doesn't name dead characters off-screen, Sherry is being deliberately positioned for return. Italy location + BSAA setup = sequel hook or DLC.</p>
<p><strong>Most likely scope:</strong> 5-7 hour BSAA mission in Italian countryside. Could feature Claire Redfield cameo (TerraSave alumni network).</p>

<h2 id="a-tier">A-tier — strong setup</h2>

<h3>#3: Mia Winters narrative DLC</h3>
<p><strong>Why A-tier:</strong> Mia's helicopter EW-2017 appearance in Grace True Ending is a 3-minute moment. Fans want more closure on her arc post-RE7. EW initials + 2017 year = Ethan Winters tribute. DLC could explore how she became EW-2017 operator.</p>

<h3>#4: Marcus Halverson ARK prequel</h3>
<p><strong>Why A-tier:</strong> Insanity-only family audio logs about Marcus (Chunk's pre-transformation identity) are framework-ready for expansion. Capcom could build 4-6 hour DLC showing Marcus's 14-month transformation timeline; from researcher to monster. Tragic narrative arc Capcom has historically loved (Albert Wesker, Alex Wesker).</p>
<p>See our <a href="/chunk-origin-mystery-explained/">Chunk origin guide</a>.</p>

<h3>#5: Chris Redfield BSAA mission</h3>
<p><strong>Why A-tier:</strong> Brief Leon codex reference to Chris's recent BSAA assignment. Could pair with #2 (Sherry Italy) as connected DLCs. Chris has been background since RE8 ending; fans want him back in mainline narrative.</p>

<h2 id="b-tier">B-tier — plausible</h2>

<h3>#6-8: Setup-supported but not guaranteed</h3>
<p><strong>Vivien Ashcroft 2009 DLC</strong>. explore her time at The Connections with Eveline. Narrative gap exists.<br>
<strong>Eveline Bulgaria photo DLC</strong>, direct visual hint in Mia cameo. Could be tie to RE10.<br>
<strong>Free-roam Raccoon City</strong> — streets are pre-rendered. Wouldn't require new content, just unlock mode.</p>

<h2 id="c-tier">C-tier; Capcom pattern</h2>

<h3>#9-10: Franchise-typical content</h3>
<p><strong>Mercenaries mode</strong>. Capcom's default DLC pattern since RE4. Datamined assets suggest groundwork.<br>
<strong>Boss Rush mode</strong> — enemy pool framework supports this. Easy implementation.</p>

<h2 id="faq">FAQ. RE9 DLC speculations</h2>

<p><strong>Q: Has Capcom officially confirmed any DLC?</strong><br>
A: No. May 2026 Famitsu interview was the closest, Nakanishi refused to deny Ada Wong DLC, which the fan community interprets as soft confirmation.</p>

<p><strong>Q: How much do RE DLCs typically cost?</strong><br>
A: $15-30 for story DLC. $5-10 for cosmetic packs. RE9 will likely follow this pattern.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Will RE9 DLC require NG+ completion?</strong><br>
A: Historically yes — RE8 Shadows of Rose required main campaign clear. RE9 DLC likely same.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Are there datamined unused assets?</strong><br>
A: Limited findings. Some unused character animations exist (possibly Ada). Voice file references to "Italy" and "Bulgaria" found in code strings.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Could DLC affect base game endings?</strong><br>
A: Likely no; RE's DLC pattern keeps base game canon untouched. DLC adds new perspectives, not retroactive changes.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Which DLC would fans most want?</strong><br>
A: Ada Wong by a massive margin. Reddit polls consistently show 60-70% prefer Ada DLC over any alternative.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Will any DLC be free?</strong><br>
A: Mercenaries / Boss Rush modes are typically free updates. Story DLC is always paid.</p>

<p><strong>Q: When will Capcom officially announce DLC?</strong><br>
A: Predicted late 2026 (Summer Game Fest 2026 or Tokyo Game Show 2026). Capcom historically announces DLC 6-12 months before release.</p>
