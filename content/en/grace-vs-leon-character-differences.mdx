<h2>Grace Ashcroft, abilities and exclusives</h2>

<h3>// BLOOD CRAFTING SYSTEM</h3>

<p>Grace's signature mechanic is the <strong>Blood Collector</strong>, a device that harvests Infected Blood from enemies. Combined with Scrap, this enables crafting:</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>S&S M232 S&S M232 handgun Ammo</strong> — basic crafting recipe (Scrap + Blood)</li>
 <li><strong>Hemolytic Injector</strong>; one-shots mini-bosses from behind</li>
 <li><strong>Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol Magazines</strong>, extended mag for the M232</li>
 <li><strong>First Aid Items</strong> — supplemental healing</li>
</ul>

<p>Recipes unlock by analyzing Blood Specimens at the Blood Lab. <strong>Solve Specimen 1 (Denatured) ASAP</strong>. it unlocks Hemolytic Injectors which trivialize the Chef and Chunk fights.</p>

<h3>// SAFE-CRACKING (EXCLUSIVE)</h3>

<p>Only Grace can interact with the 5 locked safes. If you encounter a safe as Leon, you cannot open it; switch back to Grace's section in the story.</p>

<h3>// STEALTH FOCUS</h3>

<p>Grace's chapters emphasize avoidance. Many encounters are designed around sneaking past enemies rather than fighting. Use the Hemolytic Injector for silent stealth kills from behind.</p>

<h2>Leon S. Kennedy — abilities and exclusives</h2>

<h3>// WEAPON UPGRADE SYSTEM</h3>

<p>Leon visits <strong>Supply Boxes</strong> across Raccoon City and the ARK to spend Credits on weapon upgrades:</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Tier 1 upgrades</strong>, base improvements (damage, capacity, accuracy)</li>
 <li><strong>Tier 2 upgrades</strong>. specialized stats (rate of fire, reload speed)</li>
 <li><strong>Tier 3 (Advanced Tuning)</strong> — unlocked by collecting all 25 Mr. Raccoons</li>
</ul>

<h3>// HATCHET FINISHER</h3>

<p>When an enemy is staggered (low HP or post-parry), Leon can perform a Hatchet finisher; an instant-kill execution. Three finishers unlock the "Chop Chop" trophy.</p>

<h3>// HEAVY WEAPON ACCESS</h3>

<p>Leon has access to weapons Grace cannot use:</p>

<ul>
 <li>Requiem Revolver (his signature weapon)</li>
 <li>MSBG 500 / W870 / 990-TAC Shotguns</li>
 <li>Marksman 1A Sniper Rifle</li>
 <li>Clatter Carbine (Clatter Carbine)</li>
 <li>Rocket Launcher (single-use)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Story-driven character switching</h2>

<p>You don't choose which character to play; the story switches automatically based on chapter progression:</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Wrenwood</strong> — Grace (intro)</li>
 <li><strong>Care Center 1F-Basement</strong>, Grace</li>
 <li><strong>Care Center Attic-Courtyard</strong>; Leon</li>
 <li><strong>Raccoon City</strong> — Leon (mostly)</li>
 <li><strong>ARK Facility</strong>; Leon (final boss)</li>
</ul>

<div>
 ⚠ <strong>Critical:</strong> Once the story switches to Leon, <strong>you cannot return as Grace</strong>. Any Grace-exclusive activities (safe-cracking, missable Mr. Raccoons in Care Center) must be completed before the transition.
</div>

<h2>Optimal play for each character</h2>

<h3>// AS GRACE</h3>
<ul>
 <li>Solve Blood Specimen (Denatured) ASAP for Hemolytic Injectors</li>
 <li>Open all 5 safes during her chapters</li>
 <li>Kill the optional Chunk and Chef bosses for unique drops</li>
 <li>Use stealth approach. most fights are avoidable</li>
 <li>Conserve Blood — recipes get expensive late-game</li>
</ul>

<h3>// AS LEON</h3>
<ul>
 <li>Pick up MSBG 500 W870 Police shotgun in Care Center Attic immediately</li>
 <li>Visit every Supply Box to upgrade weapons</li>
 <li>Collect all 25 Mr. Raccoons for Advanced Tuning (Tier 3)</li>
 <li>Master parry → Hatchet counter for boss fights</li>
 <li>Save Requiem Bullets for boss weak points only</li>
</ul>

<h2>Which character is "better"?</h2>

<p>Neither; they're complementary halves of the same game. Capcom designed the campaign so Grace's setup work (safe codes, blood crafting, exploration) directly benefits Leon's combat sections. A player who hoards Hemolytic Injectors as Grace will struggle once Leon takes over.</p>

<p>If you must pick a favorite for replay value:</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Speedrunners</strong> prefer Leon. Infinite Requiem trivializes his sections</li>
 <li><strong>Completionists</strong> prefer Grace — her exploration is where most missables live</li>
 <li><strong>Casual players</strong> often find Grace more relaxing (less combat pressure)</li>
 <li><strong>Combat fans</strong> prefer Leon for the boss fights and weapon variety</li>
</ul>
