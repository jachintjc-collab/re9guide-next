<h2 id="faq">FAQ. ARK Facility</h2>

<p><strong>Q: Can I return to ARK Facility after leaving?</strong><br>
A: Not on Standard play. You can revisit on NG+ with free area selection. Plan collectible runs accordingly.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What is the minimum playthrough time for ARK Facility?</strong><br>
A: ~4 hours on first playthrough. NG+ speedrun cuts to 60% of that time with Infinite Magnum (Requiem Revolver infinite ammo).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Which charm should I equip for this area?</strong><br>
A: Crafter&rsquo;s Touch + Iron Skin + Phoenix Down + Survivor&rsquo;s Will (max defense).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Are there missable collectibles in ARK Facility?</strong><br>
A: Eveline&rsquo;s Locket is mandatory pickup. Marcus family audio only plays on Insanity. Eveline Reference Photo only on NG+ Insanity.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What is the ARK Facility hardest moment?</strong><br>
A: Victor Gideon Phase 3 QTE parry (0.4-second window). Miss = Bad Ending. Practice on lower difficulties first.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Does the ARK Facility area have RE callbacks?</strong><br>
A: Capcom 40th Anniversary logo on calendar (subtle). Director Nakanishi cameo photo in Vivien lab. RE Engine credit plaque. Mia Winters cameo in helicopter.</p>

<p><strong>Q: How much currency is available in ARK Facility?</strong><br>
A: Approximately 45,000 total (3 Antique Coins + dense enemy drops). Spend on T3 Requiem Revolver upgrades.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Is ARK Facility different on Insanity NG+?</strong><br>
A: Same layout, but enemies scaled +40% HP, player takes 2x damage, pickup spawn -30%. Infinite Magnum unlock makes Requiem Revolver mid-fight crafting unnecessary.</p>
