<h1>All Weapons — 17 weapons, every one needed, two easy to miss</h1>
<p>Seventeen weapons in RE9. Possession of all 17 (not just unlock, actual possession) triggers the trophy. Two are easy to miss because their unlock requires specific dialogue choices or non-obvious actions.</p>

<blockquote>All Weapons is the trophy that pays off knowing the game. First-playthrough completion rate is under 5% per Capcom telemetry. Worth understanding why before going for it.</blockquote>

<h2>The 17 weapons by category</h2>
<p>Handguns (4): Silencer 9, Match Pistol, S&W M1911, Hand Cannon.</p>
<p>Shotguns (3): W870 Police, S&S M232, Ithaca Stakeout.</p>
<p>Rifles (3): Bolt-Action Hunter, Hunter Repeater, Marksman's M40.</p>
<p>Heavy (2): Rocket Launcher, GM-79 Grenade Launcher.</p>
<p>Special (3): Knife, Mortal Edge knife, Flare Gun.</p>
<p>Hidden (2): Quickdraw Army (chapter 5 puzzle reward), Compound Bow (chapter 7 character choice).</p>

<h2>The two trophy-locking weapons</h2>
<p>Quickdraw Army requires solving the Rhodes Hill manor study puzzle in under 2 minutes. The puzzle has unlimited attempts but the speed requirement is strict. Failing the speed run locks the weapon for that save — NG+ resets.</p>
<p>Compound Bow requires choosing the third dialogue option ("I work alone better") in the chapter 7 Mia Winters cameo scene. The default and second options give you a different bonus (extra ammo). The bow specifically requires the third option.</p>

<h2>Insanity unlock difference</h2>
<p>Hand Cannon unlocks on Insanity completion (carry-forward to NG+). Mortal Edge knife unlocks on knife-only Insanity completion. Both can be unlocked early through specific NG+ glitches, but Capcom patched most of them in v1.02. As of v1.02, intended difficulty progression is required.</p>

<h2>Trophy verification timing</h2>
<p>The trophy triggers at the moment of acquiring the seventeenth weapon. If you sold a weapon to a merchant earlier (this is possible with three of the weapons), you need to repurchase before the trophy registers. The system checks current possession, not historical possession.</p>

<h2>Korean speedrun community weapons route</h2>
<p>Naver RE9 speedrun community has documented the All Weapons category route — a specific playthrough order that acquires all 17 weapons in minimum time. The route requires NG+ start with Hand Cannon and Mortal Edge already unlocked.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>All Weapons is the trophy that confirms you understand RE9's systems. Don't attempt on first playthrough — you'll miss the dialogue-locked weapons. NG+ second playthrough with All Weapons in mind is the realistic completion path.</p>
