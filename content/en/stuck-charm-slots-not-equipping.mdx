<h1>Stuck: charm slots not equipping — the slot allocation rules</h1>
<p>Charm slot equipping has specific allocation rules. Conflicts produce equipping failure messages that confuse players.</p>

<blockquote>Charm slots have hidden compatibility rules. Some charms can't share slots with specific others. The rules aren't documented in-game well.</blockquote>

<h2>The hidden rules</h2><p>Predator and Survivor's Will share resistance tag — only one can equip. Marksman's Eye and Lucky Star share crit tag — only one can equip. Phoenix Down occupies dedicated slot (can't be replaced mid-encounter).</p><h2>What the failure message says</h2><p>'This charm conflicts with currently equipped slot' — but doesn't specify which slot conflicts.</p><h2>How to debug</h2><p>Unequip all charms. Equip one at a time. Identify conflict by trial.</p><h2>The Korean reference</h2><p>Korean Naver community published complete charm compatibility table. Search 'RE9 부적 호환성' for full reference.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>The conflict system is undocumented. Capcom should patch UI to show conflicts explicitly. Until then, use Korean compatibility table.</p>
