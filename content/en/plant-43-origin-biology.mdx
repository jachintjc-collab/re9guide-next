<p><strong>Plant 43 is not a plant, it&rsquo;s a mold-based bioweapon.</strong> Created at ARK Facility in 2018 from research into Eveline&rsquo;s E-virus strain. Marcus Halverson&rsquo;s exposure protocol is what transformed him into Chunk. This is the complete biology + origin breakdown.</p>

<div><table><tr><th>When</th><th>Event</th></tr><tr><td><strong>2016 ARK Facility research</strong></td><td>ARK Facility commissioned by The Connections. Initial mandate: study Eveline&rsquo;s E-virus mold strain for therapeutic applications.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2018 Plant 43 creation</strong></td><td>Plant 43 designated as the modified E-virus mold variant. Originally synthesized for fast-spreading wound treatment.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2019 first incidents</strong></td><td>Plant 43 contained breach. 3 lab technicians infected. Quick suppression. Records sealed.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2021 weaponization decision</strong></td><td>ARK leadership pivots Plant 43 from medical to military application. Marcus Halverson assigned to safety review.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2023 Lily&rsquo;s diagnosis</strong></td><td>Marcus discovers his daughter has leukemia. He proposes Plant 43 reverse-engineering for treatment. Rejected.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2024 Marcus self-experimentation</strong></td><td>Marcus injects himself with Plant 43 culture. 14-month transformation begins. Becomes &ldquo;Chunk.&rdquo;</td></tr>
