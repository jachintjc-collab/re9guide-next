<h1>RE9 control scheme options — 3 schemes explained</h1>
<p>RE9 offers 3 control schemes: Modern, Classic RE, Custom. Each suits different player types.</p>

<blockquote>Custom is the answer for serious players. Modern is fine for first playthrough. Skip Classic RE.</blockquote>
