<h1>990 Tac shotgun — the chapter 7 unlock that splits players</h1>
<p>The 990 Tac shotgun unlocks at chapter 7 through specific ARK facility interaction. It's the franchise's most modern-tactical shotgun design. Players love or hate it. The honest assessment depends on your playstyle.</p>

<blockquote>The 990 Tac is a sidegrade to W870 Police, not an upgrade. Don't switch unless your playstyle specifically benefits from semi-auto fire rate over per-shot damage.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Base damage: 75 per pellet × 8 pellets = 600 total point-blank (vs W870's 855). Magazine: 10 shells (vs W870's 7). Reload speed: 2.8s (faster than W870). Fire mode: semi-auto.</p>

<h2>The semi-auto advantage</h2>
<p>990 Tac's semi-auto fire mode allows rapid follow-up shots. Reload speed is faster than W870's pump action. Magazine capacity is larger.</p>

<h2>The damage disadvantage</h2>
<p>990 Tac's per-shot damage is 30% lower than fully upgraded W870. Stagger threshold is lower. Boss encounters favor W870.</p>

<h2>When 990 Tac wins</h2>
<p>Group zombie encounters where multiple targets need rapid suppression. Chapter 7 ARK security guards (the encounter the weapon was designed for). Sustained engagement scenarios.</p>

<h2>When W870 wins</h2>
<p>Boss encounters. Single-target high-damage requirements. Stagger-critical situations.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>On Insanity, W870's per-shot damage advantage matters more than 990 Tac's rate-of-fire advantage. Most Insanity players keep W870 primary.</p>

<h2>Korean community split</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 community is split on 990 Tac. Naver discussion threads have unresolved debates. Speedrun community uses W870 almost exclusively.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>990 Tac is for players who prefer modern tactical aesthetics over RE-classic pump shotgun feel. Mechanically it's a sidegrade. Pick based on preference, not assumed superiority.</p>
