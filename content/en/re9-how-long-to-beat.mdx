<h1>How Long is RE9? (Full Playtime Guide)</h1>
<div>
<p><strong>Author note:</strong> Written by <strong>the writer</strong>, a 23-year Resident Evil series fan based in Seoul, South Korea. This guide is based on 6 full RE9 playthroughs on PS5 and Steam (Casual → Insanity → NG+ Insanity completions). All strategies, timings, and locations have been personally tested on the v1.02 patch. AI tools were used only for grammar review and note organization; no AI-generated content appears in strategy analysis.</p>

<p><strong>Short answer:</strong> RE9 takes <strong>12-15 hours</strong> on Standard difficulty for a first playthrough. Casual reduces to 8-10 hours. Insanity extends to 18-22 hours. Platinum trophy completionist run is 35-45 hours across 3 playthroughs.</p>
<div>
<strong>📍 Game structure note (v1.02):</strong> RE9 is officially divided into <strong>4 main areas</strong>: Wrenwood, Rhodes Hill, Raccoon City, and ARK Facility. Our "8 chapters" framing reflects the 8 sub-sections within these 4 areas. The game has <em>no Chapter Select</em> after completion and <em>no Free-Roam</em>. Area progression is strictly linear.
</div>

<h2>Time investment for completion levels</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Beat the game:</strong> 12-15 hours (Standard, one ending)</li>
 <li><strong>All endings:</strong> 25-30 hours (3 playthroughs)</li>
 <li><strong>All trophies:</strong> 35-45 hours (Platinum)</li>
 <li><strong>100% completion:</strong> 50-60 hours (all collectibles, all moments)</li>
</ul>

<h2>What slows you down?</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Collectibles:</strong> Hunting all 75 files adds 6-8 hours total</li>
 <li><strong>Puzzle solving:</strong> First-time Organless Corpse puzzle averages 15-20 min</li>
 <li><strong>Boss retries:</strong> Insanity bosses cost 1-2 hours each on average</li>
 <li><strong>Chapter exploration:</strong> Optional rooms add 30-60 min per chapter</li>
</ul>
