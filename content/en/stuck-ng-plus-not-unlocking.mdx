<h1>Stuck: NG+ not unlocking — the completion conditions</h1>
<p>NG+ requires specific completion conditions. Some players hit credits without unlocking NG+ due to incomplete saves.</p>

<blockquote>NG+ won't unlock if save state was corrupted or chapter completion wasn't registered. Solutions exist.</blockquote>

<h2>The requirements</h2><p>Complete chapter 8. View full credits. Save game post-credits when prompted.</p><h2>Common failures</h2><p>Quit before credits complete = no NG+ unlock. Quit during post-credits = potential state corruption. Skipping cutscenes can cause completion not to register.</p><h2>How to recover</h2><p>Load save from chapter 8 boss area. Complete boss again. Watch credits to end without skipping. Save post-credits prompt.</p><h2>If state corrupted</h2><p>Cloud backup recovery. Or replay chapter 8.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Always watch credits to completion on first playthrough. Don't skip the post-credits prompt. NG+ depends on the completion sequence.</p>
