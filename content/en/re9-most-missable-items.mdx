<h2>Mistakes to absolutely avoid</h2>

<h3>1. Don't skip the Care Center records office</h3>
<p>It looks like an optional side room. It's not, the Family Photograph there is trophy-required. Five minutes of detour saves 12+ hours of replay.</p>

<h3>2. Don't sell Antique Coins</h3>
<p>Each is worth 10,000 currency at vendor, but collecting all 6 unlocks "Treasure Hunter" trophy. Hold them.</p>

<h3>3. Don't miss Quartz Fragment 5</h3>
<p>It's at the top of Raccoon City Clock Tower. Easy to skip if you bypass the climb. Required for Platinum.</p>

<h3>4. Don't fail Grace True Ending QTE</h3>
<p>0.4-second knife parry window. Practice on lower difficulties. Failing = Bad Ending, no Mia cameo.</p>

<h3>5. Don't avoid Insanity NG+</h3>
<p>"True Survivor" trophy + Chunk family audio + Eveline Reference Photo all require this. Skipping = no Platinum.</p>
