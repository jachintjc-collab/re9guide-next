<h1>Commander X1 — Master Plagas integration profile</h1>
<p>Commander X1 is RE9's chapter 7 antagonist and the franchise's first successful Master Plagas integration shown on-screen. His character work elevates him from boss encounter to ethically complex figure.</p>

<h2>Background</h2>
<p>Commander X1 (real name unrevealed) was a BSAA-affiliated researcher who volunteered for the Master Plagas integration program. He's the third successful integration. The first two are dead.</p>

<h2>The integration motivation</h2>
<p>Commander X1's chapter 7 dialogue reveals he volunteered to understand the Plagas integration mechanism from inside. His research goal was preventing future coerced integrations through documenting the successful integration process.</p>

<h2>Voice work</h2>
<p>Steve Blum voices Commander X1. The voice direction emphasizes dying dignity over villain menace. The integration program reveal monologue is the character's vocal anchor.</p>

<h2>The dying state</h2>

<h2>The sixty-two candidates</h2>
