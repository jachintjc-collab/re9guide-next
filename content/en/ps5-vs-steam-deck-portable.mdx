<h2>Real-world performance</h2>
<p>Real-world frame rate testing across these platforms reveals differences that on-paper specs don't capture. Frame pacing, stutters during transitions, and input lag all factor into the experience.</p>

<h3>Frame pacing analysis</h3>
<p>RE9 maintains stable 60fps on PS5 and Xbox X in most chapters. Frame drops happen during:</p>
<ul>
<li>Chapter 7 ARK Lower transitions (-5 fps for ~3 seconds)</li>
<li>Vivien Stage 2 boss room (steady 60 but tight margins)</li>
<li>RPD area transitions on Xbox X (slight drops, fixed in v1.02)</li>
<li>Plant 43 greenhouse vines (heavy particle effects)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Recommendation by player type</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Casual player + 4K TV:</strong> PS5 or Xbox X. Set to Quality mode (60fps 4K).</li>
<li><strong>Competitive speedrunner:</strong> PC. Use Performance mode + low latency settings.</li>
<li><strong>Insanity NG+ challenge:</strong> Wired controller, wired headphones, low input lag display.</li>
<li><strong>Portable / handheld:</strong> Switch 2 acceptable for casual, frustrating for Insanity.</li>
<li><strong>HDR enthusiast:</strong> PS5 + OLED TV. Best HDR pipeline.</li>
</ul>
