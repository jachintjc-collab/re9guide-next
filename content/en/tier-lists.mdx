<h2>How these tier lists were built</h2>
<p>Each tier list represents 12-20 hours of focused testing on top of my normal playthroughs. For weapons, I ran kill-time experiments against the same enemies with each gun. For charms, I did NG+ Insanity boss attempts with rotating 3-charm loadouts. For chapters, I logged time-to-completion and rated replay sessions. All rankings reflect post-v1.02 balance.</p>

<p><strong>Update cadence:</strong> Tier lists are reviewed monthly. If Capcom ships a balance patch (May 2026 v1.02 buffed W870 Police shotgun, nerfed AR), I re-test affected items within 2 weeks and update rankings if shifts justify.</p>

<h2>Want a different ranking?</h2>
<p>If you disagree with a ranking, the methodology section in each article shows the exact dimensions used. You can re-weight for your playstyle (e.g., bump "Acquisition ease" higher if you're early-game). The underlying scores per item are documented in each article.</p>
