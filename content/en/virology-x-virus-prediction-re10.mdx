<h1>X-virus — predicting what RE10's pathogen will be called and what it'll do</h1>
<p>RE has had T, G, T-Veronica, T-Abyss, T-Phobos, C, E, A, Uroboros, and various Plagas variants. RE10 needs a new pathogen. Based on RE9's setup work and Capcom's naming patterns, here's the educated guess at what the next pathogen will be.</p>

<blockquote>RE10's pathogen will be X-virus or named with an X derivative. It'll be a CRISPR-engineered designer pathogen, not a discovered natural one. I'll commit to this prediction.</blockquote>

<h2>The naming pattern</h2>
<p>RE pathogen names roughly follow alphabet order with some breaks: T (original), G, T-Veronica, T-Abyss, T-Phobos, C, E, A. The remaining unused letters most plausibly accessible: X, Y, Z, plus single-letter naming for specific variants like K or M.</p>
<p>X is the most narratively loaded option. X-virus would have implicit "unknown variable" framing. Capcom's marketing department would love this. I expect X.</p>

<h2>What X-virus will probably be</h2>
<p>Based on RE9's setup signals (real CRISPR research framing, dual-use bioethics emphasis, modernized biotech institutional framing), X-virus will most likely be:</p>
<p>A deliberately engineered pathogen rather than a discovered natural one. The engineering will use CRISPR-equivalent precision rather than Progenitor's universal delivery system. The threat will be designer-customizable for specific target populations.</p>
<p>This direction extends RE9's biology framework rather than restarting. It also addresses the franchise's "increasingly dated compared to real medicine" problem.</p>

<h2>The geographic setting</h2>
<p>RE9 leaves three plausible future settings: Korean peninsula (the franchise has Korean translation and Korean community investment but has never been set in Korea), Eastern European Wesker children traces, and South American or African Plagas-derivative regional outbreak.</p>
<p>My bet: Korean peninsula. RE9's Korean localization investment was substantial. Capcom's Korean market reception has been strong. A Korean setting would reward the Korean fan community that's been building since RE2 era.</p>

<h2>The protagonist question</h2>
<p>RE9 left several characters available for protagonist promotion in RE10: Grace Ashcroft (returning), a new BSAA field commander (set up through chapter 6), or a fresh character introduced in RE10 itself.</p>
<p>My bet: new BSAA field commander. The institutional framing setup in RE9 wants a BSAA-internal protagonist. Grace's arc closed cleanly in RE9. A fresh BSAA character lets RE10 explore the institutional ethics dimension RE9 set up.</p>

<h2>What threat scale will RE10 use</h2>
<p>RE9 was chapter-level threat (Care Center outbreak with regional implications). RE10 needs to escalate. The plausible escalation tiers:</p>
<p>Regional outbreak (city or country level). National crisis (multi-million population affected). Continental threat. Global existential.</p>
<p>My bet: national crisis, escalation arrested by protagonist intervention. Going global feels premature. Staying regional feels insufficient for sequel scaling.</p>

<h2>The Wesker successor question</h2>
<p>RE9 set up multiple unaccounted Project W survivors. RE10 could feature one or more as antagonists. This would extend established lore rather than introducing fresh villain origin.</p>
<p>My read: yes, a Project W survivor will appear, but as secondary villain not primary. The primary antagonist will be either an institutional figure (BSAA insider gone rogue) or a researcher figure (Vivien-class character with broader plot leverage).</p>

<h2>Timeline prediction</h2>
<p>Capcom's RE mainline cycle is roughly 4 years. RE7 (2017), RE Village (2021), RE9 (2026 — slight extension). RE10 most likely 2029-2030.</p>
<p>Pre-RE10 we'll probably see: a major DLC for RE9 (2026-2027), a Revelations-style spinoff (2027-2028), maybe a Code Veronica-style mainline supplement. The path to RE10 is paved.</p>

<h2>Korean fan theory consensus on RE10</h2>
<p>Korean RE community theories for RE10 are split. The majority Naver consensus: Korean or Eastern Asian setting, BSAA institutional plot, returning legacy character (probably Wesker successor or Eveline derivative). My predictions overlap with the Korean consensus on geographic and institutional dimensions.</p>

<h2>Verdict</h2>
<p>RE10 will arrive 2029-2030. It will probably feature X-virus or similar named CRISPR-engineered pathogen, a Korean or East Asian setting, a new BSAA protagonist, a Wesker-derivative secondary antagonist, and national-scale crisis with institutional ethics as thematic anchor. Capcom is currently making most of these decisions. We'll know in 3-4 years which guesses were right.</p>
