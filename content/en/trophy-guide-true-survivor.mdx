<h1>True Survivor — 100% completion on Insanity</h1>
<p>100% completion of a single save on Insanity difficulty. The franchise's hardest trophy. Estimated 40+ hours of focused play with extensive save management. Trophy ticks at game completion confirmation.</p>

<blockquote>True Survivor is RE9's elite trophy. Only completionists should attempt. Plan as a multi-week project, not a weekend grind.</blockquote>

<h2>What 100% on Insanity means</h2>
<p>All Mr. Raccoon plushies (25). All Charms (18). All audio logs (47). All documents (89). All safes opened (8). All weapons (17). All named rooms visited (31 ARK rooms specifically). True ending unlocked. All on a single Insanity save.</p>
<p>The collectible count is identical to lower difficulties. The challenge is doing collection while surviving Insanity.</p>

<h2>The chapter-locked items risk</h2>
<p>Same chapter locks apply on Insanity. Missing a chapter-locked item locks the entire save for True Survivor. The risk is amplified because Insanity replay takes 30+ hours.</p>
<p>Use the Korean Naver community's chapter checklists obsessively. Verify before chapter transitions.</p>

<h2>Insanity-specific completion challenges</h2>
<p>Two collectibles are in rooms that are inaccessible without defeating an Insanity-only mini-boss. The chapter 5 conservatory Charm is behind a Plant 43 spawn that doesn't exist on lower difficulties.</p>
<p>The ARK biolab document requires defeating an Insanity-specific Tyrant encounter. Skipping the encounter (possible on lower difficulties) isn't an option on Insanity.</p>

<h2>Save management on Insanity</h2>
<p>Use all 6 save slots. Rotate. Cloud backup essential. Lose 15+ hours of progress without backup = quit the trophy attempt.</p>
<p>Save before any boss. Save before any collectible attempt in dangerous rooms. Save after every chapter completion.</p>

<h2>Time estimate</h2>
<p>40-60 hours for a skilled player who completed Insanity already. 60-90 hours for first-time Insanity attempt while pursuing True Survivor.</p>

<h2>Korean True Survivor community</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 elite trophy community on Naver has documented optimal True Survivor playthroughs with chapter checklists, save management protocols, and Insanity-specific tactics. The Korean coverage is significantly more thorough than English-language equivalents.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>True Survivor is for completionists who consider the difficulty itself part of the reward. Don't pursue casually. Plan it. Use Korean Naver resources. Backup obsessively. The trophy is meaningful only if you can complete without burning out.</p>
