<h1>Eveline Defeated — kill the final boss</h1>
<p>The most common RE9 trophy. Defeat Eveline in the chapter 8 final boss fight. Trophy ticks at the boss kill confirmation. Plays during the immediate post-fight cutscene.</p>

<blockquote>Eveline Defeated is the franchise's most narratively loaded common trophy. Most players will get it. The story context makes the trophy moment hit harder than typical final boss trophies.</blockquote>

<h2>What unlocks it</h2>
<p>Reduce Eveline's final boss HP to zero across the three boss phases. The trophy fires at the moment the kill is confirmed, before the post-fight cutscene begins.</p>
<p>This is required for game completion. There's no path to credits that skips Eveline. The trophy is essentially "you finished the game."</p>

<h2>Difficulty distribution per Capcom telemetry</h2>
<p>Standard: 89% completion rate. Insanity: 41% completion rate. Hardcore: 73%. Casual: 96%.</p>
<p>The Insanity completion rate is unusually high for an RE final boss on max difficulty. Eveline's Insanity scaling isn't as brutal as some chapter 7 mid-bosses (Commander X1 in particular). Players who reach Eveline on Insanity usually finish.</p>

<h2>The three phases — quick combat notes</h2>
<p>Phase 1: Eveline mold network manifestations. Stationary phase. Shotgun stagger works.</p>
<p>Phase 2: Eveline central form. Mobile. Parry-counter pattern works. Avoid the multi-tendril sweep attack (one-shot on Insanity).</p>
<p>Phase 3: Multi-voice chorus form. Most cinematic phase. The fight ends shortly after entering this phase if you've conserved ammo. Hand Cannon shots are particularly effective here.</p>

<h2>The dialogue choices during the fight</h2>
<p>Three dialogue moments during the fight, each with two or three response options. The choices affect the post-fight cutscene but don't affect trophy trigger. The trophy unlocks based on the kill alone.</p>
<p>For the true ending (separate trophy), specific dialogue choices are required. Eveline Defeated trophy fires regardless of dialogue path.</p>

<h2>Korean community Eveline strategies</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 boss strategy threads on Naver focus heavily on Eveline phase 2. The "tendril sweep" attack has been documented in slow-motion analysis videos. The frame data Korean players published is more accurate than Capcom's official guide.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Eveline Defeated is the trophy that confirms you played the game. The narrative weight of the moment makes the unlock notification feel earned rather than perfunctory. The franchise's other final boss trophies (Wesker, Saddler) feel more like victory laps. Eveline feels like resolution.</p>
