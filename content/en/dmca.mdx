<h2>DMCA takedown requests</h2>

<p>If you are a copyright holder and believe content on this site infringes your rights, please submit a DMCA takedown notice. Per 17 U.S.C. § 512(c), notices must include:</p>

<ol>
 <li>A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized agent</li>
 <li>Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed</li>
 <li>Identification of the allegedly infringing material with sufficient detail to locate it (URL preferred)</li>
 <li>Contact information (address, phone, email)</li>
 <li>A statement of good faith belief that the use is unauthorized</li>
 <li>A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate and the submitter is authorized to act</li>
</ol>
