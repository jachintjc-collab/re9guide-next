<h1>Knife Only — complete chapter 5 using only knife combat</h1>
<p>Complete the entire chapter 5 Rhodes Hill sequence using only knife combat. Firearm use during chapter 5 disqualifies the trophy. Chapter completes normally; trophy ticks at chapter transition.</p>

<blockquote>Knife Only is the franchise's best chapter-specific knife challenge. Chapter 5 is designed for varied combat; restricting to knife is a real challenge that rewards Mortal Edge investment.</blockquote>

<h2>What disqualifies</h2>
<p>Any firearm discharge during chapter 5. Any thrown explosive. Any environmental-trigger explosion. Any non-knife damage to any enemy.</p>
<p>Defensive use (knife block, knife parry without follow-up attack) doesn't disqualify. Only damage delivery to enemies via non-knife methods disqualifies.</p>

<h2>Chapter 5 encounters by knife difficulty</h2>
<p>Standard zombies (mostly easy). Blister Borne hybrids (moderate, parry-counter pattern works). Plant 43 mini-boss (extremely difficult — knife damage is reduced against Plants).</p>
<p>The Plant 43 boss is the trophy's main challenge. Mortal Edge knife essential. Multiple parry-counter cycles required.</p>

<h2>Required preparation</h2>
<p>Mortal Edge knife unlocked (Insanity completion prerequisite). Endurance Charm equipped. Marksman's Eye Charm equipped (knife crit damage).</p>
<p>Without Mortal Edge, knife damage against Plant 43 boss is borderline non-viable. Don't attempt without unlock.</p>

<h2>Insanity Knife Only</h2>
<p>Insanity Chapter 5 with knife only is one of the franchise's harder challenges. Plant 43 boss on Insanity with knife only requires near-flawless parry execution.</p>

<h2>Korean knife-only category</h2>
<p>Korean speedrun community has a knife-only chapter 5 category with documented routing. The Korean route avoids unnecessary combat through specific positioning, reducing total parry burden.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Knife Only is for committed players who have Mortal Edge and want a specific skill demonstration. Don't attempt without Mortal Edge. The trophy is the kind of achievement worth pursuing if you enjoy combat mastery.</p>
