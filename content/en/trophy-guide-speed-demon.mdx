<h1>Speed Demon — complete the game in under 6 hours</h1>
<p>Complete RE9 in under 6 hours real-time. The timer counts all gameplay, including cutscenes (skippable on NG+). Trophy ticks at credits start if time is under 6:00:00.</p>

<blockquote>Speed Demon is achievable on NG+ Standard with skip-everything routing. First-playthrough Speed Demon is for masochists.</blockquote>

<h2>The 6-hour target</h2>
<p>Standard playthrough with no exploration: 14-18 hours. NG+ playthrough with cutscene skip: 7-10 hours. NG+ with speedrun routing: 5-7 hours. NG+ with optimized speedrun: 3-5 hours.</p>
<p>Speed Demon target (6 hours) is roughly at the NG+ casual speedrun level. Doesn't require speedrun community elite tricks.</p>

<h2>Critical skip patterns</h2>
<p>All cutscenes: enable cutscene skip in NG+ settings. Saves ~45 minutes.</p>
<p>All exploration: skip every collectible, every safe, every optional room. Saves ~90 minutes.</p>
<p>All combat that's avoidable: run past enemies in non-boss-required areas. Saves ~60 minutes.</p>

<h2>Chapter time budgets for 6:00 target</h2>
<p>Chapter 1: 25 minutes. Chapter 2: 20 minutes. Chapter 3: 45 minutes. Chapter 4: 35 minutes. Chapter 5: 40 minutes. Chapter 6: 60 minutes. Chapter 7: 60 minutes. Chapter 8: 65 minutes.</p>
<p>Total: 5:50. 10-minute buffer for unexpected events.</p>

<h2>The chapter 6 RPD time sink</h2>
<p>Chapter 6 is the unavoidable time investment. Mr. X pursuit can't be sped past — his patrol timing is fixed. Budget 60 minutes minimum even on optimal speedrun.</p>

<h2>Insanity Speed Demon</h2>
<p>Insanity Speed Demon is 8+ hours minimum. Same trophy doesn't unlock for Insanity time alone — Standard or below required.</p>

<h2>Korean speedrun community</h2>
<p>Korean speedrun community on Naver has documented the Speed Demon route with timing splits. The Korean route includes one specific glitch skip (chapter 4 forest route) that isn't widely known in English-language speedrun communities.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Speed Demon on NG+ Standard with cutscene skip + casual speedrun = achievable in 1-2 dedicated attempts. Don't pursue blind. Watch a YouTube speedrun playthrough first to understand the routing.</p>
