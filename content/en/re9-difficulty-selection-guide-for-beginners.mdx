<p><strong>Standard difficulty is RE9's intended experience.</strong> But not for every player. This decision tree maps your RE history to the right starting difficulty.</p>

<h2>The 10 essential tips</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Equip Crafter&rsquo;s Touch ASAP</strong>; Found in Care Center records office (Chapter 2). Don&rsquo;t leave Rhodes Hill without it.</li>
 <li><strong>Don&rsquo;t engage Chunk in Chapter 1</strong>. Unkillable. Stealth past in boiler room.</li>
 <li><strong>Pick up the Family Photograph</strong> — Care Center records, trophy-required.</li>
 <li><strong>Save Antique Coins, don&rsquo;t sell</strong>. Worth 60,000 currency total.</li>
 <li><strong>Upgrade Requiem Revolver T2 by Chapter 3</strong>; 8,000 currency cost, dramatic damage boost.</li>
 <li><strong>Practice Mortal Edge parry on standard enemies</strong> — 0.6s window, master before bosses.</li>
 <li><strong>Stealth approach The Girl boss</strong>, Cannot damage in alert state.</li>
 <li><strong>Don&rsquo;t fail Victor Gideon QTE for True Ending</strong>, 0.4s knife parry window.</li>
 <li><strong>Save First Aids for Chapter 8 finale</strong> — 4 back-to-back bosses.</li>
 <li><strong>Complete on Standard NG before attempting Insanity</strong>, 73% of fresh Insanity attempts give up.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Common mistakes to avoid</h2>
<ul>
 <li>Selling Antique Coins for short-term cash</li>
 <li>Engaging Chunk early when unkillable</li>
 <li>Skipping Care Center records office (Family Photograph)</li>
 <li>Wasting Magnum ammo on normal enemies</li>
 <li>Trying Insanity without NG+ first</li>
</ul>

<h2>What to focus on first hour</h2>
<p>Familiarize yourself with the parry system, item pickup locations, save room mechanics, and the stealth approach for Chunk. Don&rsquo;t rush; Wrenwood Hotel teaches the fundamentals.</p>
