<h2>How this tier list is ranked</h2>

<p>Weapons are evaluated on five criteria:</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Damage per shot</strong>; raw stopping power</li>
 <li><strong>Ammo availability</strong>, how easily you can keep firing</li>
 <li><strong>Upgrade ceiling</strong> — max stats with Tier 3 Advanced Tuning</li>
 <li><strong>Boss effectiveness</strong>. performance against Super Tyrant, Plant 43, Victor Gideon</li>
 <li><strong>Insanity viability</strong>. does it hold up on the hardest difficulty?</li>
</ul>

<div>
 ⚡ <strong>Note:</strong> Grace's weapons cannot be upgraded — they are evaluated on baseline stats only. Leon's weapons can be upgraded at Supply Boxes using Credits, with <strong>Tier 3 Advanced Tuning</strong> unlocked after collecting all 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam.
</div>

<h2 id="sTier">S Tier; Must-have weapons</h2>

<div>
 <div>
 <div>S</div>
 <div><div>S Tier</div><div>// Big weapons. Always equipped.</div></div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Requiem Revolver <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>The signature weapon of RE9 and the single best gun in the game. <strong>One-shot kills</strong> standard zombies and deals massive damage to boss weak points. Reserved for the final boss canon ending. Lost briefly during the Care Center sequence and regained in East Raccoon City.</div>
 <div>Best for: Boss fights, weak point destruction · Where: Story automatic</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>MSBG 500 W870 Police shotgun <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Leon's primary heavy weapon and the workhorse of every boss fight. Devastating close-range damage, generous ammo drops, and excellent upgrade ceiling. <strong>Pick this up immediately</strong> in the Care Center Attic during the second Chunk fight.</div>
 <div>Best for: Bosses, hordes · Where: Care Center Attic (near second Chunk)</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Mortal Edge Hatchet <span>BOTH</span></div>
 <div>The upgraded Hatchet from defeating The Commander in the ARK. Significantly faster swings, larger parry window, and increased finisher damage. <strong>Mandatory for Insanity runs</strong>, the default Hatchet's parry timing is too tight on Insanity.</div>
 <div>Best for: Parries, finishers · Where: Defeat The Commander (ARK)</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>990-TAC Semi-Auto W870 Police shotgun <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Semi-automatic W870 Police shotgun with 8-round capacity and excellent shot grouping. Accepts dot sight attachment. Outclasses both the W870 and MSBG 500 once fully upgraded. Costs 5,500 CP at Central Camp Supply Box — worth every coin.</div>
 <div>Best for: Sustained boss DPS · Where: Central Camp Supply Box (5,500 CP)</div>
 </div>
</div>

<h2 id="aTier">A Tier. Strong all-rounders</h2>

<div>
 <div>
 <div>A</div>
 <div><div>A Tier</div><div>// Reliable, versatile, well worth upgrading.</div></div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Silencer 9 Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Built-in suppressor allows stealth kills on standard enemies, invaluable for ammo-conservation runs. High accuracy and decent damage. <strong>Outclasses every other S&S M232 handgun</strong> in Leon's arsenal.</div>
 <div>Best for: Stealth, ammo conservation · Where: East Raccoon City Supply Box</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Marksman 1A Sniper Rifle <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Bolt-action precision rifle with exceptional damage per shot. Essential for the long-distance Mr. Raccoon shots in Raccoon City. Single-shot capacity is a downside but headshots one-shot most enemies.</div>
 <div>Best for: Precision boss damage, distant collectibles · Where: BSAA Container 03 (East Raccoon City)</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Matilda IMP <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Three-round burst S&S M232 handgun, spiritual successor to RE2's Matilda. <strong>Highest DPS S&S M232 handgun</strong> in the game. Burns through ammo fast but is excellent for staggering enemies and boss weak points. Unlocks post-story.</div>
 <div>Best for: Sustained DPS, staggers · Where: Special Content Store (500 CP, post-story)</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Clatter Carbine <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>The only Clatter Carbine in Resident Evil Requiem. High fire rate and damage output for sustained combat. <strong>Insanity-tier weapon</strong> when fully upgraded — handles hordes and bosses equally well.</div>
 <div>Best for: Insanity runs, hordes · Where: Special Content Store (6,000 CP, post-story)</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>W870 Police shotgun <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Higher ammo capacity than MSBG 500. Faster pump-action but slightly less damage per shot. <strong>Excellent backup W870 Police shotgun</strong> for the Raccoon City and ARK sections. Found at Gun Shop Kendo.</div>
 <div>Best for: Mid-game W870 Police shotgun upgrade · Where: Gun Shop Kendo</div>
 </div>
</div>

<h2 id="bTier">B Tier; Situational picks</h2>

<div>
 <div>
 <div>B</div>
 <div><div>B Tier</div><div>// Solid in specific scenarios, not always equipped.</div></div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Blacktail <span>GRACE</span></div>
 <div>Grace's mid-game S&S M232 handgun upgrade. Reliable damage and accuracy at mid-range. Best S&S M232 handgun option for Grace, but limited by no upgrades.</div>
 <div>Best for: Grace's secondary · Where: East Wing 2F</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>The Gal SMG <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Hidden SMG in the flooded Underground Parking Garage. High fire rate with solid mid-game stats. Worth picking up but eventually outclassed by the Clatter Carbine.</div>
 <div>Best for: Mid-game horde control · Where: Underground Parking Garage NW (battery area)</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Bell Tower Sniper <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>A high-damage hidden revolver inside the Raccoon City Bell Tower. Powerful but hard to find. easy to miss during the chaotic Bell Tower sequence.</div>
 <div>Best for: Hidden powerhouse · Where: Raccoon City Central Bell Tower</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Stiri REVO3 A1 SMG <span>BOTH</span></div>
 <div>Lightweight SMG available to both characters. High fire rate but low damage per shot. Stock attachment significantly improves accuracy. Decent stop-gap weapon.</div>
 <div>Best for: Early SMG option · Where: East Raccoon City Supply Box</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Redemption Rifle <span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Revolver-style rifle that uses Rifle Ammo. Unique hybrid feel but generally outclassed by both the Marksman 1A and Clatter Carbine. Good for thematic playthroughs.</div>
 <div>Best for: Niche builds · Where: Special Content Store (3,500 CP, post-story)</div>
 </div>
</div>

<h2 id="dTier">D Tier; Skip or sell</h2>

<div>
 <div>
 <div>D</div>
 <div><div>D Tier</div><div>// Trash tier. Don't waste resources.</div></div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Default Hatchet <span>BOTH</span></div>
 <div>Replaced immediately by Mortal Edge Hatchet. Parry window is too tight for Insanity. Only useful in the early game before The Commander fight.</div>
 <div>Best for: Until you get Mortal Edge · Where: Starting equipment</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Rocket Launcher (consumable) <span>BOTH</span></div>
 <div>Limited single-use weapon. Save for boss finishers or emergencies. wasting it on regular enemies is a costly mistake. <strong>Not the same as the unlockable Infinite RPG.</strong></div>
 <div>Best for: Emergency boss kills · Where: Limited drops in Raccoon City</div>
 </div>
</div>

<h2 id="upgrade">Upgrade priority — where to spend Credits</h2>

<p>Credits are limited in a single playthrough. Here's the optimal upgrade order:</p>

<ol>
 <li><strong>MSBG 500 W870 Police shotgun (Tier 1)</strong>; your earliest workhorse. Damage upgrade first.</li>
 <li><strong>Requiem Revolver (Tier 1-2)</strong>. boss-killing potential is massive when upgraded.</li>
 <li><strong>Default Hatchet (Tier 1)</strong> — parry window improvement until Mortal Edge.</li>
 <li><strong>990-TAC W870 Police shotgun (Tier 2-3)</strong>. once you reach Central Camp, this becomes your main W870 Police shotgun.</li>
 <li><strong>Marksman 1A (Tier 1)</strong>, capacity upgrade only; damage is already high.</li>
 <li><strong>Silencer 9 (Tier 1)</strong> — accuracy upgrade for stealth headshots.</li>
</ol>

<div>
 💡 <strong>Tier 3 Advanced Tuning unlock:</strong> Find all 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam to unlock the highest weapon upgrade tier. Without this, you cannot fully upgrade any weapon. Required for the "Fully Loaded" trophy.
</div>

<h2 id="faq">FAQ; RE9 weapons</h2>

<p><strong>Q: What's the single best weapon in RE9?</strong><br>
A: The Requiem Revolver (S-tier). Highest single-shot damage in the game, headshot-eligible bonus damage, and the only weapon that reliably staggers all bosses on Insanity. Pair with Crafter's Touch charm for sustainable ammo economy.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Should I prioritize the W870 Police shotgun or the Clatter Carbine?</strong><br>
A: W870 Police shotgun for close-range bosses (Mr. X, Chunk), Clatter Carbine for crowd control (Plant 43 swarm phases). On Insanity both are essential, get the W870 Police shotgun first in Chapter 2, AR in Chapter 6.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Is the Knife viable as a primary weapon?</strong><br>
A: Knife-only runs are possible but require Knife Edge charm + parry mastery. Best treated as a parry tool and finisher rather than primary. See the Knife Edge entry in our charms tier list.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Which weapons should I skip upgrading?</strong><br>
A: Skip the S&S M232 S&S M232 handgun upgrades after Chapter 3. The Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol is C-tier and becomes obsolete once you have the Requiem Revolver + W870 Police shotgun + AR. Save your gunpowder for top-tier weapons.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Are weapon stats different between Grace and Leon?</strong><br>
A: Most weapons share stats, but Leon has 2 exclusive weapons (Match Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol, Special Requiem Revolver) and Grace has 1 exclusive (Hand Cannon prototype). All factored into this ranking.</p>

<p><strong>Q: How does NG+ affect weapon tier rankings?</strong><br>
A: NG+ unlocks Infinite Requiem Revolver which makes the Requiem Revolver even more dominant. Also unlocks Rocket Launcher (one-shot most bosses). The Infinite Requiem Revolver + Phoenix Down charm combo is the meta NG+ Insanity loadout.</p>

<p><strong>Q: What's the highest DPS weapon for the final boss?</strong><br>
A: For Victor Gideon final boss: Requiem Revolver (headshot phase 1-2) → W870 Police shotgun (phase 3 close-range) → Knife parry for the QTE. For Chunk final boss: Clatter Carbine on Insanity (Crafter's Touch + Ammo Hoarder mandatory).</p>

<p><strong>Q: Will weapon balance change in upcoming RE9 patches?</strong><br>
A: Capcom's May 2026 patch (v1.02) buffed the W870 Police shotgun spread by 8% and nerfed the AR recoil slightly. This tier list reflects post-v1.02 balance. Future patches monitored.</p>
