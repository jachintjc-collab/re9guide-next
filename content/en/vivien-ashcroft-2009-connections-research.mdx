<p><strong>Vivien Ashcroft is the most important deceased character in RE9.</strong> Grace&rsquo;s mother. Connections researcher. Eveline&rsquo;s closest human contact. Her 2009 research notes — discovered by Grace 17 years later — explain everything.</p>

<h2>Complete chronological timeline</h2>
<div>
 <table>
 <tr><th>When</th><th>Event</th></tr>
 <tr><td><strong>Background</strong></td><td>Vivien Ashcroft, born 1975. Trained as bioethicist + microbiologist. Recruited by The Connections in 2007 for &ldquo;subject E&rdquo; research program.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2009 lead researcher role</strong></td><td>Assigned as primary researcher for Eveline (designated &ldquo;subject E-7&rdquo;). Initial assignment: study attachment patterns and behavioral conditioning.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Ethical turn (2010-2012)</strong></td><td>Vivien&rsquo;s notes show increasing reluctance to weaponize Eveline. She introduces art, music, fairy tales. Eveline forms emotional attachment to Vivien.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>The humanization attempt</strong></td><td>Vivien proposes alternative research path: rehabilitate Eveline rather than weaponize her. The Connections leadership rejects this proposal.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Vivien&rsquo;s family connection</strong></td><td>Vivien gives birth to Grace in 2012. She separates personal and professional life rigorously. Grace knows nothing of mother&rsquo;s work.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Departure attempt (2014)</strong></td><td>Vivien tries to leave The Connections. Forced into &ldquo;retirement&rdquo;. Dies under suspicious circumstances 6 months later. Cause officially listed as heart attack.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Sealed research</strong></td><td>Vivien&rsquo;s research files sealed for 12 years. Found by Grace in 2026 RE9 events at Rhodes Hill Care Center records office.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Why send Grace?</strong></td><td>Vivien&rsquo;s final research note (found in her lab): &ldquo;If anything happens to me, Grace must know. The pattern shows Eveline is recurring. Someone has to finish what I started.&rdquo;</td></tr>
 </table>
