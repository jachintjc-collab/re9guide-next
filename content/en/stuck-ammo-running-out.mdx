<h1>Stuck: ammo running out — what to actually do</h1>
<p>Running low on ammo is RE's most common player problem. Specific RE9 strategies extend ammo supply effectively.</p>

<blockquote>Running out of ammo means you're using too much firearm and not enough knife. The Mortal Edge transformation is real; learn parry.</blockquote>

<h2>Immediate solution</h2><p>Switch to knife combat for next 2-3 encounters. Conserve firearm ammo for boss or critical encounters.</p><h2>Medium-term solution</h2><p>Adjust playstyle: parry-counter pattern, knife light-attack chains, environmental kills (gas tanks, electrical panels).</p><h2>Long-term prevention</h2><p>Equip Ammo Hoarder charm. Take Crafter's Touch for ammo crafting yield. Don't waste shots on stunned enemies — let knife finish them.</p><h2>If genuinely stuck</h2><p>Use Standard difficulty if Insanity-stuck. Restart chapter if currently mid-chapter. NG+ if save is past chapter end.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Ammo scarcity is intended design. RE9 wants you to use knife. Learn parry; ammo problems disappear.</p>
