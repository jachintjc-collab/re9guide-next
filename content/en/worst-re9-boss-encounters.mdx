<h1>Worst RE9 boss encounters — the bosses that don't earn their spots</h1>
<p>RE9 has 8 boss-class encounters. Six are well-designed. Two are notably weaker. Here's which fail and why.</p>

<blockquote>Boss design quality in RE9 is overall strong, but Vivien Plant 43 phase 2 hitbox and final boss phase 1 padding are the franchise's worst current decisions.</blockquote>

<h2>Vivien Plant 43 phase 2 hitbox</h2><p>The vine sweep attack has a hitbox 30% larger than the visual model. Players who dodge based on visuals get hit. Frustrating, not difficult.</p><h2>Eveline final phase 1 padding</h2><p>Phase 1 of the final boss is 40% padding before meaningful combat begins. Should be cut to 50%.</p><h2>What works</h2><p>Commander X1 is the best-designed boss in the franchise this generation. The Chef optional is excellent.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Patch the Plant 43 hitbox in v1.03. Padding can't be patched but should inform RE10 boss design.</p>
