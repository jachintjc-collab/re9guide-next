<h2 id="what-is-this">What this diary tracks</h2>
<p>Every Capcom patch to a live Resident Evil game changes <em>something</em>. sometimes just bug fixes, sometimes balance tweaks that quietly invalidate published guides. The patch notes Capcom officially releases are usually short, often vague, and almost never list every change. The Patch Diary's job is to bridge that gap.</p>
<p>For each patch, this diary will track three things:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Verified changes</strong> — guides on this site that I personally re-tested after the patch dropped, with a note of what (if anything) needed updating</li>
<li><strong>Community-reported observations</strong>, findings from Reddit, Discord, X, Steam Community, and Korean / Japanese RE forums, each tagged with how confident I am in the source</li>
<li><strong>What this means for play</strong>. the editorial layer competitor sites usually skip. Patch notes describe what changed; this diary describes what to <em>do</em> about it</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="methodology">My verification methodology</h2>
<p>For each patch, I run the following protocol on PS5 + Steam:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Fresh install on both platforms</strong>. PS5 is my primary; Steam serves as cross-platform sanity check.</li>
<li><strong>Re-clear all named bosses on Standard difficulty</strong>. Each fight gets timed and notes are taken on whether parry windows, attack patterns, or weak-point HP appear different from my pre-patch records.</li>
<li><strong>Spot-check Insanity-only content</strong>. Insanity-specific damage multipliers and parry-window changes are where Capcom most often makes "stealth" balance changes that don't appear in patch notes.</li>
<li><strong>Re-verify all 5 safe codes and Quartz puzzle solutions</strong>. Codes <em>shouldn't</em> change between patches but Capcom has historically tweaked one or two combinations in past RE games (notably RE7 and RE8 had safe code changes mid-lifecycle).</li>
<li><strong>Cross-reference with two community sources</strong>. Reddit r/residentevil's patch megathread and the Korean Inven RE9 board are my primary external checks.</li>
</ol>
<p>Every guide on this site shows a "LAST VERIFIED" date in its editorial header. After each patch, that date updates only if I actually re-ran the verification protocol — not just because time passed.</p>

<h2 id="v102-status">v1.02 verification status</h2>
<p>The v1.02 patch was issued in May 2026. Below is the verification status of every guide on this site as of the latest re-test pass.</p>

<div>
<div>// VERIFIED · MAY 14, 2026</div>
<h3>Boss strategy guides; all 10 bosses confirmed unchanged</h3>
<p>Re-cleared Victor Gideon, The Commander, Mr. X / Super Tyrant, Plant 43, The Girl, Titan Spinner, Blister Borne, Chunk (Grace and Leon paths), and The Chef on Standard difficulty. Attack patterns, parry windows, and weak-point HP behave identically to pre-v1.02 testing. <strong>No guide updates required.</strong></p>
</div>

<div>
<div>// VERIFIED · MAY 14, 2026</div>
<h3>Safe codes, all 5 combinations unchanged</h3>
<p>Re-tested every safe combination on a fresh save file post-v1.02 install. All combinations from the launch version remain valid. The All Safe Codes guide carries forward without modification.</p>
</div>

<div>
<div>// VERIFIED · MAY 14, 2026</div>
<h3>Trophy progression — no missables behavior change</h3>
<p>The 7 missable trophies (including Truth Seeker, Mr. Raccoon Hunter, and Coin Collector) trigger and persist identically to launch behavior. Platinum trophy roadmap unchanged.</p>
</div>

<div>
<div>// PARTIALLY VERIFIED · MAY 14, 2026</div>
<h3>Insanity boss damage, anecdotal change reports</h3>
<p>I re-cleared Victor Gideon and The Commander on Insanity. Both fights felt identical to my pre-patch attempts within margin-of-error timing. <strong>However, multiple community reports on r/residentevil and Inven note that Plant 43 incendiary damage feels reduced post-v1.02.</strong> I have not yet re-cleared Plant 43 on Insanity to test this directly. Treating as unconfirmed pending re-test.</p>
</div>

<div>
<div>// PENDING · MAY 14, 2026</div>
<h3>Speedrun route, Pump Room skip pending re-test</h3>
<p>The frame-perfect Pump Room skip used in sub-4-hour speedruns has not been re-tested post-v1.02. Speedrun community reports it still works on PC; PS5 console-side verification pending. The Sub-4-Hour Speedrun Guide remains marked v1.02 verified for everything except this specific skip.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="community">Community-reported observations (unverified)</h2>
<p>The following changes have been reported by the broader RE9 community but I have not personally verified. Each is tagged with the source and my current confidence level. I will move items to the verified section above as I re-test.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Plant 43 incendiary multiplier</strong> — multiple Reddit threads claim incendiary rounds deal noticeably less damage to Plant 43 cysts post-v1.02. Source: r/residentevil patch megathread + Inven board. <em>Confidence: medium. Re-test pending.</em></li>
<li><strong>ARK Facility lighting tweak</strong>, community notes the ARK descent corridor visibility was adjusted, possibly to make the Pump Room skip slightly less reliable. Source: speedrun.com discussion. <em>Confidence: low, could be placebo.</em></li>
<li><strong>Mr. Raccoon collision detection</strong> — 2 reports of a previously-difficult Raccoon (specifically the one in the Care Center attic) being slightly easier to grab. Source: Discord screenshots. <em>Confidence: low; could be lighting / camera angle perception.</em></li>
<li><strong>Achievement unlock timing</strong>, some users report the "Mr. Raccoon Hunter" trophy now pops on the 25th Raccoon collection rather than at next save, which is a quality-of-life improvement. Source: PSNProfiles forum. <em>Confidence: medium-high.</em></li>
</ul>

<div>
<strong>Important note on community reports:</strong> Even when multiple players report something, perception bias is real. Patch v1.02 is the first major patch — placebo effect is at peak. I default to skepticism and only update guides after first-hand re-verification. If you've personally tested and confirmed a community report, please email me with your platform, save state, and a short repro description.
</div>
