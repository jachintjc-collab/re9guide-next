<h1>RPD Veteran — complete chapter 6 without taking damage</h1>
<p>Complete the entire chapter 6 RPD sequence without losing any HP. The Mr. X pursuit, the Cerberus encounters, the lobby return — all must happen without damage. Trophy ticks at chapter completion.</p>

<blockquote>RPD Veteran is the franchise's best damage-free trophy in years. The chapter design supports careful play. Doable for engaged players willing to learn the routing.</blockquote>

<h2>What counts as damage</h2>
<p>HP loss from any source: enemy attacks, environmental hazards, scripted scenes (none in chapter 6 fortunately), poison effects, etc. Even 1 HP of damage breaks the run.</p>
<p>Doesn't count: cinematic-only health bar movements (none in chapter 6), Charm or item state changes that don't reduce HP.</p>

<h2>The Mr. X pursuit — the run-killer</h2>
<p>Mr. X's pursuit sequence is the most likely failure point. His swipe attack deals 35-50 HP on Standard, instant-kill on Insanity. Mistiming the route causes damage.</p>
<p>Specific route: archive room exit → western corridor → main lobby → eastern stairwell. Don't backtrack. Don't enter the chief's office (Mr. X patrols there). Don't take the basement shortcut (Cerberus spawn point).</p>

<h2>Cerberus encounters</h2>
<p>Two Cerberus encounters in chapter 6. Both are avoidable through specific routing. Don't enter the lobby fountain area without Cerberus path clearance. Don't approach the western evidence locker without checking the patrol corner.</p>

<h2>Save management</h2>
<p>Use a dedicated save slot for chapter 6 attempts. Save at the chapter start. Reload immediately on any damage. Don't try to "recover" — the trophy is binary.</p>

<h2>Insanity RPD Veteran</h2>
<p>Insanity multiplies Mr. X's threat. Single mistake = instant kill = lost run. Achievable but requires near-perfect knowledge of patrol patterns. Save scumming essential.</p>

<h2>Korean speedrun community route</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 chapter 6 damage-free routing is documented in Naver speedrun threads. The Korean route avoids Mr. X with specific timing windows that aren't in English-language guides. Translation recommended.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>RPD Veteran is achievable for patient players who learn the routes. Practice on Standard first. The Insanity version is for true completionists only. The Standard version rewards 1-2 hours of dedicated attempts.</p>
