<!-- FAQ -->
<div>
 <div>Collectibles FAQ</div>
 <div>

 <div>
 <div>How many collectibles are in Resident Evil Requiem?</div>
 <div>There are <strong>5 main categories</strong>: 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam, 75 Files, 22 Antique Coins, 5 Locked Safes, and 9 Plant Seedlings. Total <strong>136 individual items</strong> across the game.</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Which collectibles are missable?</div>
 <div>Many collectibles are missable due to point-of-no-return transitions. The most critical missables include Mr. Raccoon #5, ARK-area Files (#70-73), and the optional boss-locked Pantry Inventory File. <strong>Use manual saves before each major area transition.</strong></div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Do I need 100% collectibles for Platinum?</div>
 <div>Yes for some, no for others. <strong>Required:</strong> Case Closed (75 Files), You Little Rascal (25 Mr. Raccoons), and No Safe Is Safe (5 safes). <strong>Easier:</strong> Coin Collector only needs one Antique Coin spent.</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Can I collect everything in one playthrough?</div>
 <div>Yes if you're careful. Use this hub's location guides + save before every major transition. If you miss something, NG+ retains your weapons and unlocks but resets collectibles.</div>
 </div>

 <div>
 <div>Which character collects what?</div>
 <div><strong>Grace-only:</strong> Antique Coins, Safe codes, NG+ Final Puzzle items. <strong>Both characters:</strong> Mr. Raccoon Memoriams, Files. <strong>Leon-only:</strong> Most Raccoon City and ARK area collectibles.</div>
