<p><strong>Short answer:</strong> <strong>1) Requiem Revolver first (Chapter 2 immediately)</strong>. <strong>2) W870 Police shotgun second (Chapter 4-5)</strong>. <strong>3) Clatter Carbine third (Chapter 6)</strong>. <strong>Skip Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol after Chapter 3</strong>. Save currency for tier-3 upgrades — partial upgrades waste resources.</p>

<p>If you keep all Antique Coins (recommended), you have 95,000+ available. That covers Requiem Revolver T3 + W870 Police shotgun T2 + AR T1.</p>

<h2>Upgrade priority by chapter</h2>

<h3>Ch. 2: Upgrade Requiem Revolver T1 → T2 immediately</h3>
<p>You'll have ~15,000 currency at end of Care Center. T2 Requiem Revolver costs 8,000. Spend it. Save the rest for T3.</p>

<h3>Ch. 4-5: Upgrade W870 Police shotgun T1 → T2</h3>
<p>By Chapter 5, you should have ~25,000 saved. W870 Police shotgun T2 costs 6,000. Save rest for Requiem Revolver T3.</p>

<h3>Ch. 6: Requiem Revolver T3 + AR T1</h3>
<p>Requiem Revolver T3 (15,000) is the biggest single purchase. Pair with AR T1 (6,000) for RPD encounters.</p>

<h3>Ch. 8: W870 Police shotgun T3 + final preparations</h3>
<p>Final boss Requiem Revolver + W870 Police shotgun T3 combo dominates Victor Gideon phases.</p>

<h2>Why this order?</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Requiem Revolver first:</strong> +200% damage on Insanity vs Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol. Boss damage is what matters.</li>
 <li><strong>W870 Police shotgun second:</strong> Critical for Mr. X transformation phase (Chapter 6). Don't upgrade earlier, Chapter 1-3 doesn't need it.</li>
 <li><strong>AR third:</strong> Crowd control. Pair with Plant 43 swarm phases.</li>
 <li><strong>Skip Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol:</strong> +5% damage = ~3 less shots per fight. Not worth 3,000 currency.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Common upgrade mistakes</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Upgrading Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol at Ch. 1:</strong> Wastes currency you'll need for Requiem Revolver.</li>
 <li><strong>Skipping Requiem Revolver T3:</strong> The final boss is way harder without it.</li>
 <li><strong>Upgrading too late:</strong> If you reach Ch. 6 without Requiem Revolver T2, you'll struggle with Mr. X.</li>
 <li><strong>Selling Antique Coins:</strong> Costs you 60,000 currency you can't recover.</li>
</ul>
