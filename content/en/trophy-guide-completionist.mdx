<h1>Completionist — 100% on all difficulty modes</h1>
<p>Completionist requires 100% completion across Casual, Standard, Hardcore, and Insanity difficulty modes. Single playthrough on each, all collectibles and side content per playthrough. Estimated 80-120 hours total.</p>

<blockquote>Completionist is the franchise's most demanding RE9 trophy. Plan it across multiple sessions. Don't try to grind through.</blockquote>

<h2>What 100% means per difficulty</h2>
<p>100% on a difficulty = all collectibles (Mr. Raccoons, Charms, audio logs, documents, safes), all weapons acquired, all named rooms visited, all dialogue branches explored, true ending completion.</p>
<p>This must happen on a single save per difficulty. NG+ continuations count as the same save. New saves don't carry forward — fresh 100% required.</p>

<h2>Estimated time per difficulty</h2>
<p>Casual: 18-22 hours. Standard: 22-28 hours. Hardcore: 25-32 hours. Insanity: 28-40 hours. Total: 93-122 hours for full Completionist.</p>
<p>The estimate assumes you know the game. First-time blind playthrough for any difficulty would add roughly 50% to the estimate.</p>

<h2>The recommended order</h2>
<p>Standard first (for learning the game). Then Casual (quickest, lowest stress). Then Hardcore (manageable challenge). Then Insanity (final challenge).</p>
<p>Reverse order (Insanity first) is the speedrun community approach for skilled players. Adds frustration. Not recommended for typical players.</p>

<h2>Common failure modes</h2>
<p>Missing one collectible on one difficulty requires entire NG+ replay of that difficulty. The trophy doesn't pro-rate — 99.5% completion on a difficulty doesn't count.</p>
<p>Selling a weapon to merchant before achieving All Weapons trophy on that difficulty locks completion. The collectible counts are strict.</p>

<h2>Insanity-specific risk</h2>
<p>Save scumming is essential on Insanity Completionist. The Insanity difficulty has multiple instant-death scenarios that can corrupt 25+ hours of progress. Save before any boss encounter, before any chapter transition, before any collectible attempt in dangerous rooms.</p>
<p>Cloud save backup recommended. The PS5 auto-backup feature should be enabled.</p>

<h2>Korean Completionist community</h2>
<p>The Korean RE9 Completionist community on Naver has documented optimal playthrough orders, time budgets, and risk mitigation strategies. Korean Completionist guides are generally more thorough than English-language equivalents.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Completionist is a project, not a trophy. Plan for 100+ hours. Use a tracker (Korean Naver community has spreadsheet templates). Don't burn out on Insanity — take breaks. The trophy is significant but the time investment is the real cost.</p>
