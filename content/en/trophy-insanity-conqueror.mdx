<h1>Insanity Conqueror — complete the game on Insanity difficulty</h1>
<p>Complete RE9 from chapter 1 through Eveline final boss on Insanity difficulty. Trophy unlocks at credits. Estimated 25-40 hours focused playthrough. NG+ allowed but not required.</p>

<blockquote>Insanity Conqueror is the franchise's standard hardcore trophy. Required for the harder challenge trophies. Pursue once for the achievement, then move on.</blockquote>

<h2>What unlocks it</h2>
<p>Complete the game on Insanity difficulty setting. The difficulty must be set to Insanity at chapter 1 start (or higher; no difficulty above Insanity exists in RE9). Lower-difficulty start that's later changed to Insanity doesn't count.</p>
<p>NG+ Insanity counts. First-playthrough Insanity counts. Specific save state requirements: must complete on a single Insanity-difficulty save.</p>

<h2>The Insanity difficulty modifications</h2>
<p>Enemy HP +75% over Standard. Enemy damage +50% over Standard. Reduced ammo drops. Reduced medication drops. No auto-aim. No tutorial pop-ups. Save scumming required for survival in most encounters.</p>

<h2>Most dangerous Insanity encounters</h2>
<p>Chapter 3 Vivien Plant 43 boss. The boss can one-shot if you don't have Endurance Charm equipped.</p>
<p>Chapter 6 Mr. X. One-shot on contact. Routing must be perfect.</p>
<p>Chapter 7 Commander X1. Phase 3 has multiple one-shot attacks. Frame-perfect parry required.</p>
<p>Chapter 8 Eveline phase 2 tendril sweep. One-shot if positioned in sweep path.</p>

<h2>Required Charm loadout for Insanity</h2>
<p>Endurance (essential — converts some one-shots to survivable damage). Marksman's Eye (essential — crit damage compensates for reduced ammo). Quickstep (recommended — faster dodge frames). Mortal Edge (NG+ only — knife combat).</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Insanity Conqueror is the gateway to higher trophy challenges (True Survivor, knife-only Insanity, etc.). Pursue once. Use Korean Naver tactics for boss fights. The completion itself is the reward; don't expect the trophy notification to feel special.</p>
