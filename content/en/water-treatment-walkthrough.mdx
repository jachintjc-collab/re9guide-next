<h2 id="overview">Water Treatment overview</h2>
<p>The Water Treatment Plant is the campaign's first true puzzle-heavy boss chapter, and the first chapter Grace plays solo since the Wrenwood Hotel intro. The objective is simple in concept — restart three sunlight units to expose The Girl's weakness, then beat her; but the execution requires you to plan three valve sequences in the right order while the lights are out.</p>
<p>Bring at least <strong>30 SMG rounds</strong>, <strong>2 First Aid Sprays</strong>, and the upgraded <strong>Mortal Edge Hatchet</strong> if Leon had it equipped last chapter. The hatchet carries between characters.</p>

<h2 id="entry">Entry from the Care Center Courtyard sewers</h2>
<p>Grace climbs out of the sewer hatch into the plant's main filtration room. Power is offline; the only light is from your handheld lantern. Two wanderer zombies in this room; both are blind and can be stealth-killed with a single hatchet swing if you stay behind them.</p>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Pick up the Maintenance Key</strong> from the dead foreman at the south end.</li>
 <li><strong>Loot the locker</strong> by the foreman — first SMG magazine + a Hemorrhetic Injector.</li>
 <li><strong>Open the door labeled "VALVE CONTROL"</strong> with the Maintenance Key.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="valves">Three valve sequence puzzle</h2>
<p>The Valve Control room has three pressure valves (A / B / C) mounted on the wall. Each valve has a 0-9 dial. The wall placard tells you the goal pressure for each tank, but the valves themselves interact. turning one shifts the others. The solution is fixed per difficulty (Normal: <em>A=3, B=7, C=5</em>; Insanity: <em>A=4, B=2, C=8</em>).</p>
<p>Set each valve in this order: <strong>A → C → B</strong>. The order matters because of the cross-feed plumbing. Get it right and the basement door unlocks.</p>

<h2 id="units">Three sunlight units restart sequence</h2>
<p>Through the basement door is the cathedral-like Sunlight Hall. three giant skylight units, all dark. You need to restart each unit to draw sunlight into the central plaza where The Girl waits.</p>
<h3>// UNIT A (north)</h3>
<p>Pull the breaker, then run the manual crank for 5 full rotations. Easy — no enemies.</p>
<h3>// UNIT B (east)</h3>
<p>The breaker is behind a locked grate. The grate key is in the foreman's office on the mezzanine. Three wanderers patrol the path. stealth or hatchet-parry through them.</p>
<h3>// UNIT C (west)</h3>
<p>The hardest of the three. The breaker room is flooded and you need the Filter Plate (looted from a side closet) to drain it. Once drained, pull the breaker and crank as Units A + B.</p>

<h2 id="girl">The Girl boss encounter</h2>
<p>All three sunlight units active triggers the boss cutscene. The Girl drops from the ceiling into the central plaza. She is fast, hits hard, and is invulnerable except when fully exposed to direct sunlight.</p>
<p>Full strategy in our <a href="how-to-beat-the-girl.html">The Girl boss guide</a>. Quick summary:</p>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Stay in the lit zones</strong>, the floor markings show where the sunlight reaches.</li>
 <li><strong>Lure her into the central beam</strong>. SMG bursts to expose her, then switch to W870 Police shotgun for the kill window.</li>
 <li><strong>The fight has two phases</strong> — after 50% HP, she destroys Unit B and you must restart it under fire.</li>
</ol>
<div>⚠ <strong>Do not panic-reload in shadow.</strong> Her grab attack one-shots if your back is dark. Always reload inside a lit zone.</div>

<h2 id="aftermath">Post-boss collectibles and save room</h2>
<p>The Girl drops the <strong>Sunlight Lens</strong> on death. required for the next chapter. The plant's main door unlocks automatically.</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Antique Coin</strong> in the broken Unit B housing</li>
 <li><strong>File: Plant Operator's Final Log</strong> on the foreman's desk</li>
 <li><strong>Mr. Raccoon #7</strong> hidden in the foreman office locker</li>
 <li><strong>Save room</strong> through the east door; typewriter + item box</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="transition">Transition to Raccoon City</h2>
<p>The east door of the save room opens onto the rooftop access ladder up to Raccoon City's waterfront district. Grace hands off the Sunlight Lens to Leon in a cutscene as he arrives — the next chapter is back to Leon for the <a href="rpd-orphanage-walkthrough.html">RPD + Orphanage</a> sequence.</p>

<h2 id="next">What comes next: RPD + Orphanage</h2>
<p>The next chapter is one of the campaign's most iconic; Leon arrives at the abandoned Raccoon City Police Department, encounters Mr. X for the first time, and proceeds to the Orphanage where the campaign's emotional core unfolds. Save at the plant and brace.</p>
