<h1>Stun Gun — the non-lethal tool for Pacifist and stealth play</h1>
<p>Stun Gun is RE9's only dedicated non-lethal weapon, unlocked through chapter 4 stealth path. Specific trophy pursuits (Pacifist, Stealth Master) benefit from it. General combat doesn't.</p>

<blockquote>Stun Gun is the niche tool that justifies its existence through trophy support. Pursue Pacifist or Stealth Master? Use Stun Gun. Otherwise, ignore.</blockquote>

<h2>Core stats (v1.02)</h2>
<p>Damage: 0 lethal. Stun duration: 8 seconds Standard, 5 seconds Insanity. Charge: 3 charges per battery. Reload: insert new battery (1.5s).</p>

<h2>Best use cases</h2>
<p>Pacifist trophy (chapter 4 no-kills). Stealth Master trophy (chapter 7 detection avoidance). Specific quest objectives requiring non-lethal subdual.</p>

<h2>What it doesn't do</h2>
<p>Doesn't kill. Doesn't damage. Doesn't trigger explosion chains. Doesn't help with boss encounters or main combat.</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>Stun Gun on Insanity has 5-second stun (vs Standard 8-second). Tighter window for follow-up actions. Plan accordingly.</p>

<h2>Korean community tier</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 community treats Stun Gun as trophy-pursuit tool only. Universal consensus: ignore unless pursuing specific trophies.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Stun Gun has clear use cases. Don't overestimate it. Trophy pursuit aid; not a general combat tool.</p>
