<h1>True Survivor — second listing (duplicate trophy variant)</h1>
<p>Note: True Survivor appears twice in RE9 trophy listings due to naming overlap. This entry covers the differentiation. The primary True Survivor (100% Insanity) is covered in trophy-guide-true-survivor.</p>

<blockquote>Capcom's RE9 trophy naming has two duplicates (RPD Veteran and True Survivor). The naming should be cleaner. The duplicate listings track distinct internal trophy IDs.</blockquote>

<h2>The naming overlap</h2>
<p>"True Survivor" appears as both trophy-guide-true-survivor (100% Insanity completion) and trophy-true-survivor (this entry). Same visible name, distinct internal IDs.</p>
<p>This entry covers the secondary True Survivor: completing the game without using any consumable items (no herbs, no first aid, no grenades).</p>

<h2>What this True Survivor unlocks</h2>
<p>Complete RE9 from chapter 1 to Eveline final boss without consuming any item from the consumables inventory category. Weapon ammunition consumption is allowed; consumables (herbs, sprays, throwables) are not.</p>

<h2>The challenge</h2>
<p>Without healing items, all damage is permanent. Damage management through positioning and parry execution required throughout 25-30 hours of gameplay.</p>
<p>Without throwables, the GM-79 Grenade Launcher cannot fire (it requires grenade consumables). Same for incendiary mods.</p>

<h2>Required loadout</h2>
<p>Mortal Edge knife essential. Endurance Charm (damage reduction critical). Marksman's Eye Charm (faster combat resolution). Heavy firearms (Hand Cannon, fully upgraded W870).</p>

<h2>Insanity considerations</h2>
<p>Insanity True Survivor (no consumables) is the franchise's hardest single trophy. Most completionists don't attempt it on Insanity. Standard difficulty is the realistic target.</p>

<h2>Korean restriction community</h2>
<p>Korean RE9 challenge run community on Naver has documented Standard no-consumables routes. The Korean coverage is essential for any attempt.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>This True Survivor is the hidden hardcore trophy. Don't pursue without completing other major trophies first. The challenge is real and the time investment substantial. Reserved for completionists who've exhausted other trophy goals.</p>
