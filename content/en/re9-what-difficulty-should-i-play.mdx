<p><strong>Short answer:</strong> Most players should start on <strong>Standard</strong>. If you've never played survival horror, start on <strong>Casual</strong>. If you've completed RE4 Remake / RE2 Remake recently, you can handle <strong>Insanity NG+</strong> after one Standard run. Detailed guidance below.</p>

<h2>Specific recommendations</h2>

<h3>If you want story focus → Standard</h3>
<p>Pacing matches the cinematic experience. You'll see all cutscenes without dying repeatedly.</p>

<h3>If you want challenge → Insanity NG+</h3>
<p>After one Standard run, NG+ gives you Infinite Requiem Revolver + max charms. Then Insanity is hard but possible. Best version of RE9.</p>

<h3>If you're horror-averse → Casual</h3>
<p>Less ambient tension. Fewer enemies. Skips some atmospheric beats but the story still lands.</p>

<h3>Don't do this</h3>
<p>Don't start on Insanity fresh. 73% of fresh Insanity attempts give up before Chapter 5 (The Girl). Wasted time.</p>
