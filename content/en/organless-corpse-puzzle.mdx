<h2 id="setup">Setup: entering the Anatomy Room</h2>
<p>The Anatomy Room is in the Care Center Basement's west corridor. After descending from the East Wing 2F (via laundry chute or maintenance staircase; see <a href="/care-center-basement-walkthrough/">Care Center Basement walkthrough</a>), follow the west passage past the broken vending machine.</p>
<p>The door is unlocked by default. Inside: a metal autopsy slab with a partially-dissected corpse, two empty organ shelves, and a locked exit door at the far end.</p>

<div>
⚠ <strong>Don't trigger the door yet.</strong> Approaching the exit door before placing organs spawns a zombie ambush. Locate both organs first, then approach.
</div>

<h2 id="heart">Step 1: Find the Artificial Heart</h2>
<p>The Artificial Heart is locked inside the <strong>wall cabinet across from the slab</strong> (east wall). You need the <strong>cabinet key</strong> first.</p>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Exit the Anatomy Room</strong> back toward the central corridor.</li>
 <li><strong>Enter the Records Office</strong> (adjacent door on your right).</li>
 <li><strong>Open the desk's middle drawer</strong> — the cabinet key is inside, on top of a stack of pathology reports.</li>
 <li><strong>Return to the Anatomy Room</strong> and use the key on the wall cabinet.</li>
 <li><strong>Take the Artificial Heart.</strong> It's a small glass-encased mechanical heart, glows faint red.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="lungs">Step 2: Find the Artificial Lungs</h2>
<p>The Artificial Lungs are on the <strong>upper shelf of the storage closet to the east</strong> of the Anatomy Room. You need the <strong>rolling ladder</strong> from the same closet.</p>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Enter the storage closet</strong> (door labeled "Surgical Supplies").</li>
 <li><strong>Push the rolling ladder</strong> to the back wall (interact with it to move it 2 meters east).</li>
 <li><strong>Climb the ladder</strong> and take the Artificial Lungs from the top shelf.</li>
 <li><strong>Note:</strong> the closet door auto-closes behind you, don't worry, it doesn't lock.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="assembly">Step 3: Place organs in correct order</h2>
<p>Return to the corpse on the slab. Approach the body to bring up the placement prompt.</p>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Insert the Artificial Heart first</strong> (left cavity).</li>
 <li><strong>Then insert the Artificial Lungs</strong> (right cavity).</li>
 <li>The body twitches briefly (don't panic; it's not coming back to life), then drops a <strong>Hemorrhetic Injector</strong>.</li>
 <li>The exit door at the far end of the room unlocks. The Sterilization Chamber awaits.</li>
</ol>

<div>
💡 <strong>Order matters.</strong> If you try to insert lungs before heart, the prompt rejects it. Heart always goes first.
</div>

<h2 id="reward">Reward + what's behind the door</h2>
<p>Direct rewards from the puzzle:</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>1× Hemorrhetic Injector</strong> (from the corpse — one-shot kill on most enemies)</li>
 <li><strong>"Anatomy 101" Bronze trophy</strong> unlock</li>
 <li>Access to the <strong>Sterilization Chamber</strong> (next puzzle: <a href="/blood-specimen-analyzer/">Blood Specimen Analyzer</a>)</li>
 <li>Eventual <strong>Quartz Piece</strong> drop (after solving the Blood Analyzer puzzle)</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="tips">Tips and gotchas</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>The cabinet key respawns nothing</strong>, once you use it, it's consumed.</li>
 <li><strong>The rolling ladder can get stuck</strong> if you push it before clearing the floor debris. Pick up the broken pipe first.</li>
 <li><strong>On Insanity, two zombies wander the corridor</strong> between Records Office and Anatomy Room. Knife-stealth or parry.</li>
 <li><strong>The corpse drop varies by difficulty</strong>. Standard: 1 Hemorrhetic Injector; Insanity: 2 + crafting materials.</li>
 <li><strong>Don't skip the Records Office</strong> — there's also a file ("Patient #047 Autopsy Notes") that counts toward the <a href="/all-75-files-locations-guide/">75 files trophy</a>.</li>
</ol>
