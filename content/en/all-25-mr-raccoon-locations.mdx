<p>All 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam locations in Resident Evil Requiem, organized by area in story order. Destroying all 25 unlocks the <em>"You Little Rascal!"</em> trophy and the <strong>Advanced Tuning</strong> upgrade tier for Leon's weapons (Tier 3 max upgrades).</p>

<div>
 <div>Quick reference</div>
 <p><strong>Total:</strong> 25 Mr. Raccoon Memoriam statues</p>
 <p><strong>How to destroy:</strong> Knife, gunshot, or grenade; all work. Knife is free.</p>
 <p><strong>Reward:</strong> "You Little Rascal!" trophy + Advanced Tuning (Tier 3 weapon upgrades)</p>
 <p><strong>Bonus:</strong> Some are missable. Once you leave an area, you cannot return.</p>
</div>

<div>
 ⚠ <strong>Important:</strong> Several Mr. Raccoons are character-locked. Some can only be reached by Grace, others only by Leon. Look for the character tag on each entry. Wear headphones — Memoriams emit a faint high-pitched chime when you're within ~5 meters.

<h2 id="carecenter">Care Center. Rhodes Hill <span>// #1-9</span></h2>

<p>The Rhodes Hill Care Center is the largest area in the game and contains the most Mr. Raccoons. <strong>Grab the Raccoon Roundup map</strong> from the Workshop safe (basement) using code <strong>R60-L40-R80</strong>, it marks all Care Center Memoriams on your map.</p>

<div>
 <div>#01</div>
 <div>
 <div>Fireplace mantel — Rehabilitation Ward<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>After Leon defeats the chainsaw maniac and zombie patients in the Rehabilitation Ward, look on the fireplace mantel in the hallway just south of the ward. <strong>The first Mr. Raccoon in the game.</strong> Has an attached note reading: "Hunt us, seek us! Find us, break us!" Unmissable. also unlocks the "The Hunt Begins" trophy.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#02</div>
 <div>
 <div>East Wing Lobby; Reception desk<span>GRACE</span></div>
 <div>Sitting on top of the receptionist's desk in the East Wing Lobby. Out in the open — easy to spot when you walk through.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#03</div>
 <div>
 <div>East Wing Stairwell. Table by grandfather clock<span>GRACE</span></div>
 <div>East of the Waiting Room, in the stairwell on a small table beside the grandfather clock. Easy to miss if you head straight upstairs.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#04</div>
 <div>
 <div>Kitchenette; Coffee machine<span>BOTH</span></div>
 <div>In the Kitchenette connected to the West Wing Office. On top of the coffee machine on the second floor.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#05</div>
 <div>
 <div>Medication Room — Locked cell beside Emily's<span>GRACE</span></div>
 <div>Inside the Level 3-locked cell adjacent to Emily's room. Behind the bed in the empty cell. <strong>Requires Level 3 wristband</strong> obtained from the Isolation Ward sequence.</div>
 <div>Missable if you progress past the Isolation Ward without backtracking.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#06</div>
 <div>
 <div>Records Room (2F). Warped cabinet<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Inside a cabinet with a warped door on the second floor. <strong>Only Leon can pry open warped cabinets</strong> with his hatchet, Grace cannot reach this one.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#07</div>
 <div>
 <div>Basement Bunkroom — On top of TV<span>GRACE</span></div>
 <div>In the cells beneath the Care Center, sitting on top of the television in the Bunkroom. Unmissable when you sweep the basement cells.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#08</div>
 <div>
 <div>Basement Workshop; On workbench<span>GRACE</span></div>
 <div>Sitting on the workbench in the Basement Workshop. Access via the vent opposite the basement forklift, or by powering the door from the holding cells.</div>
 <div>Same room as the Workshop Safe (R60-L40-R80); open it for the Raccoon Roundup map.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#09</div>
 <div>
 <div>Private Lab — Victor's office desk<span>GRACE</span></div>
 <div>In Victor's house Private Lab while searching for the helicopter key. On the desk near the Helicopter Keys pickup. <strong>Last chance before transitioning to Leon's chapter</strong>. destroy it before triggering the cutscene.</div>
 <div>Highly missable, easy to miss during the tense escape sequence.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<h2 id="raccooncity">Raccoon City <span>// #10-22</span></h2>

<p>Raccoon City is the largest combat zone with Mr. Raccoons spread across rooftops, sewers, and inside buildings. <strong>You'll need a sniper rifle</strong> (Marksman 1A from BSAA Container 03) for several long-distance shots in this area. A Raccoon Roundup map for Raccoon City is purchasable from the bonus menu post-story for a few hundred CP.</p>

<div>
 <div>#10</div>
 <div>
 <div>Front gate pedestal — Sniping support sequence<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>During the first sniper support sequence where Leon defends Grace and Emily on their way to the church. Aim down your scope, look to the <strong>left of the gate</strong> near a light fixture. You'll see a moving object on a pedestal. that's the Mr. Raccoon. Shoot it with the sniper rifle.</div>
 <div>If you miss it during sniping, you can sometimes return to ground level before entering the chapel. Grace cannot reach this earlier due to obstacle blocking.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#11</div>
 <div>
 <div>Cafe Oasis. Pine cabinet behind counter<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Inside Cafe Oasis on the route toward the Applegate Hotel. The Mr. Raccoon is in the pine cabinet behind the counter / bar area. If you miss it, you can return through the subway later.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#12</div>
 <div>
 <div>Underground Parking Garage — Side room crates<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>After visiting BSAA Central Camp, head into the Underground Parking Garage. In the side room (near the stealth kill prompt area), sitting on top of large plastic-wrapped crates.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#13</div>
 <div>
 <div>Bus interior. Driver's side window<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>From Central Camp, cross to the other side of the gap. There's a burned-out bus on the road north of Cedarbrook Apartments near the Gas Powered Generator. The Mr. Raccoon sits in the bus window on the driver's side.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#14</div>
 <div>
 <div>Broken sewer pipe, In the road hole<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>From the bus, continue forward. Use the crane near the gap to drop cargo and form a bridge. Once on the bridge vantage point, <strong>look down into the hole below</strong> — the Mr. Raccoon is on a broken sewer pipe at the bottom. Shoot from above. Can also be reached later from inside the sewer pipes near Ridgewood Station.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#15</div>
 <div>
 <div>Highway overpass, On top of a van<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>From the same bridge position, tilt your view nearly straight up. A van is parked on the overpass, the Mr. Raccoon is on top of the van. Snipe it from below by lining up the angle. Alternative shot: from Cedarbrook Apartments rooftop during the detonator search, facing west.</div>
 <div>Tricky shot — bring the Marksman 1A or any scoped weapon.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#16</div>
 <div>
 <div>Gas Station; Shelf opposite front door<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Inside the Gas Station in East Raccoon City, on the shelf directly opposite the front door. Sweep the interior, not just the pumps outside.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#17</div>
 <div>
 <div>Subway car / Train; On the seat<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>On your way to Ridgewood Station, head west into the subway car. The Mr. Raccoon sits on a seat on the north side of the train. Spiders spawn in this area — that's your indicator you're in the right spot.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#18</div>
 <div>
 <div>Willis Tower; Side room shelving<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>While moving through Willis Tower toward the first detonator section. After the first staircase, before continuing south (the correct path), <strong>head east (right)</strong> into the room at the end of the corridor. The Mr. Raccoon is on the shelving unit.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#19</div>
 <div>
 <div>Cedarbrook Apartments; Inside open fridge<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>After using the rusty crank/handle to progress in Cedarbrook Apartments. In the corridor area, look for an open fridge — the Mr. Raccoon is inside.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#20</div>
 <div>
 <div>Cedarbrook Stairwell. After ladder<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>After using the rusty crank on the gate on the balcony area, head up the ladder, take a right, and go inside the stairwell. The Mr. Raccoon is on the stairwell wall/ledge.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#21</div>
 <div>
 <div>Logistics Warehouse. Roof, on van<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>Once you've picked up the Disruptor from the Logistics Warehouse Roof, do a 180 and stand to the right of the elevator. Look directly ahead to the highway — the Mr. Raccoon is on the roof of a vehicle in front of a small lorry.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#22</div>
 <div>
 <div>Apartment window; Behind Gun Shop Kendo<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>In the alleyway behind Gun Shop Kendo, follow it to the end and look up. The Mr. Raccoon is in an apartment window <strong>to the left of the large Umbrella Corporation billboard</strong>.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 💡 <strong>Bonus #22b; RPD Library (some guides count this separately):</strong> Inside the Raccoon City Police Station library, behind the large central bookcase. Hidden behind a pile of books. Some collectible counters list this as #23 instead of the Kendo alley shot.
</div>

<h2 id="ark">ARK Facility <span>// #23-25</span></h2>

<p>The final area. Only 3 Mr. Raccoons here, but easy to miss in the rush toward the final boss. <strong>Sweep every room before the point of no return</strong> — once you trigger the Victor Gideon cutscene, you cannot return.</p>

<div>
 <div>#23</div>
 <div>
 <div>Staff Room hallway, Behind crates<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>From the first ARK save room (the Staff Room), head south. Just before you reach the steps leading to the Service Corridor, <strong>turn around and look behind the crates and cage</strong> you just walked past. The Mr. Raccoon is hidden behind them.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#24</div>
 <div>
 <div>First ARK hallway. On boxes behind trolleys<span>LEON</span></div>
 <div>In the first hallway of the ARK Facility, head to the end and turn right. The Mr. Raccoon sits on top of a box behind some hospital trolleys. Easy to miss if you don't sweep the corner.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>#25</div>
 <div>
 <div>Operations Room — Computer console<span>GRACE</span></div>
 <div><strong>The final Mr. Raccoon.</strong> Reach the Operations Room as Grace; the same room where you obtain the Noblesse Orb key item. The figure is on top of the computer console near the massive monitors on the southern wall. <strong>Watch out for Lickers</strong> in this area.</div>
 <div>Last chance for 100%, break this before triggering the final boss cutscene.</div>
 </div>
</div>

<h2 id="rewards">Rewards for finding all 25</h2>

<div>
 <div>What you unlock</div>
 <p><strong>1. "You Little Rascal!" trophy</strong> — Silver. Auto-unlocks on the 25th destruction.</p>
 <p><strong>2. Advanced Tuning</strong>; Permanently unlocks Tier 3 weapon upgrades for all Leon weapons at Supply Boxes. This is the highest upgrade tier in the game and required for the "Fully Loaded" trophy.</p>
 <p><strong>3. CP rewards</strong>, Each Mr. Raccoon destroyed grants Challenge Points. Spend at the Special Content Store on Infinite Ammo and the RPG-7.</p>
 <p><strong>4. "The Hunt Begins" trophy</strong> — Bronze. Unlocks on the very first Mr. Raccoon destruction (#1).</p>
</div>

<h2 id="tips">Tips for finding them faster</h2>

<div>
 🎧 <strong>Wear headphones.</strong> Each Memoriam emits a faint high-pitched chime when you're within about 5 meters. On TV speakers, it's easy to miss entirely.
</div>

<div>
 🗺️ <strong>Get the Raccoon Roundup map first.</strong> The Workshop Safe in the Care Center basement (code R60-L40-R80) contains a map that highlights all Care Center Mr. Raccoons on your map. A separate Raccoon City map is purchasable from the bonus menu post-story.
</div>

<div>
 📷 <strong>Check the Records menu regularly.</strong> The collectibles tracker shows your progress. If you finish an area and the count is below the expected total, return immediately before the point of no return.
</div>

<div>
 💀 <strong>Destroyed counts persist across deaths.</strong> If you break one and die before reaching a save point, it still counts as destroyed. Confirmed on PS5 and PC.
</div>
