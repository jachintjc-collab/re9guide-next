<h1>Lorekeeper — read all 47 audio logs</h1>
<p>Listen to all 47 audio logs in RE9. Audio logs are distinct from documents — they're recorded voice messages found at specific environmental locations. Listening to all 47 unlocks Lorekeeper.</p>

<blockquote>Lorekeeper is the audio version of Detective. Pursue for the voice acting, not just the trophy. Several audio logs are franchise-best voice performances.</blockquote>

<h2>The 47 audio logs by area</h2>
<p>Wrenwood: 9 logs (mostly Father Riley sermons + town historical recordings).</p>
<p>Rhodes Hill: 14 logs (Vivien Davies research dictation + her husband's voice memos).</p>
<p>Raccoon City: 8 logs (RPD archive recordings + Sherry Birkin BSAA briefings).</p>
<p>ARK: 16 logs (Commander X1 personal voice journal + BSAA staff briefings + Wesker historical recordings).</p>

<h2>The five most consequential audio logs</h2>
<p>"Vivien Davies — final dictation October 6" (Rhodes Hill chapter 5). Vivien's voice in her last clearly-recorded research entry. Lays out her ethical framework.</p>
<p>"Father Riley — confessional reflection 2023" (Wrenwood chapter 1, hidden). Henriksen's strongest voice performance.</p>
<p>"Commander X1 — integration day journal" (ARK chapter 7). His own voice recording of the integration choice.</p>
<p>"Sherry Birkin — BSAA briefing 2021" (RPD chapter 6). Karen Strassman as adult Sherry, planning the eventual RE9 institutional plot.</p>
<p>"Wesker — Project W observations 1978" (ARK chapter 7). Original Wesker voice recordings from childhood enhancement period.</p>

<h2>Audio log access mechanics</h2>
<p>Audio logs are interacted with the use button. They auto-play through to completion. Skipping the playback doesn't count for Lorekeeper. Must let each log play fully.</p>

<h2>Trophy timing</h2>
<p>Trophy fires at the 47th audio log completion. The menu tracks which logs have been fully heard.</p>

<h2>Insanity audio logs</h2>
<p>All accessible on Standard. Insanity doesn't add or remove audio logs.</p>

<h2>Korean voice acting reception</h2>
<p>Korean dub of audio logs received specific praise on Naver. Korean voice work for Vivien Davies audio logs in particular is considered among the best Korean RE voice work in franchise history.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Lorekeeper rewards patient audio engagement. The voice work alone justifies pursuing all 47. Don't skip — listen to each log. The voice performances are part of RE9's best work.</p>
