<p><strong>The Bulgarian Incident is RE9&rsquo;s biggest off-screen mystery.</strong> Eveline sighted in rural Bulgaria in 2023. Photographed. Photo appears in Mia&rsquo;s helicopter during RE9 cameo. This is the complete decode.</p>
<h2>Complete chronological breakdown</h2>
<div><table><tr><th>When</th><th>Event</th></tr><tr><td><strong>2023 first reports</strong></td><td>Rural Bulgarian villages report &ldquo;child apparitions&rdquo;. Initially dismissed as folklore. Bulgarian government quietly investigates.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2023 photograph</strong></td><td>A villager photographs a child matching Eveline&rsquo;s appearance. Photo distributed to intelligence networks. Reaches Ada Wong (then still Connections).</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2024 Ada investigation</strong></td><td>After defecting from Connections, Ada travels to Bulgaria. Investigates sighting locations. Photographs sealed area (later shown in Mia&rsquo;s helicopter).</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2024 Connections cover-up</strong></td><td>The Connections sends extraction team to suppress Bulgarian sightings. Local witnesses silenced. Photographs confiscated except Ada&rsquo;s copy.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2025 Mia briefed</strong></td><td>Mia Winters receives Bulgarian intelligence from Ada Wong indirectly. Adds photograph to her files. Carries it to RE9 helicopter cameo.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>February 2026 RE9 cameo</strong></td><td>Mia&rsquo;s helicopter EW-2017 has the Bulgarian photograph visible. Brief 2-second visual. Hidden in plain sight.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Photograph contents</strong></td><td>Eveline appears as small girl in rural countryside. Background: Bulgarian Orthodox church visible. Time of photo: estimated late autumn 2023.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Significance</strong></td><td>Confirms Eveline survived RE7. Confirms Vivien&rsquo;s 2009 prediction about recurring Eveline appearances. Sets up RE10 plot.</td></tr>
