<h1>Stealth Master — complete chapter 7 without being detected</h1>
<p>Complete the chapter 7 ARK facility infiltration without triggering any enemy detection alarm. Specific stealth-tracked encounters count; combat encounters don't affect detection state.</p>

<blockquote>Stealth Master rewards exploration of chapter 7's optional stealth design. Most players speedrun through; the stealth design rewards careful play.</blockquote>

<h2>What counts as detection</h2>
<p>Detection alarm triggered by ARK security cameras or guards. The alarm UI flashes when detection occurs. Any detected state for any duration disqualifies.</p>
<p>Combat encounters with enemies (Plant 43 mini-bosses, etc.) don't trigger detection. Only camera/guard awareness affects Stealth Master.</p>

<h2>Chapter 7 stealth zones</h2>
<p>BSL-2 corridor: security cameras on patrol pattern, requires timing.</p>
<p>Sub-level 1 administrative wing: guard patrols, requires distraction or routing.</p>
<p>BSL-3 transition airlock: dual camera coverage, requires diagonal sneak.</p>
<p>BSL-4 critical containment: heavy security, requires complete patrol memorization.</p>

<h2>Required loadout</h2>
<p>Quickstep Charm (faster stealth movement). Stealth-mode toggle equipped. No noisy weapons in primary slot (silenced pistol acceptable for non-stealth segments).</p>

<h2>Save management</h2>
<p>Save before each stealth zone entry. Reload on detection. Saves cost time but allow recovery.</p>

<h2>Insanity Stealth Master</h2>
<p>Insanity adds shorter patrol windows + faster guard reaction times. Substantially harder than Standard. NG+ recommended for Insanity attempts.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Stealth Master rewards stealth-game preferences. Don't pursue if you favor direct combat play styles. Standard difficulty achievable in 5-10 attempts for engaged players.</p>
