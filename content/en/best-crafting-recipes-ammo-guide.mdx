<h2>Blood Specimen recipes (Grace)</h2>

<p>The three Blood Specimen puzzles in the Care Center unlock progressively better crafting recipes. Solve them in order:</p>

<div>
 <div>Specimen 1; Denatured <span>DO IMMEDIATELY</span></div>
 <div>Click middle square <span>→</span> Recipe unlocked</div>
 <div>
 Located in the Blood Lab beside the Blood Collector. <strong>Solve this first thing on entering the lab.</strong> Unlocks: S&S M232 S&S M232 handgun Ammo recipe + Hemolytic Injector recipe. The Injector instant-kills Chunk and the Chef from behind.
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>Specimen 2, Converged <span>RECOMMENDED</span></div>
 <div>Top-middle <span>→</span> 2nd from bottom <span>=</span> 2 steps</div>
 <div>
 Found in the Ward Office (East Wing). Requires Level 1 ID Wristband. Unlocks: SMG Ammo recipe + Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol Magazine extender. Worth doing for the SMG ammo if you've found The Gal SMG.
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>Specimen 3 — Reversible <span>END-GAME</span></div>
 <div>Bot-left <span>→</span> middle <span>→</span> top-right <span>→</span> 2nd top <span>=</span> 4 steps</div>
 <div>
 Behind a security door in the Blood Lab. Requires Level 1 Wristband. Unlocks: Premium Hemolytic Injector (boss-tier damage) + High-Quality Blood crafting tier. Useful for late Grace sections only.
 </div>
</div>

<h2>Crafting recipe priorities</h2>

<h3>// HIGH PRIORITY (Always craft)</h3>

<div>
 <div>Hemolytic Injector <span>★★★★★</span></div>
 <div>Scrap ×1 <span>+</span> Infected Blood ×2 <span>=</span> 1 Injector</div>
 <div>
 The single most valuable craft. <strong>Instant-kills mini-bosses</strong> (Chef, Chunk) when used from behind. Stockpile 4-5 before Grace's combat-heavy sections.
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>S&S M232 S&S M232 handgun Ammo <span>★★★★</span></div>
 <div>Scrap ×1 <span>+</span> Infected Blood ×1 <span>=</span> 6 rounds</div>
 <div>
 Reliable bread-and-butter ammo. Craft whenever your S&S M232 handgun reserves drop below 20 rounds. Both Grace and Leon use this.
 </div>
</div>

<h3>// MEDIUM PRIORITY (Craft when available)</h3>

<div>
 <div>First Aid Spray <span>★★★</span></div>
 <div>Scrap ×2 <span>+</span> High-Grade Herb ×1 <span>=</span> 1 spray</div>
 <div>
 Full health restore. Save Scrap for crafting these in critical moments. Don't waste Scrap on minor crafts when you're at full HP.
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>SMG Ammo <span>★★★</span></div>
 <div>Scrap ×1 <span>+</span> Infected Blood ×1 <span>=</span> 12 rounds</div>
 <div>
 Best ammo-to-resource ratio. Craft if you're using The Gal SMG or Stiri REVO3 A1. Skip if you don't have an SMG equipped.
 </div>
</div>

<h3>// LOW PRIORITY (Skip unless needed)</h3>

<div>
 <div>W870 Police shotgun Shells <span>★★</span></div>
 <div>Scrap ×2 <span>+</span> Gunpowder ×1 <span>=</span> 4 shells</div>
 <div>
 Resource-expensive. Better to find shells in containers. Only craft for Leon's Care Center MSBG 500 sections if you're running low.
 </div>
</div>

<div>
 <div>Silencer 9 Silencer 9 pistol Magazine Extender <span>★</span></div>
 <div>Scrap ×3 <span>+</span> Premium Scrap ×1 <span>=</span> 1 attachment</div>
 <div>
 Permanent capacity boost for Grace's M232 or Blacktail. Worth crafting once for an Insanity run.
 </div>
</div>

<h2>Ammo management strategy</h2>

<h3>// THE 30/30/30 RULE</h3>

<p>Maintain 30 rounds in three weapon categories at all times:</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>S&S M232 S&S M232 handgun:</strong> 30 rounds (S&amp;S M232, Blacktail, or Silencer 9)</li>
 <li><strong>W870 Police shotgun:</strong> 30 shells (MSBG 500 or 990-TAC)</li>
 <li><strong>Special:</strong> 30 of your specialty (SMG rounds, sniper rounds, or Requiem)</li>
</ul>

<p>If any drops below 15, craft or loot before continuing. If above 50, stop crafting that type and focus elsewhere.</p>

<h3>// REQUIEM BULLETS, TREAT LIKE GOLD</h3>

<div>
 💡 <strong>Requiem Revolver rule:</strong> Never use Requiem rounds on standard zombies. Reserve them exclusively for boss weak points (Mr. X heart, Plant 43 flower, Victor's pustules). The Requiem one-shots most enemies, but you only get ~30 rounds in a full playthrough.
</div>

<h3>// HATCHET FINISHERS = FREE KILLS</h3>

<p>When an enemy is staggered (low HP, post-parry, or after a headshot), the Hatchet finisher prompt appears. <strong>Always use it</strong>, finishers cost no ammo, restore some stamina, and unlock the "Chop Chop" trophy. Leon-only ability.</p>

<h2>Hemolytic Injector tactics</h2>

<p>The Injector deserves its own section because it's that important:</p>

<h3>// USE CASES</h3>

<ul>
 <li><strong>The Chef</strong> — Sneak behind, inject. Instant kill. Drops Pantry Key.</li>
 <li><strong>Chunk #1 (East Wing)</strong>, 3 Injectors needed (use doorways to reset stealth)</li>
 <li><strong>Chunk #2 (Attic)</strong>. Less effective; he's faster. Use W870 Police shotgun instead.</li>
 <li><strong>Standard zombies</strong> — Wasteful. Use the knife or Silencer 9 pistol.</li>
</ul>

<h3>// HOW TO INJECT FROM BEHIND</h3>

<ol>
 <li>Approach from outside the enemy's vision cone</li>
 <li>Crouch within 2 meters</li>
 <li>The Injector prompt appears</li>
 <li>Press the action button. Grace performs an instant-kill animation</li>
</ol>

<h2>Insanity-specific crafting tips</h2>

<div>
 ⚠ On Insanity, ammo drops are halved. <strong>Crafting becomes mandatory</strong>, not optional. You cannot rely on world drops alone.
</div>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Solve all 3 Blood Specimens</strong> as Grace. every recipe matters</li>
 <li><strong>Loot every container</strong> for Scrap and Blood — even minor amounts add up</li>
 <li><strong>Skip optional bosses if low on Injectors</strong>; save resources for mandatory fights</li>
 <li><strong>Craft First Aid Sprays before bosses</strong>. you'll need them</li>
 <li><strong>Use the Hemolytic Injector on any mini-boss</strong> you encounter — they're worth it on Insanity</li>
</ul>

<h2>Trophy considerations</h2>

<ul>
 <li><strong>"The Power of Blood"</strong>, Bronze. Auto-unlocks on first Blood Collector craft.</li>
 <li><strong>"Science!"</strong>, Bronze. Solve any Blood Specimen.</li>
 <li><strong>"Order Up!"</strong> — Bronze. Defeat the Chef (Hemolytic Injector instant-kill is the easiest path).</li>
 <li><strong>"Eye Spy"</strong>; Bronze. Defeat Chunk for the charm (3× Hemolytic Injectors needed).</li>
</ul>
