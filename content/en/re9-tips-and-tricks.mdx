<h2 id="combat">Combat tips</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Aim for the head, but track parry timing</strong>; Headshots deal ~3x Silencer 9 pistol damage on Standard, but if a zombie is mid-lunge, parry first and counter for guaranteed stagger.</li>
 <li><strong>Hemorrhetic Injectors are one-shot kills from behind</strong> on most enemies — including The Chef.</li>
 <li><strong>The MSBG 500 W870 Police shotgun two-shots most zombies at point-blank</strong> but wastes ammo at mid-range. Save shells for crowd control.</li>
 <li><strong>Marksman 1A is the universal boss weapon</strong>, Plant 43 flower, Victor Gideon head pustules, Titan Spinner head, Mr. X heart.</li>
 <li><strong>The default Hatchet's parry window is too tight on Insanity</strong>, upgrade to Mortal Edge at every Supply Box.</li>
 <li><strong>Grenades break boss armor</strong> — Blister-borne cysts, Victor pustule clusters, Mr. X armor. Save 2-3 per boss arena.</li>
 <li><strong>Knife durability resets every save</strong> on the Infinite Knife unlock (Safe #5 reward).</li>
 <li><strong>Sprinting cancels all reload animations</strong>; useful for retreating mid-reload, but you lose the loaded round.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="exploration">Exploration tips</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Always tag save rooms</strong>, they double as item-box checkpoints. The typewriter ribbon resource is unlimited on Casual/Standard.</li>
 <li><strong>Investigate every glowing object</strong> — files, herbs, gunpowder, and hidden Mr. Raccoons all glow faintly.</li>
 <li><strong>Backtrack after picking up new keys</strong>. every key unlocks 2-4 previously-inaccessible rooms with significant loot.</li>
 <li><strong>The Care Center has 8 missable files</strong> tied to chapter progression. See our <a href="/re9-missables-complete-guide/">missables guide</a>.</li>
 <li><strong>Map markers persist across deaths</strong>; placed markers stay even if you die before saving. Useful for tracking incomplete collectibles.</li>
 <li><strong>Camera lighting reveals secrets</strong> — toggle the in-game flashlight rapidly in dark rooms to spot wall-mounted safes and hidden niches.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="crafting">Crafting tips</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Always craft Requiem rounds last</strong>; they need High-Grade Gunpowder + Chemical Flask, which are scarce. Save for Victor Gideon.</li>
 <li><strong>Combine green + red herbs</strong> for the most efficient healing.</li>
 <li><strong>Don't waste Hemorrhetic Injectors on regular zombies</strong>. reserve for instant boss kills.</li>
 <li><strong>The crafting bench unlocks new recipes after each boss</strong> — check after every boss kill.</li>
 <li><strong>Gunpowder limit is 99 per type</strong>, craft to cap before transitioning chapters.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="inventory">Inventory management</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Inventory slots are tight</strong>; drop the default Combat Knife once you have Mortal Edge Hatchet (Hatchet does both knife + parry).</li>
 <li><strong>Use the item box every save room</strong> — store excess ammo, carry only 1 First Aid Spray.</li>
 <li><strong>Carry exactly 1 grenade per boss arena</strong>. more is wasted, fewer means missing key armor breaks.</li>
 <li><strong>Key items don't count against inventory cap</strong>, collect them aggressively.</li>
 <li><strong>The Eye Spy Charm doesn't take a slot</strong> — equip permanently for Insanity (auto-saves from one fatal hit per save).</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="parry">Parry mastery tips</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>The parry window is the red flash on enemy weapons</strong>, press L1/LB exactly when the flash appears, not before.</li>
 <li><strong>Mortal Edge Hatchet has a 30% larger parry window</strong> than the default.</li>
 <li><strong>The Commander has 4 parryable attacks</strong> and 2 unparryable (grab, charge dash). Memorize the difference.</li>
 <li><strong>Successful parry = ~1.5s damage window</strong>, long enough for 1 W870 Police shotgun blast or 2 Marksman shots.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="insanity">Insanity-specific tips</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Eye Spy Charm is non-negotiable</strong> — saves you from one fatal hit per save.</li>
 <li><strong>Repair body armor at every Supply Box</strong>. Insanity damage values make broken armor lethal in 2 hits.</li>
 <li><strong>Save in 3+ slots throughout the run</strong>. rotate to avoid soft-locking on mid-fight states.</li>
 <li><strong>The Girl boss one-shots if you reload in shadow</strong> — always reload inside a lit zone.</li>
 <li><strong>Plant 43's seed projectiles deal 40-60% HP per hit</strong>; never stand still.</li>
 <li><strong>Infinite Ammo trivializes Insanity</strong>; Safe #5 reward (Infinite Knife) + trophy completion unlocks Infinite Requiem.</li>
</ol>
