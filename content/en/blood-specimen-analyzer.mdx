<h2 id="mechanic">How the analyzer works</h2>
<p>The Blood Specimen Analyzer sits on a steel pedestal in the center of the Sterilization Chamber. It has <strong>4 slots arranged left-to-right</strong> (positions 1, 2, 3, 4) and you pick up <strong>4 blood vials</strong> from the fridge along the north wall. Each vial is labeled with a blood type (A+, A−, B+, B−, O+, O−, AB+, AB−).</p>
<p>On the south wall there's a faded chart showing <strong>which blood type can transfuse into which</strong> (the universal donor/recipient rules). Your job is to place 4 vials so that <em>each vial in position N can transfuse into the vial in position N+1</em> — i.e. a "donation chain" from left to right.</p>

<div>
 <strong>⚠ COMMON MISTAKE:</strong> Players try to place all 4 of the same blood type. The chart is about <em>donation compatibility</em>, not matching types. Read the section below before you place anything.
</div>

<h2 id="solution">The solution (4 vials, left to right)</h2>
<p>You need a donation chain. The intended (and only) chain that uses the 4 vials Grace finds in the fridge is:</p>

<div>
 <strong>✓ CORRECT ORDER (left → right)</strong><br>
 <span>Slot 1: O−</span> →
 <span>Slot 2: A−</span> →
 <span>Slot 3: A+</span> →
 <span>Slot 4: AB+</span>
</div>

<p><strong>Why this works:</strong></p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>O− → A−</strong>: O− is universal donor, so it donates to A− ✓</li>
 <li><strong>A− → A+</strong>: A− can donate to A+ (negative donates to positive within same letter) ✓</li>
 <li><strong>A+ → AB+</strong>: A+ donates to AB+ ✓</li>
 <li><strong>AB+</strong> in slot 4, universal recipient, terminates the chain ✓</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="walkthrough">Step-by-step walkthrough</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Enter Sterilization Chamber</strong> via the Lab Access Card A door (Lower Lab → west corridor).</li>
 <li><strong>Pick up all 4 vials</strong> from the fridge on the north wall. The fridge contains: O−, A−, A+, AB+. You don't need any other vials.</li>
 <li><strong>Read the blood-type chart</strong> on the south wall (or just use the solution above).</li>
 <li><strong>Walk to the analyzer pedestal</strong> in the center.</li>
 <li><strong>Place vials in this order:</strong> Slot 1 = O−, Slot 2 = A−, Slot 3 = A+, Slot 4 = AB+.</li>
 <li><strong>Press the green Analyze button</strong>. Chamber hums for 8 sec, then lights turn green.</li>
 <li><strong>Lab Access Card B</strong> drops from a slot on the right side of the pedestal. Pick it up.</li>
 <li><strong>First Aid Spray</strong> spawns on the analyzer's top tray. Grab it.</li>
 <li><strong>Card B opens the next door</strong>, leads to the <a href="all-quartz-puzzle-solutions.html">Quartz Puzzle Hub</a>.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="tips">Tips &amp; what NOT to do</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Don't waste vials on wrong slots</strong> — placing a vial in the wrong slot doesn't consume it, but 2 wrong configurations triggers a 60-sec lockout. Use the chart.</li>
 <li><strong>O− goes in slot 1 always</strong>, universal donor is the only vial that can sit at the start of any chain.</li>
 <li><strong>AB+ goes in slot 4 always</strong>. universal recipient is the only vial that can sit at the end.</li>
 <li><strong>Slots 2 and 3 must bridge</strong> — A−/A+ pair is the only valid bridge with the vials Grace finds.</li>
 <li><strong>Insanity mode bonus</strong>: solving on the first try awards a <strong>Hemorrhetic Injector</strong> in the analyzer's bottom drawer (skip if you got 1+ wrong tries).</li>
 <li><strong>NG+ wisdom</strong>: the fridge contents don't change between difficulties, the solution is always O− → A− → A+ → AB+.</li>
</ol>
