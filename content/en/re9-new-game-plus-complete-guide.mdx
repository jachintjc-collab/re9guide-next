<h1>RE9 New Game Plus complete guide — what carries, what resets, optimal NG+ strategy</h1>
<p>RE9's New Game Plus mode inherits weapons, charms, currency, and weapon attachments from your completion save. Collectibles, documents, and audio logs reset. This complete guide covers inheritance rules, optimal NG+ strategies for different goals (speedrun, trophy completion, story exploration), and the specific NG+ exclusive content most players never see.</p>

<blockquote>NG+ is RE9's most-rewarding second-playthrough mode. Don't skip the inherited weapons; don't try to NG+ with vanilla loadout. The mode is designed around inheritance.</blockquote>

<h2>What carries to NG+ from your completion save</h2>
<p>Weapons: all unlocked weapons carry forward, including Hand Cannon Prototype (Insanity completion unlock) and Mortal Edge knife (Insanity completion unlock). Weapon attachments and upgrades also carry.</p>
<p>Charms: all 18 charms carry, including charm slots already filled. Active charm loadout carries to NG+.</p>
<p>Currency: full currency balance carries. Merchants in NG+ have inflated prices to balance for inherited currency, but the inflation is roughly 30% — not enough to negate inheritance value.</p>
<p>Weapon attachments: all purchased and looted attachments carry. Stack mods (e.g., extended magazine, recoil compensator) carry.</p>

<h2>What resets in NG+</h2>
<p>Story progress: full reset. NG+ starts from chapter 1 opening cinematic.</p>
<p>Documents: all 89 documents reset. You must re-read for the Detective trophy if not earned previously.</p>
<p>Audio logs: all 47 audio logs reset. You must re-listen for the Lorekeeper trophy if not earned previously.</p>
<p>Mr. Raccoon plushies: all 25 reset. Re-collection required for All Raccoons trophy if not previously earned.</p>
<p>Treasures: all 18 reset. Re-collection required for Treasure Hunter trophy if not previously earned.</p>
<p>Dialogue choice history: all dialogue branches reset. Earn different endings in NG+ runs.</p>

<h2>NG+ exclusive content</h2>
<p>Dialogue meta-awareness: chapter 1 Grace dialogue includes meta-recognition that player has been here before. Specific lines change between first playthrough and NG+. Korean Naver community first documented this in March 2026.</p>
<p>Chapter 5 Vivien NG+ recognition: Vivien's chapter 5 dialogue includes a line about Grace returning. The implication is that Vivien somehow recognizes Grace's prior experience. Reading this as metafiction or actual narrative element is up to player interpretation.</p>
<p>Chapter 7 Leon NG+ acknowledgment: Leon's chapter 7 farewell dialogue includes acknowledgment of player's previous completion. Smaller meta touch but emotionally rewarding for engaged players.</p>
<p>Rocket Launcher: NG+ unlocks the Rocket Launcher in inventory at chapter 1 start. 5 total rockets per NG+ playthrough.</p>

<h2>Optimal NG+ strategies by goal</h2>
<p>Speedrun goal: target sub-4-hour completion. NG+ with cutscene skip + optimal routing achievable. See speedrun-guide-sub-4-hours for detailed route. Trophy reward: Speedrunner.</p>
<p>Trophy completion goal: NG+ enables most challenging trophies. True Survivor requires 100% Insanity NG+. Knife Only Insanity requires Mortal Edge inheritance. Pacifist requires NG+ stealth knowledge.</p>
<p>Story exploration goal: NG+ allows exploring alternative endings without first-playthrough story commitment. Use save scumming at chapter 8 final decision to access multiple endings efficiently.</p>
<p>Combat practice goal: NG+ with all weapons available lets you experiment with builds without first-playthrough difficulty pressure.</p>

<h2>Recommended NG+ playthrough order</h2>
<p>First NG+: pursue trophy completion goals you missed in first playthrough. Use inherited equipment to clean up trophy backlog efficiently. Target trophies: Detective, Lorekeeper, All Charms, All Raccoons, Mosaic.</p>
<p>Second NG+ (NG++): pursue alternative endings. Use save scumming at branch points. Target endings: Grace true ending, Leon true ending, Father Riley confession ending, Secret ending (if eligible).</p>
<p>Third NG+ (NG+++): pursue Insanity for ultimate completion. Use Hand Cannon and Mortal Edge knife inherited from previous Insanity completion if available. Target: True Survivor trophy.</p>

<h2>Charm reallocation strategy in NG+</h2>
<p>Standard NG+ playthrough recommended loadout: Endurance + Marksman's Eye + Quickstep. Optimized for trophy hunting and exploration.</p>
<p>Knife-only NG+ playthrough: Mortal Edge + Knife Edge + Quickstep. Note that Knife Edge stacks with Mortal Edge base damage; combined damage is roughly 3.4x base knife damage on critical hits.</p>
<p>No-heal NG+ playthrough: Endurance + Phoenix Down + Survivor's Will. Damage mitigation focus.</p>
<p>Speedrun NG+ playthrough: Quickstep + Marksman's Eye + Magnetic Hand. Movement and combat efficiency.</p>

<h2>NG+ chapter pacing</h2>
<p>Chapter 1: 15-25 minutes with cutscene skip. Skip Brass Whistle collection if already obtained.</p>
<p>Chapter 2: 20-30 minutes. Skip Mr. Raccoon collection unless completionist goal.</p>
<p>Chapter 3: 25-35 minutes. Boss encounters dramatically easier with Hand Cannon (1-2 rounds to defeat Vivien Plant 43).</p>
<p>Chapter 4: 20-30 minutes with forest glitch + cutscene skip.</p>
<p>Chapter 5: 25-35 minutes. Quartz puzzle remains problematic regardless of NG+ status.</p>
<p>Chapter 6: 45-60 minutes. Mr. X chase still requires precise routing.</p>
<p>Chapter 7: 35-50 minutes. Commander X1 boss easier with Hand Cannon. Skip dialogue cutscenes for trophy-focused runs; keep them for story exploration runs.</p>
<p>Chapter 8: 35-55 minutes. Eveline final boss easier with Hand Cannon. Final ending dialogue depends on prior chapter 6 archive completion.</p>

<h2>NG+ specific trophies</h2>
<p>NG+ Master: complete one NG+ playthrough. The simplest NG+ trophy.</p>
<p>Speed Demon: complete in under 6 hours. NG+ casual speedrun.</p>
<p>Speedrunner: complete in under 4 hours. NG+ optimized speedrun. Korean Naver routing required.</p>
<p>True Survivor: 100% on Insanity. Requires NG+ Insanity playthrough.</p>
<p>Grace True Ending: requires specific dialogue + chapter 6 archive reading. NG+ doesn't carry archive read status, so re-read required.</p>

<h2>Common NG+ mistakes</h2>
<p>Selling inherited weapons: don't do this. The currency value is low; the weapon retention value is high.</p>
<p>Skipping dialogue cutscenes for first NG+: don't do this. NG+ meta-aware dialogue is meaningful content. Skip cutscenes on third or fourth playthrough, not second.</p>
<p>Attempting Insanity NG+ as first NG+: don't do this. Build NG+ familiarity on Casual or Standard first.</p>
<p>Not enabling cloud backup: critical mistake. Save corruption on long NG+ runs destroys hours of progress.</p>

<h2>Final NG+ recommendation</h2>
<p>If you completed RE9 first playthrough on Standard: do first NG+ on Hardcore to bridge difficulty before attempting Insanity. The inheritance helps; difficulty step matters.</p>
<p>If you completed first playthrough on Insanity: NG+ on Standard for relaxed trophy hunting. Save Insanity NG+ for True Survivor pursuit specifically.</p>
<p>If you completed on Casual: NG+ on Standard. Build skills before attempting harder modes.</p>
