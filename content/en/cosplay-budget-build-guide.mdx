<h1>RE9 cosplay on a budget — what to spend on and what to skip</h1>
<p>RE9 cosplay can run $50 or $5,000 depending on choices. Here's how to allocate budget when you have limits.</p>

<blockquote>Most RE9 cosplays don't need premium materials. Spend on what cameras see; skip what they don't.</blockquote>

<h2>What to spend on</h2><p>Wig, weathering paint, accurate footwear. These read on camera at any distance.</p><h2>What to skip</h2><p>Interior costume linings, accurate undergarments, custom-tailored fitting. Cameras don't see these.</p><h2>Korean cosplay community guidance</h2><p>Korean RE cosplay scene on Naver has detailed budget breakdowns. Korean cosplayers report ~$80-150 average per RE9 character build with thrift store sourcing.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Budget cosplay works if you focus on photographable details. RE9 character designs are forgiving for budget builds because most rely on weathered tactical aesthetics.</p>
