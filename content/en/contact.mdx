<h2>What I can help with</h2>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Guide corrections</strong>; incorrect codes, missing locations, outdated strategies</li>
 <li><strong>Content suggestions</strong> — guides you'd like to see written</li>
 <li><strong>Partnership inquiries</strong>; collaborations, content sharing, media outreach</li>
 <li><strong>Technical issues</strong>, broken links, page errors, mobile display problems</li>
</ul>

<h2>What I can't help with</h2>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Game support</strong> — bugs, crashes, save corruption (contact Capcom support)</li>
 <li><strong>Account issues</strong>; PSN, Xbox Live, Steam (contact the platform)</li>
 <li><strong>Spoiler-heavy questions in public</strong>; if your question has spoilers, mention "SPOILER" in the subject line</li>
 <li><strong>Personal coaching</strong> — I don't offer 1-on-1 gameplay help, but I'm happy to expand a guide based on your question</li>
</ul>
