<h2>How Antique Coins work</h2>

<p>Antique Coins are exclusive to Grace's character, Leon cannot pick them up or use them. They serve as the Parlor merchant's primary currency.</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Spending location:</strong> The Parlor (West Wing 1F)</li>
 <li><strong>Trophy:</strong> "Coin Collector" Bronze unlocks on first Parlor purchase</li>
 <li><strong>Carry-over:</strong> Unspent coins do NOT carry over to NG+</li>
 <li><strong>Total in game:</strong> 22 coins, 4-5 more than needed to buy everything</li>
</ul>

<h2>What to spend coins on at the Parlor</h2>

<p>The Parlor merchant offers various items in exchange for Antique Coins:</p>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Weapon attachments</strong>; Scopes, extended mags, suppressors (3-5 coins each)</li>
 <li><strong>Rare crafting materials</strong>. Premium Scrap, High-Quality Blood (2-3 coins each)</li>
 <li><strong>First Aid Sprays</strong> — At a discount vs. crafting (2 coins each)</li>
 <li><strong>Hemolytic Injectors</strong>, Pre-crafted for Grace (3 coins each)</li>
 <li><strong>Cosmetic items</strong>. Outfits and weapon skins for post-game (5-10 coins)</li>
</ul>

<div>
 💡 <strong>Priority spending:</strong> Buy weapon attachments first. The extended magazine for the Blacktail and the suppressor for stealth S&S M232 handgun use are the biggest gameplay improvements.
</div>

<h2>Missable warnings</h2>

<div>
 ⚠ <strong>Missable coins:</strong> The RPD Library coin (#16) is locked behind the Mr. X chase. If you don't return after defeating Super Tyrant, you can't get it. The Underground Parking Garage coin (#17) requires draining water — if you skip the battery puzzle, you can't reach it.
</div>

<h2>Coin Collector trophy</h2>

<p>The "Coin Collector" Bronze trophy unlocks the moment you spend your <strong>first</strong> Antique Coin at the Parlor. You don't need to find all 22. even a single 2-coin Hemolytic Injector purchase triggers it.</p>
