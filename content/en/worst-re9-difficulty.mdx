<h1>Worst RE9 difficulty design — Hardcore specifically</h1>
<p>RE9 offers four difficulty modes: Casual, Standard, Hardcore, Insanity. Hardcore is the worst-tuned, sitting between Standard and Insanity without distinct identity.</p>

<blockquote>Hardcore difficulty serves no clear audience. Standard and Insanity are well-designed. Hardcore should be removed from RE10.</blockquote>

<h2>What Hardcore does</h2><p>Enemy HP +30% over Standard. Damage +25%. Slightly reduced healing drops. No other major changes.</p><h2>Why it fails</h2><p>Standard players who want challenge go to Insanity. Insanity players don't want Hardcore's reduced challenge. Hardcore is gatekept from neither audience.</p><h2>The Korean tier</h2><p>Korean community lists Hardcore as the franchise's most-skipped difficulty across all RE games.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>Three-mode design (Casual / Standard / Insanity) serves RE9 better than four-mode. RE10 should drop Hardcore.</p>
