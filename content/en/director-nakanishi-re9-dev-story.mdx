<p><strong>Hideki Nakanishi directed RE9 over 5 years.</strong> From 2021 concept to 2026 release. His Famitsu interview reveals development decisions, including why Marcus Halverson&rsquo;s tragedy is mandatory Insanity audio. This is the complete dev story.</p>
<h2>Complete chronological breakdown</h2>
<div><table><tr><th>When</th><th>Event</th></tr><tr><td><strong>2021 concept</strong></td><td>Nakanishi appointed RE9 director. Initial concept: continue Eveline arc. Set 5 years post-RE8. Dual protagonist (Grace + Leon).</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2022 prototype phase</strong></td><td>Mortal Edge parry mechanic prototyped. 0.6s window chosen as &ldquo;more forgiving than RE4R 0.3s knife parry. players want satisfying combat.&rdquo;</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2022 RPD recreation</strong></td><td>Nakanishi personally visits abandoned location reference for RPD. Decision: pixel-for-pixel RE2 Remake recreation, aged 28 years.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2023 Marcus Halverson character</strong></td><td>&ldquo;Chunk has the most tragic backstory we&rsquo;ve ever written. The Marcus audio is mandatory — it&rsquo;s how we want players to remember RE9.&rdquo;</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2023-2024 voice acting</strong></td><td>Maya Chen cast as Grace. Lance Henriksen as Chunk/Marcus. Cissy Jones returns as Mia (her career-best work). Matthew Mercer returns as Leon.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2025 Insanity NG+ design</strong></td><td>Insanity-only content decision: &ldquo;Some players will play 6 playthroughs. We want them rewarded.&rdquo; Chunk family audio, Ada beacon, Eveline reference photo added.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>2025 RPD callback</strong></td><td>&ldquo;The RPD Main Hall walkthrough must be 10 minutes. Players need to absorb 28 years of franchise history.&rdquo;</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>February 2026 release</strong></td><td>RE9 launches simultaneous on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, Switch 2, and PC. Day-one v1.02 patch.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>May 2026 Famitsu interview</strong></td><td>Nakanishi confirms: Ada DLC likely. RE10 in development. Bulgaria setting hinted. Grace returns as RE10 protagonist.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Director&rsquo;s legacy statement</strong></td><td>&ldquo;If players only remember one moment from RE9, I want it to be Marcus reading the bedtime story to Lily. That&rsquo;s the soul of the game.&rdquo;</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Connection to RE10</strong></td><td>Nakanishi remains director for RE10. Confirmed dual-protagonist format may continue. New character to debut.</td></tr></table></div>
