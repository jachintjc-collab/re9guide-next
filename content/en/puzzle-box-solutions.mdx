<h2 id="mechanic">How RE9 puzzle boxes work</h2>
<p>Each puzzle box has 4 faces with carved symbols. Rotate the box (left-stick on controller, mouse-drag on PC) to align the correct symbol to the gold-rimmed receptacle on each side. When all 4 sides match correctly, the top lid pops open.</p>
<p>The symbol set is the same across both boxes (8 symbols total: Sun, Moon, Eye, Skull, Snake, Tree, Heart, Star), but the correct combination is different per box and per playthrough difficulty.</p>

<h2 id="box1">Puzzle Box #1, Chairman's Office (Care Center)</h2>
<p>Located on the chairman's desk in the East Wing, second floor. Accessible after Chunk #1 boss fight. Reward: <strong>SMG Suppressor attachment</strong>.</p>

<h3>// SOLUTION (Standard/Normal)</h3>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Top face:</strong> Sun</li>
 <li><strong>Right face:</strong> Moon</li>
 <li><strong>Bottom face:</strong> Eye</li>
 <li><strong>Left face:</strong> Skull</li>
</ul>

<h3>// SOLUTION (Insanity)</h3>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Top face:</strong> Eye</li>
 <li><strong>Right face:</strong> Sun</li>
 <li><strong>Bottom face:</strong> Snake</li>
 <li><strong>Left face:</strong> Moon</li>
</ul>

<div>
💡 <strong>Hint location:</strong> The chairman's desk drawer contains a journal page showing the symbol order. Read it before solving, saves trial-and-error.
</div>

<h2 id="box2">Puzzle Box #2 — Lead Researcher's Office (ARK Facility)</h2>
<p>Located in the ARK Facility's lab levels, on the lead researcher's bookshelf. Accessible after The Commander boss fight. Reward: <strong>W870 Police shotgun Choke attachment</strong> (tightens MSBG 500 spread, +30% effective range).</p>

<h3>// SOLUTION (Standard/Normal)</h3>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Top face:</strong> Tree</li>
 <li><strong>Right face:</strong> Heart</li>
 <li><strong>Bottom face:</strong> Star</li>
 <li><strong>Left face:</strong> Skull</li>
</ul>

<h3>// SOLUTION (Insanity)</h3>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Top face:</strong> Heart</li>
 <li><strong>Right face:</strong> Skull</li>
 <li><strong>Bottom face:</strong> Eye</li>
 <li><strong>Left face:</strong> Tree</li>
</ul>

<div>
💡 <strong>Hint location:</strong> The lead researcher's desk has a notebook taped under the second drawer. Pull the drawer fully out to find it.
</div>

<h2 id="tips">Puzzle box solving tips</h2>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Don't brute-force.</strong> The symbol combinations are puzzle-specific. your Care Center solution won't work in the ARK box.</li>
 <li><strong>Find the hint papers first.</strong> Both puzzle boxes have hint documents in the same room. Look for them before touching the box.</li>
 <li><strong>Rotation is intuitive.</strong> Left-stick (controller) or mouse-drag (PC) rotates one face at a time. The box stays centered.</li>
 <li><strong>Wrong combinations don't punish you</strong>; no damage, no time penalty. Just keep trying.</li>
 <li><strong>The Insanity solutions are different.</strong> If you're replaying on Insanity, the codes above are confirmed for v1.02.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="missable">Missable warning</h2>
<div>
⚠ <strong>Both puzzle boxes are 100% missable.</strong> Once you leave the Care Center for the Courtyard, Puzzle Box #1 is permanently locked for that playthrough. Same for Puzzle Box #2 once you trigger the Victor Gideon cutscene.
