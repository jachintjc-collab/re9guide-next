<h1>Worst RE9 inventory management — the persistent franchise complaint</h1>
<p>RE9 inherits RE's classic limited-inventory design. The implementation is fine; the philosophy is dated.</p>

<blockquote>RE9 inventory management is acceptable but the franchise should consider modernization for RE10. Not every player wants Tetris with item slots.</blockquote>

<h2>What works</h2><p>Visual inventory grid is clear. Hotbar for combat is responsive. Quick-equip menu functional.</p><h2>What doesn't</h2><p>Slot allocation requires constant attention. Inventory expansion gates content unnecessarily. Specific items take inappropriately many slots.</p><h2>The crafting interaction</h2><p>Crafting + inventory management together create cognitive load that distracts from combat focus. Either should be simplified.</p><h2>My take</h2><p>RE9 inventory works for RE veterans. New players struggle. RE10 should offer an optional simplified inventory mode.</p>
