<h1>No Heal — complete chapter 3 without using any healing items</h1>
<p>Complete the chapter 3 Care Center sequence without using any healing items (medicinal herbs, first aid sprays, etc.). Damage you take cannot be healed via items during chapter 3.</p>

<blockquote>No Heal is the franchise's most demanding chapter-specific restriction trophy. Mortal Edge parry mastery essential.</blockquote>

<h2>What restricts</h2>
<p>No use of any healing item during chapter 3. This includes: Green Herbs, Red Herbs, Yellow Herbs, First Aid Spray, any Charm-activated healing.</p>
<p>Charm passive damage reduction (Endurance) doesn't count as healing — it modifies damage taken, doesn't restore HP.</p>

<h2>Chapter 3 health management</h2>
<p>The chapter is roughly 45-60 minutes of gameplay with multiple encounter types. Without healing, Grace must avoid damage through positioning and parry execution.</p>
<p>The Plant 43 boss at chapter end is the main challenge. Boss damage is unhealable; must reach the kill with sufficient HP buffer.</p>

<h2>Required preparation</h2>
<p>Endurance Charm equipped (essential — converts some attacks to non-lethal damage).</p>
<p>Marksman's Eye Charm equipped (faster combat resolution = less exposure time).</p>
<p>W870 Police shotgun fully upgraded (stagger compensates for parry mistakes).</p>

<h2>Insanity No Heal</h2>
<p>Insanity No Heal Chapter 3 is one of the franchise's hardest restriction trophies. Plant 43 boss on Insanity with no healing requires near-flawless parry execution and ammo conservation.</p>

<h2>Korean No Heal community</h2>
<p>Korean speedrun community has chapter 3 No Heal route documentation with frame-precise parry timing. The Korean coverage is essential reading for pursuing this trophy.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>No Heal requires combat mastery rather than save management. Practice Plant 43 parry timing extensively before attempting. The trophy unlocks at chapter 3 completion — single-chapter focus, doesn't affect overall save.</p>
