<p><strong>The Girl Insanity is the hardest pre-Chapter 8 fight.</strong> Cannot damage in alert state. Bloodhound charm mandatory. Magnum windows are 0.8s each. Stay on platforms. water = 40% HP/sec.</p>

<h2>Insanity-specific requirements</h2>
<p>Before engaging The Girl on Insanity NG+, equip the recommended charm loadout and stock the recommended weapons. This fight punishes underprepared players.</p>

<h2>Required charm loadout (Insanity NG+)</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Crafter&rsquo;s Touch</strong>; ammo crafting +25%</li>
 <li><strong>Iron Skin</strong> — -15% damage reduction</li>
 <li><strong>Phoenix Down</strong>, revive once per chapter at 50% HP</li>
 <li><strong>4th NG+ slot:</strong> Marksman&rsquo;s Eye OR Survivor&rsquo;s Will based on phase</li>
</ul>

<h2>Required weapons</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Requiem Revolver T3</strong> + Infinite Magnum charm (NG+ unlock)</li>
 <li><strong>W870 Police shotgun T3</strong> for close phases</li>
 <li><strong>Mortal Edge</strong> for parry timing</li>
 <li><strong>Clatter Carbine T2</strong> as backup DPS</li>
</ul>

<h2>Frame-perfect timing data</h2>
<p>Each boss has specific attack windows requiring sub-second precision. Practice on Standard difficulty first to memorize the patterns, then attempt Insanity NG+ with confidence.</p>

<h2>Common Insanity NG+ mistakes</h2>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Not equipping Phoenix Down</strong>; One mistake = death without it</li>
 <li><strong>Healing mid-phase</strong> — Wastes spray, phase transitions reset arena</li>
 <li><strong>Wrong weapon for phase</strong>. Match weapon to mechanic</li>
 <li><strong>Panic parries</strong>; Mistimed parry = punishment damage</li>
</ul>

<h2>Insanity-only rewards</h2>
<p>Defeating The Girl on Insanity NG+ unlocks specific audio logs, codex entries, and trophy progress. Many players miss these because they only play Standard difficulty.</p>
