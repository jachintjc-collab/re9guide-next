<h1>Rocket Launcher (NG+) + Phoenix Down — combo analysis</h1>
<p>Pairing Rocket Launcher (NG+) with Phoenix Down for the 5-rocket emergency tool niche. Honest assessment of when this works and when it doesn't.</p>

<blockquote>This combo rewards skilled play but doesn't rescue weak fundamentals. Solid mid-tier choice, not meta-defining.</blockquote>

<h2>Why this combo actually works</h2>
<p>Rocket Launcher (NG+) fills the 5-rocket emergency tool niche. Phoenix Down contributes auto-revive once per encounter at 1 HP. The pairing matters because the charm's effect lines up with where the weapon's natural damage curve has gaps.</p>

<h2>Effectiveness rating</h2>
<p>On Standard: A-tier. On Insanity: B+. The drop on Insanity reflects ammo and survival pressure rather than the combo's mechanical synergy.</p>

<h2>Best chapters to use this combo</h2>
<p>Chapters 3-6 reward this combo most directly. Chapter 3 Care Center encounters fit the 5-rocket emergency tool profile. Chapter 6 RPD layout creates the precise scenarios the charm boost was designed for.</p>

<h2>Where it underperforms</h2>
<p>The weapon's weakness — 5 total rockets per playthrough — isn't fixed by the charm. If you need to compensate for that weakness specifically, a different charm pairing serves better.</p>

<h2>Korean meta tier</h2>
<p>Korean Naver community combo lists rank this pairing as A- on the standard meta. The Korean speedrun category specific to this loadout sits in mid-pack — not optimal but not bottom tier either.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>This combo rewards players who already understand the weapon's strengths and want to amplify them. It does not rescue the weapon's weaknesses. Use it after you've decided you like the weapon, not as a way to discover whether you like the weapon.</p>
