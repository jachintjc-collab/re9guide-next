<h1>Grace Ashcroft — full character profile + protagonist analysis</h1>
<p>Grace Ashcroft is RE9's primary protagonist. BSAA contract investigator, late 30s, originally from a Raccoon City-adjacent town. Her character work is the strongest RE protagonist writing since Leon in RE2.</p>

<blockquote>Grace is RE's best new protagonist in 20 years. The character writing is restrained in a way the franchise rarely manages.</blockquote>

<h2>Background</h2>
<p>Grace was born in 1992 in a Massachusetts town near Raccoon City. Her older brother (referenced in chapter 1 photo, fully revealed in chapter 8) was 9 years older. Grace was 9 years old during the 1998 Raccoon City outbreak. Her brother's official cause of death was given as Raccoon City incident victim. The chapter 8 Eveline reveal contradicts this.</p>

<h2>Career trajectory</h2>
<p>Grace joined BSAA as contract investigator in 2018, age 26. Specializes in cold-case bioterror investigations. The Wrenwood Care Center case is her 47th BSAA contract.</p>

<h2>Voice work</h2>
<p>Cissy Jones voices Grace (English). Strong performance throughout, with restraint as the defining quality. The "I'll think about it" line in chapter 1 establishes her character tone.</p>

<h2>Korean dub</h2>
<p>Korean VA 박지윤 delivers parallel performance. Korean reception of Grace was strongly positive — the Korean community has been ready for a female RE protagonist for years.</p>

<h2>Story arc</h2>
<p>Grace begins as professional investigator with personal investment she doesn't acknowledge. The chapter 8 brother truth recontextualizes her entire investigation. By RE10 setup, she's allied with Sherry Birkin (or Leon, depending on true ending route) against BSAA institutional faction.</p>

<h2>RE10 trajectory</h2>
<p>Grace will return in RE10 as supporting character. The Korean BSAA field commander (post-credits) takes primary protagonist position. Grace remains active in the institutional opposition plot.</p>

<h2>My take</h2>
<p>Grace works because the writing trusts her without dramatizing her. Compare to recent RE protagonists who needed exposition dumps to establish character. Grace establishes character through behavior.</p>
