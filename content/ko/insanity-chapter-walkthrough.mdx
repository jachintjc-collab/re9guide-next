<h2 id="prereq">Prerequisites and unlock</h2>

<p>Insanity is locked until you complete the main campaign on any difficulty. After credits, the option appears as a third difficulty tile on the NG+ menu. Full unlock details and prep checklist are in our <a href="/ko/how-to-unlock-insanity-difficulty/">Insanity difficulty unlock guide</a>.</p>

<p>Before starting, run the <a href="/ko/re9-insanity-readiness-check/">Insanity readiness check</a>; it confirms you have the carry-forward weapons (Marksman 1A, Mortal Edge Hatchet, infinite-ammo bonuses if earned) and at least one save slot dedicated to a fresh Insanity run.</p>

<div>
 ⚠ <strong>No auto-save.</strong> Insanity disables checkpoints entirely. If you die between typewriters you restart at the last manual save. Save at every typewriter. every single one.
</div>

<h2 id="ch1">Chapter 1 — Care Center West (preserve ammo, parry practice)</h2>

<p>The opening Care Center West Wing chapter is your low-stress runway for learning Insanity's new damage values. Conserve ammo aggressively. you will need 15+ S&S M232 handgun rounds banked for The Chef.</p>

<h3>// AMMO DISCIPLINE</h3>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Skip every avoidable zombie</strong>; most Bar &amp; Lounge encounters can be sprinted past</li>
 <li><strong>Practice knife parries</strong> on lone zombies — parry stagger lets you knife-execute without ammo</li>
 <li><strong>Pick up every gunpowder</strong> for crafting; see <a href="/ko/best-crafting-recipes-ammo-guide/">crafting recipes</a></li>
 <li><strong>Save the Hemorrhetic Injector</strong>; single-use heal, do not waste before Chef fight</li>
</ul>

<p>End of chapter brings you to The Chef encounter in the Care Center kitchen. See <a href="/ko/how-to-beat-the-chef/">Chef boss guide</a> for the freezer corner exploit that works on every difficulty.</p>

<h2 id="ch2">Chapter 2 — Care Center East + Basement (Chef, Chunk #1, blood specimen puzzle)</h2>

<p>The middle Care Center chapter is the first Insanity wall. Chunk #1 (the larger variant) appears in the East Wing 2F corridor and one-shots you if you panic-shoot instead of parry-rolling.</p>

<h3>// CHUNK ROUTE</h3>

<ol>
 <li><strong>Lure Chunk into the Examination Room.</strong> Tight walls cancel his charge attack.</li>
 <li><strong>Use W870 Police shotgun shells, not S&S M232 handgun.</strong> Five W870 Police shotgun rounds drop him; thirty S&S M232 handgun rounds barely scratch.</li>
 <li><strong>Reserve the Pharmacy's First Aid Spray</strong> for this fight only. do not pick up early.</li>
</ol>

<p>Full Chunk strategy in the <a href="/ko/how-to-beat-chunk/">Chunk boss guide</a>. Basement section requires the blood specimen puzzle. solution path is in the <a href="/ko/all-quartz-puzzle-solutions/">quartz puzzle solutions</a> page (same mechanic, different shrine).</p>

<h2 id="ch3">Chapter 3 — Courtyard (Blister-borne, sewer routing)</h2>

<p>Courtyard opens with the Blister-borne ambush. On Insanity, three Blister-borne spawn simultaneously instead of two, and their pustule burst has a 1.3× larger radius.</p>

<h3>// BLISTER-BORNE STRATEGY</h3>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Stay on the high planter wall</strong>; Blister-borne cannot climb it</li>
 <li><strong>Marksman 1A headshots</strong> from elevation pop pustules safely</li>
 <li><strong>Do not melee</strong>; pustule burst on contact deals 60% HP on Insanity</li>
</ul>

<p>Full strategy in <a href="/ko/how-to-beat-blister-borne/">Blister-borne boss guide</a>. After the fight, sewer routing splits — take the East tunnel for the second save room, skip the West tunnel (dead end + ammo trap).</p>

<h2 id="ch4">Chapter 4. Water Treatment (The Girl boss strategy)</h2>

<p>Water Treatment is Grace's solo chapter and the second Insanity wall. The Girl is genuinely fast on Insanity; her dash covers ¾ of the arena in under a second.</p>

<h3>// THE GIRL — INSANITY DELTA</h3>

<ol>
 <li><strong>Pre-load the upper catwalk</strong> with Hand Grenades before the fight triggers.</li>
 <li><strong>Use the pumping station valve</strong>; turning it floods the lower deck, forcing The Girl into a 4-second stun.</li>
 <li><strong>Empty Requiem Revolver during the stun window.</strong> Three Requiem Revolver rounds = 60% of her HP gone.</li>
</ol>

<p>Full strategy + arena map in <a href="/ko/how-to-beat-the-girl/">The Girl boss guide</a>. After the kill, take the maintenance lift up; do not backtrack through the flooded floor (you'll lose 15% HP per second to contamination).</p>

<h2 id="ch5">Chapter 5 — RPD + Orphanage (Mr. X chase, evidence room safe)</h2>

<p>You return to Leon for the RPD chapter. Mr. X stalks the building from cutscene one. on Insanity, his hearing radius is roughly doubled. Walk, do not run, except during scripted chases.</p>

<h3>// RPD ROUTE</h3>

<ul>
 <li><strong>Evidence room safe</strong>. combination in <a href="/ko/all-5-safe-codes-guide/">safe codes guide</a>; rewards 1× First Aid Spray + 6× Requiem Revolver rounds (vital for Tyrant phase)</li>
 <li><strong>Orphanage detour</strong> — file #41 + Mr. Raccoon #14 are in the orphanage attic; both <a href="/ko/all-25-mr-raccoon-locations/">Mr. Raccoon</a> and <a href="/ko/all-75-files-locations-guide/">file</a> guides have full coords</li>
 <li><strong>Super Tyrant arena</strong>; see <a href="/ko/how-to-beat-mr-x-super-tyrant/">Mr. X / Super Tyrant boss guide</a></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="ch6">Chapter 6, Raccoon City Square (Titan Spinner)</h2>

<p>Raccoon City Square is the largest open arena in the game. On Insanity it spawns 12 Hunter Gamma between the RPD exit and the Titan Spinner boss arena.</p>

<h3>// SKIP THE HUNTERS</h3>

<p>Hunters are not required kills. Sprint the inside-perimeter of the fountain, drop down into the subway access on the south side. This saves 40+ S&S M232 handgun rounds for the Titan Spinner fight. See <a href="/ko/how-to-beat-titan-spinner/">Titan Spinner boss guide</a> for the leg-shot stagger pattern.</p>

<h2 id="ch7">Chapter 7 — ARK Facility (Plant 43, Commander, Victor Gideon)</h2>

<p>The final chapter is three bosses back-to-back. Full chapter routing is in our <a href="/ko/ark-facility-complete-walkthrough/">ARK 시설 공략</a>; below are the Insanity-specific deltas.</p>

<h3>// PLANT 43 INSANITY DELTA</h3>

<p>Side bud HP scales ~2× on Insanity. Bring Hand Grenades. two grenades destroy both side buds in one throw each. Truck-canister finisher window shrinks to ~2.5 seconds; do not hesitate. <a href="/ko/how-to-beat-plant-43/">Full guide</a>.</p>

<h3>// THE COMMANDER INSANITY DELTA</h3>

<p>The Commander's cleaver one-shots Leon even with full armor on Insanity. Stay in the southwest corner. there's a pillar that blocks his wide-arc swing entirely. Parry timing widens by 4 frames on Insanity, ironically making the parry slightly easier if you have the timing. <a href="/ko/how-to-beat-the-commander/">Full guide</a>.</p>

<h3>// VICTOR GIDEON INSANITY DELTA</h3>

<p>Phase 2 Victor regenerates HP if you fail to land a hatchet hit within 4 seconds of stagger. You need 5 hatchet hits instead of 3 on Insanity. Bring the Mortal Edge Hatchet at minimum 80% durability into the final chamber. <a href="/ko/how-to-beat-victor-gideon/">Full guide</a>.</p>

<div>
 💡 <strong>Final boss readiness:</strong> Before stepping into the Victor arena, run through the <a href="/ko/re9-insanity-readiness-check/">Insanity readiness check</a> one more time. It will flag any inventory gap that has historically caused 70%+ of Victor wipes on Insanity.
</div>
